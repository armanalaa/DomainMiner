 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: New User, Customer Service Representative

Activity:
- A new user creates an account by entering their email address and password.
- The customer service representative verifies the new user's email address.
- The customer service representative assigns a unique customer ID to the verified user.
- The new user sets their personal details, including name, address, phone number, and income.
- The customer service representative updates the customer's profile with their age, gender, and country.

## 2. Customer Login

Performed By: Existing User

Activity:
- An existing user logs into their account using their email address and password.

## 3. Product Browsing

Performed By: Customer

Activity:
- Customers browse through the catalog to view available products.

## 4. Product Search

Performed By: Customer

Activity:
- Customers search for specific products using keywords or filters like category, price range, and rating.

## 5. Product Review Reading

Performed By: Customer

Activity:
- Customers read reviews of the selected products to help inform their purchasing decision.

## 6. Order Placement

Performed By: Customer

Activity:
- Customers add desired products to their shopping cart and proceed to checkout.
- Customers enter their billing and shipping information, select a payment method, and confirm the order details.
- The customer submits the order, which is then processed by the system.

## 7. Order Processing

Performed By: Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Warehouse staff retrieve the ordered products from inventory.
- Warehouse staff package the products for shipping.
- Warehouse staff update the order status to "shipped" and record the tracking number.

## 8. Order Shipping

Performed By: Shipping Provider

Activity:
- The shipping provider picks up the packaged orders from Dell's warehouse.
- The shipping provider delivers the orders to the customer's specified address.

## 9. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- The finance department generates an invoice for each order, including the total amount and payment details.

## 10. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customer, Payment Provider

Activity:
- Customers make payments using their chosen method (credit card, PayPal, etc.).
- The payment provider processes the transaction and updates the order status accordingly.
