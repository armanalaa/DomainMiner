 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by entering their first name, last name, address details, email, phone number, region, and age into the customers table.
- IT support assigns a unique customer ID and password to each new customer.

## 2. Customer Account Management

Performed By: Customers, IT Support

Activity:
- Customers update their account information by modifying their first name, last name, address details, email, phone number, region, and income in the customers table.
- IT support changes a customer's password upon request.

## 3. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products from the inventory, specifying quantities, and providing their order details (order date, billing address) in the orderlines table.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update the quantity in stock for a product when it is received or sold by adjusting the quan_in_stock column in the inventory table.
- Purchasing teams add new products to the inventory by entering product details (product ID, sales, and expiration date) into the inventory table.

## 5. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipment by retrieving the required products from the inventory based on orderline details.

## 6. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- Finance department generates invoices by calculating the total cost of each order and recording it in the customers_hist table.

## 7. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers make payments by providing their credit card information (creditcardtype, creditcard, and creditcardexpiration) to the finance department.
- Finance department records customer payments in the customers_hist table.

## 8. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints by accessing relevant information from the customers, orders, and cust_hist tables.
