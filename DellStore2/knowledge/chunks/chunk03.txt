 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details, contact information, and account credentials. (cust_df)
- IT administrators assign unique customer IDs to each newly registered customer. (cust_df)
- Customer service representatives update customer profiles with changes in contact or account information. (cust_df)
- IT administrators manage user accounts by setting passwords and access levels for customers. (cust_df)

## 2. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers browse the online catalog to select desired products. (products)
- Sales representatives assist customers in making purchasing decisions by providing product recommendations and answering questions.
- Customers add selected products to their shopping carts.
- Customers proceed to checkout and enter shipping and billing information.
- Customers confirm their order details and complete the payment process. (orders, cust_df)

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff retrieve ordered products from inventory storage. (inventory)
- Inventory managers verify that the correct quantity of each product is available for shipment.
- Warehouse staff package and label each order for shipping.
- Inventory managers update the inventory levels to reflect the shipped products. (inventory)

## 4. Order Fulfillment and Delivery

Performed By: Shipping Clerks, Courier Services

Activity:
- Shipping clerks prepare orders for delivery by affixing shipping labels and arranging them in batches.
- Shipping clerks coordinate with courier services to schedule pickups and track shipments.
- Courier services transport the orders to their destinations and update tracking information. (orders)

## 5. Customer Support and Complaints Handling

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Support Staff

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints regarding orders, products, or account issues. (cust_df, orders)
- IT support staff troubleshoot technical problems reported by customers and provide solutions or escalate issues as necessary.

## 6. Inventory Management

Performed By: Inventory Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Inventory managers monitor stock levels and reorder products when quantities fall below a predetermined threshold. (reorder)
- Warehouse staff receive new shipments of products and update inventory levels accordingly. (inventory)
- Inventory managers maintain accurate records of product availability, pricing, and categorization. (products)

## 7. Financial Management

Performed By: Accounting Staff, Finance Managers

Activity:
- Accounting staff record financial transactions related to orders, sales, and payments. (orders, cust_df)
- Finance managers analyze financial data to generate reports on revenue, expenses, and profitability.
- Finance managers make recommendations for pricing strategies, cost reductions, and investment opportunities based on their analysis.
