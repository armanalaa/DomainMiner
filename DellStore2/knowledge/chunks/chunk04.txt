 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Customer Service Representative

Activity:
- Records customer details such as first name, last name, address, email, phone number, credit card type, and password.

Performed By: System Administrator

Activity:
- Assigns a unique customer ID and generates a username for the new customer.

## 2. Customer Account Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representative

Activity:
- Updates customer details like address, phone number, or password when requested by the customer.

## 3. Order Placement

Performed By: Customer

Activity:
- Browses and selects products based on categories, then adds them to the shopping cart.

Performed By: Customer Service Representative

Activity:
- Assists customers with any issues during the ordering process.

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Retrieves ordered products from inventory based on order ID and product ID.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Inventory Manager

Activity:
- Updates the inventory levels when products are sold or received.

## 6. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Packages and ships ordered products to the customer's address.

## 7. Customer Order History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Representative

Activity:
- Reviews and updates the customer's order history in the system.

## 8. Sales Report Generation

Performed By: Data Analyst

Activity:
- Generates sales reports based on orders, products, and customer data.
