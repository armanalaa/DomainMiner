Performed By: Data Analysts, Customer Service Representatives

## 3. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products and quantities (orderlines).
- Sales representatives confirm orders and update order statuses (orders).

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track product quantities in stock (inventory).
- Purchasing teams order product replenishments when inventory levels fall below a certain threshold (inventory).

## 5. Product Information Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Inventory Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price (cust_df).
- Inventory staff update product information when necessary (cust_df).

## 6. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack orders based on order details (orderlines).
- Shipping teams prepare and ship orders to customers (orders).
