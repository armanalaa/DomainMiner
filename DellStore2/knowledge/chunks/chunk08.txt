 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact information and creating a user account.
- IT support assigns unique customer IDs and passwords to each new account.

## 2. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, IT Support

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the catalog by recording product code, name, category, list price, and other relevant details.
- IT support updates the product database with the new item information.

## 3. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products, specifying quantities, and submitting their order.
- Sales representatives review and confirm the order details before processing it.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, IT Support

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by updating the quantity in stock for each product.
- IT support updates the inventory database with the new quantities.

## 5. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives initiate order processing by calling the purchase function with customer ID, number of items, and item details.
- IT support processes the order by creating a new order and adding line items to it.

## 6. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Department

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack orders based on the order details and inventory levels.
- Shipping department ships the orders to the customer's specified address.

## 7. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Team, IT Support

Activity:
- Finance team generates invoices for each completed order by calculating the total amount due based on the net amount, tax, and total amount from the order details.
- IT support updates the customer history database with the new invoice information.

## 8. Inventory Replenishment

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, IT Support

Activity:
- Purchasing teams order more inventory when the quantity in stock for a product falls below a certain threshold.
- IT support updates the inventory database with the new quantities once they arrive.

## 9. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints by accessing relevant order, invoice, and customer information.
- IT support assists customer service representatives by providing them with the necessary tools and data.
