 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and preferences.
- HR managers update customer information when changes occur, such as address or employment status.

## 2. Customer Interaction Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives maintain a history of interactions with customers, including demos and support requests.
- Customer service agents document customer inquiries and responses within the interaction history.

## 3. Order Placement and Management

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products and providing shipping details.
- Sales representatives confirm order details and process them for fulfillment.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and prepare orders for shipment.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update product quantities in stock when orders are fulfilled or received.
- Purchasing teams order new products based on inventory levels and demand forecasts.

## 5. Financial Transactions Processing

Performed By: Finance, Accounting

Activity:
- Finance records sales transactions and calculates revenue.
- Accounting processes payments from customers and updates account balances.

## 6. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- The data governance team ensures data quality by defining and enforcing data rules.
- They monitor data lineage and maintain a catalog of all data sources within the organization.

## 7. Support and Issue Resolution

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, IT Support

Activity:
- Customer service agents assist customers with technical issues or product questions.
- IT support diagnoses and resolves system-related problems to ensure smooth operations.
