 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, Customers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information (cust_df).
- HR managers update customer records with employment-related data (cust_df).
- Customers manage their accounts by modifying personal information (cust_df).

## 2. Customer Support Requests

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Experts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives log support requests from customers (custhist_df).
- Experts connect with customers to discuss data governance projects or resolve active issues.

## 3. Data Governance Project Management

Performed By: Founder, Sales Representatives, Customers

Activity:
- The founder discusses data governance projects with potential clients (custhist_df).
- Sales representatives book demos for interested customers to showcase Dataedo features and benefits (custhist_df).
- Customers join Dataedo after evaluating its offerings through a free trial or demo.

## 4. Data Catalog Management

Performed By: Founder, Experts

Activity:
- The founder configures preconfigured data catalogs with sample data for demonstration purposes (custhist_df).
- Experts help customers set up their own data catalogs using their own data during a 14-day free trial.
