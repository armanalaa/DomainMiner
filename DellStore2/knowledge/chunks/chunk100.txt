 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Operations Specialist, Product Manager, IT Admin & Support

Activity:
- Sales Operations Specialists register new customer details in the CUSTOMER table.
- Product Managers update product information for existing customers.
- IT Admins & Support maintain customer account security by managing passwords.

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Metadata Engineer, Tech Lead, Developer, QA Engineer

Activity:
- Developers record customer purchase history in the CUST_HIST table.
- QA Engineers verify the accuracy of customer history records.
- Metadata Engineers ensure proper data integration and consistency.

## 3. Customer Demo Scheduling

Performed By: IT Admin & Support, Developer, Tech Lead

Activity:
- IT Admins & Support schedule demos for potential customers.
- Developers prepare the demo environment as needed.
- Tech Leads oversee the demo process and provide technical support.

## 4. Data Maintenance and Management

Performed By: Metadata Engineer, QA Engineer, Developer

Activity:
- Metadata Engineers maintain the structure and relationships of data in DellStore2.
- QA Engineers verify the quality and accuracy of data within the database.
- Developers implement updates to the database schema as needed.

## 5. Data Analysis and Reporting

Performed By: Developer, Tech Lead, QA Engineer, UI/UX Designer, Junior Technical Writer

Activity:
- Developers create reports based on customer data for analysis.
- Tech Leads oversee the development of analytical tools.
- QA Engineers verify the accuracy and reliability of reports.
- UI/UX Designers design user-friendly interfaces for reporting tools.
- Junior Technical Writers document the usage and functionality of reporting tools.
