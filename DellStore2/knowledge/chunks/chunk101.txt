 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information. (cust_df)
- Customer service agents update customer records with any changes to personal or billing information. (cust_df)
- Sales representatives assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer for account access. (cust_df)
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries, resolving issues related to orders, billing, or account management. (cust_df)
- Sales representatives track customer interactions and follow-ups using the provided communication channels. (cust_df)

## 2. Customer History Management

Performed By: Data Analysts, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Data analysts record and store each customer's purchase history in the respective database. (custhist_df)
- Customer service agents access a customer's purchase history to assist with inquiries or resolve issues. (custhist_df)
- Data analysts generate reports on customer purchasing trends and behavior for analysis and decision making. (custhist_df)

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the product catalog by recording their details such as product code, name, category, and list price. (cust_df)
- Sales representatives update product information when necessary, ensuring accurate and up-to-date product details in the catalog. (cust_df)

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by confirming availability, calculating shipping costs, and generating order numbers. (cust_df)
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to the customer's specified address.
- Customer service agents handle any issues related to order processing or delivery status. (cust_df)

## 5. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Team, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- The finance team generates invoices for each customer based on their purchase history. (custhist_df)
- Customer service agents send invoices to customers via email or other communication channels. (cust_df)
- Customers make payments towards their invoices using various methods, which are recorded and tracked by the finance team. (cust_df)

## 6. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- The data governance team ensures that all data is collected, stored, and processed in compliance with relevant regulations and policies.
- They monitor the database for any potential issues or vulnerabilities and take corrective action as necessary.

## 7. Data Analysis and Reporting

Performed By: Data Analysts, Management Team

Activity:
- Data analysts analyze data from various sources to gain insights into customer behavior, sales trends, and other key performance indicators. (custhist_df)
- The management team uses these insights to make informed decisions about business strategy, product offerings, and marketing initiatives.
