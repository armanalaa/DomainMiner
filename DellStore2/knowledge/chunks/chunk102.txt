 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details, such as name, email address, and phone number. (CUSTOMER table)
- HR managers update customer information when changes occur, like job titles or addresses. (CUSTOMER table)

## 2. Customer Support Interaction

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints by recording the issue, resolution, and follow-up actions. (Not specified in provided tables)

## 3. Order Placement and Processing

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Customers place orders through the online store by selecting products and providing shipping information. (Not specified in provided tables)
- Sales representatives review and confirm orders before they are processed for shipment. (Not specified in provided tables)
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to customers. (Not specified in provided tables)

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update the inventory levels when products are received or shipped out. (Not specified in provided tables)
- Purchasing teams order new stock based on current inventory levels and sales forecasts. (Not specified in provided tables)

## 5. Financial Transactions

Performed By: Finance Department, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives record the details of each sale, including the product, price, and customer information. (Not specified in provided tables)
- The finance department processes payments, generates invoices, and records financial transactions related to sales. (Not specified in provided tables)

## 6. Data Governance and Documentation

Performed By: Data Stewards, Business Users

Activity:
- Data stewards ensure the accuracy and consistency of data across all systems. (Not specified in provided tables)
- Business users document data definitions, relationships, and lineage within the Dataedo platform. (Not specified in provided tables)
