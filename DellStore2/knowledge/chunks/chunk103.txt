 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information. (CUSTOMER table)
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer for secure access. (CUSTOMER table)
- Sales representatives update customer details as needed, such as address or email changes. (CUSTOMER table)

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives log each interaction with a customer, including sales transactions and support requests. (CUST_HIST table)
- Customer support agents record resolution details for any customer issues or complaints. (CUST_HIST table)

## 3. Customer Data Maintenance

Performed By: Database Administrators

Activity:
- Database administrators ensure the accuracy and integrity of customer data by regularly auditing and updating records. (cust_df, CUSTOMER tables)

## 4. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers

Activity:
- Product managers maintain the product catalog by adding new items, updating descriptions, and adjusting prices as needed. (cust_df table)

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and update them when products are received or sold. (cust_df table)
- Purchasing teams order new stock based on inventory levels and sales forecasts. (cust_df table)

## 6. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by confirming availability, calculating shipping costs, and scheduling delivery. (cust_df table)
- Warehouse staff prepare and ship ordered products to the customer's specified address. (cust_df table)

## 7. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Team

Activity:
- Finance team generates invoices based on customer orders and sends them to the customer for payment. (cust_df table)
- Finance team records payments received from customers and updates their account balances accordingly. (CUSTOMER table)
