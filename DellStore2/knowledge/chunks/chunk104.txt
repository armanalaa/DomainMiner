 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details, contact information, and account credentials.
- HR managers update customer records with employment history when a customer joins or leaves Dell.
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and updates related to account information.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by confirming product availability, calculating prices, and scheduling deliveries.
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to customers.

## 3. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by updating the quantity of each product in stock.
- Purchasing teams order new products when inventory falls below a predefined threshold.

## 4. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Finance generates invoices based on orders and sends them to customers.
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries related to invoices and process payments.

## 5. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives address customer concerns, troubleshoot issues, and provide technical support as needed.

## 6. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: IT Administrators, Data Governance Team

Activity:
- IT administrators ensure data security by implementing access controls and monitoring user activity.
- The data governance team maintains the accuracy and integrity of customer data through regular audits and updates.

## 7. Reporting and Analytics

Performed By: Data Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Data analysts generate reports on sales performance, inventory levels, and customer behavior for internal analysis and decision making.
- Management reviews these reports to identify trends, make strategic decisions, and set goals for future growth.
