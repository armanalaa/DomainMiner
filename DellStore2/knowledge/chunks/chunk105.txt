 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact information and account details.
- Customer service agents update customer records with changes to personal or billing data.
- Sales representatives assign customer accounts to specific sales teams for targeted marketing efforts.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Finance Team

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by entering product details and shipping information.
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to customers.
- Finance team records order payments and updates customer accounts accordingly.

## 3. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Team

Activity:
- Finance team generates invoices for each completed order based on the order details and payment terms.

## 4. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Team

Activity:
- Customers make payments towards their outstanding invoices using various methods.
- Finance team records customer payments and updates account balances accordingly.

## 5. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries and complaints, providing solutions or escalating issues as necessary.

## 6. Partner Management

Performed By: Partnership Team

Activity:
- Partnership team manages relationships with dataedo partners, coordinating demos, collaborations, and support.

## 7. Data Governance

Performed By: IT Administrators, Data Trust Associates (consultants)

Activity:
- IT administrators maintain the security and integrity of customer data within DellStore2.
- Data Trust Associates consult on data governance best practices and compliance issues.
