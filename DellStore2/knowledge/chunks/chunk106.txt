 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information. (CUSTOMER)
- Customer service agents update customer records with changes to personal or billing information. (CUSTOMER)
- Sales representatives assign customers to appropriate sales regions based on geographical location. (CUSTOMER)

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders for customers by specifying products, quantities, and delivery dates. (cust_df)
- Warehouse staff prepare items for shipment once an order is confirmed. (Not specified in the schema)
- Customer service agents handle any issues that arise during the order fulfillment process. (Not specified in the schema)

## 3. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Department, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices for completed orders by calculating total costs and applying any discounts or promotions. (Not specified in the schema)
- The finance department reviews and approves invoices before they are sent to customers. (Not specified in the schema)
- The finance department records approved invoices in the system, updating the customer's account balance accordingly. (CUSTOMER)

## 4. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers make payments on their accounts by providing payment details and choosing a payment method. (Not specified in the schema)
- The finance department processes customer payments and updates the account balance accordingly. (CUSTOMER)

## 5. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, Technical Support Staff

Activity:
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries related to orders, billing, or account information. (Not specified in the schema)
- Technical support staff assist customers with product-related issues and provide troubleshooting guidance. (Not specified in the schema)

## 6. Sales Performance Tracking

Performed By: Sales Managers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Sales managers monitor sales performance by reviewing order data and identifying trends or opportunities for improvement. (cust_df, custhist_df)
- The finance department generates reports on sales revenue, profit margins, and customer retention rates. (Not specified in the schema)

## 7. Data Maintenance

Performed By: IT Department

Activity:
- The IT department ensures data integrity by regularly backing up the DellStore2 database. (Not specified in the schema)
- The IT department performs routine maintenance tasks to optimize database performance and security. (Not specified in the schema)
