 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: HR managers, Sales representatives, Customers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, email, address, phone number).
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer.
- Customers update their personal information as needed (name, email, phone number).

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales representatives, Purchasing teams

Activity:
- Sales representatives log customer purchases (CUST_HIST).
- Purchasing teams maintain a record of all transactions made by each customer (custhist_df).

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product managers

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the product catalog (product code, name, category, list price).

## 4. Customer Support and Inquiry Handling

Performed By: Customer support team

Activity:
- Customer support team responds to customer inquiries and provides assistance.

## 5. Data Analysis and Reporting

Performed By: Data analysts, Finance team

Activity:
- Data analysts analyze sales data for insights (CUST_HIST, custhist_df).
- Finance team generates reports based on the analysis.
