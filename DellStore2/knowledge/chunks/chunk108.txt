 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customer accounts by recording their contact details and preferences.
- Customer service agents update customer information when contacted with changes or inquiries.
- Sales representatives track customer interactions and follow-ups using the communication history column.

## 2. Employee Management

Performed By: HR Managers, IT Department

Activity:
- HR managers maintain employee records, including personal details, roles, and employment history.
- The IT department assigns system access and permissions based on each employee's role within the company.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the catalog by recording product details such as name, category, list price, and images.
- Warehouse staff update stock levels when products are received or sold.

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Shipping Department

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by confirming availability, calculating shipping costs, and scheduling delivery.
- Warehouse staff pick and pack items for shipment based on order details.
- Shipping department prepares and sends out orders to customers.

## 5. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Accounting Department

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices for completed orders, including item details, quantities, prices, and taxes.
- The accounting department reviews and approves invoices before they are sent to customers.

## 6. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Accounting Department

Activity:
- Customers make payments through various methods such as credit card or bank transfer.
- The accounting department records incoming payments and updates customer accounts accordingly.

## 7. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries and complaints, providing assistance with products, orders, and billing issues.

## 8. Data Documentation

Performed By: IT Department, Data Management Team

Activity:
- The IT department maintains documentation for the company's data assets, including tables, columns, and relationships.
- The data management team updates this documentation as changes are made to the database schema.
