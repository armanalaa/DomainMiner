 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and preferences. (cust_df)
- HR managers update customer information when changes occur, such as address or employment status. (CUSTOMER)
- Sales representatives assign a unique user ID and password to each customer for account access. (cust_df)

## 2. Customer Support Requests

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives receive and log customer support requests from various channels, including phone calls, emails, or online forms. (not specified in the database schema)
- Customer service representatives categorize and prioritize support requests based on urgency and complexity. (not specified in the database schema)
- Customer service representatives resolve support requests by providing solutions or escalating them to technical teams as needed. (not specified in the database schema)

## 3. Demo Requests and Bookings

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives process demo requests from potential customers by scheduling demos with relevant product experts. (not specified in the database schema)
- Customer service representatives confirm demo details with customers and update the booking information accordingly. (not specified in the database schema)

## 4. Office Location Management

Performed By: Facilities Managers, HR Managers

Activity:
- Facilities managers maintain a list of office locations, including addresses and contact details. (not specified in the database schema)
- HR managers assign employees to specific offices based on their roles and preferences. (not specified in the database schema)

## 5. Dataedo Product Information Management

Performed By: Marketing Team, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- The marketing team updates product information, such as features, documentation, roadmap, and download links. (not specified in the database schema)
- Sales representatives access and share this product information with potential customers during sales calls or demos. (not specified in the database schema)
