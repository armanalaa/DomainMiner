 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, email, address, phone number).
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer for account access.
- Customer information is updated when changes occur (e.g., address, contact details).

## 2. Customer Interaction Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives document interactions with customers regarding product inquiries or sales.
- Customer service agents record customer complaints and resolve issues.
- All interactions are logged for future reference (e.g., notes on calls, emails).

## 3. Order Placement and Management

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products, quantities, and providing shipping details.
- Sales representatives confirm order details and process them for fulfillment.
- Order status is updated as it moves through the fulfillment process (e.g., in progress, shipped, delivered).

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and update them when products are added or removed.
- Purchasing teams order new stock based on inventory levels and sales data.
- Inventory is organized and stored efficiently to minimize waste and maximize space utilization.

## 5. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers make payments using various methods (e.g., credit card, bank transfer).
- Finance department records transactions and updates customer accounts accordingly.
- Invoices are generated for outstanding balances and sent to customers.

## 6. Shipping and Delivery

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Courier Services

Activity:
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipping by packaging products and attaching necessary documents.
- Courier services pick up and deliver orders to the specified addresses.
- Tracking information is provided to customers for order visibility.

## 7. Customer Support and Complaints Handling

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, Quality Assurance Team

Activity:
- Customer service agents address customer complaints and inquiries promptly.
- Quality assurance team investigates issues related to product quality or performance.
- Solutions are provided to customers and feedback is collected for continuous improvement.

## 8. Data Analysis and Reporting

Performed By: Analytics Team, Management

Activity:
- Analytics team collects and analyzes data on sales, inventory, customer interactions, and other key performance indicators.
- Reports are generated to inform decision-making and identify areas for improvement.
- Management reviews reports and takes action based on insights gained.
