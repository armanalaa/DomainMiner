 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information. (CUSTOMER)
- Customer service agents update customer records with any changes in personal or account data. (CUSTOMER)
- Sales representatives assign unique customer IDs to each new account. (CUSTOMER)
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries and complaints, documenting them for future reference. (Not specified table)
- Sales representatives track sales history for individual customers, updating the records accordingly. (CUST_HIST)

## 2. Customer History Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Finance Department

Activity:
- Purchasing teams record customer orders and their respective order numbers. (CUST_HIST)
- Finance department updates the financial records of each customer, including invoices and payments. (Not specified table)
- Purchasing teams update the status of each order in the customer history, indicating whether it has been shipped or cancelled. (CUST_HIST)
- Finance department generates and sends invoices to customers based on their purchase history. (Not specified table)
- Finance department records payments received from customers against their respective invoices. (Not specified table)

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price. (Not specified table)
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels for each product as they receive or ship orders. (Not specified table)
- Product managers monitor sales data to identify popular products and adjust pricing strategies accordingly. (Not specified table)
- Warehouse staff track the location of each product within the warehouse, ensuring efficient picking and shipping processes. (Not specified table)

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff receive incoming shipments and update inventory levels accordingly. (Not specified table)
- Purchasing teams place orders for products when stock levels fall below a predefined threshold. (Not specified table)
- Warehouse staff pick items for outgoing orders, ensuring the correct quantity and product is included. (Not specified table)
- Warehouse staff pack and ship orders to customers, updating the order status in the customer history. (CUST_HIST)

## 5. Employee Management

Performed By: HR Managers, IT Department

Activity:
- HR managers hire new employees by recording their personal details, employment history, and job roles. (Not specified table)
- IT department sets up user accounts for new employees, granting them access to relevant systems. (Not specified table)
- HR managers manage employee records, including promotions, transfers, and terminations. (Not specified table)
- IT department updates user account details as necessary, such as password resets or role changes. (Not specified table)
