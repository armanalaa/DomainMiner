 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details and contact information (cust_df).
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer for account access (cust_df).

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives update the customer history table (CUST_HIST) with details of each interaction.
- Data analysts analyze customer history data to identify trends and patterns (CUST_HIST).

## 3. Account Maintenance

Performed By: Customers, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customers update their account information as needed through the self-service portal (cust_df).
- Customer service representatives verify and confirm changes made to customer accounts (cust_df).

## 4. Sales Process Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Sales representatives enter product details and prices into the catalog for customers to view (not specified in provided schema).
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders based on customer requests and available inventory levels.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and update the system when stock changes (not specified in provided schema).
- Purchasing teams order new products to replenish depleted inventory (not specified in provided schema).

## 6. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack orders based on purchase orders and available stock (not specified in provided schema).
- Shipping teams prepare and dispatch orders to customers (not specified in provided schema).

## 7. Financial Processing

Performed By: Finance Teams, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Finance teams record transactions related to sales, purchases, and inventory movements (not specified in provided schema).
- Accounting staff generate financial reports based on processed transactions (not specified in provided schema).
