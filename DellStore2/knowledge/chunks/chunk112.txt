 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and creating a customer account (cust_df).
- Sales managers update customer profiles with additional information such as purchase history and preferences (CUSTOMER).

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives log interactions with customers, including issues resolved and product recommendations (custhist_df).
- Data analysts analyze customer history data to identify trends and opportunities for upselling or cross-selling (CUST_HIST).

## 3. Account Management

Performed By: Finance Department, HR Managers

Activity:
- Finance department updates customer account information related to payments and billing (cust_df).
- HR managers manage employee access to customer data based on role and permissions.

## 4. Data Governance

Performed By: Data Stewards, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Data stewards maintain the business glossary and ensure consistent use of terms across the organization (CUST_DF, CUSTOMER).
- IT administrators implement data privacy by design principles to protect sensitive business knowledge (all tables).

## 5. User Authentication

Performed By: IT Administrators

Activity:
- IT administrators integrate user authentication with enterprise identity systems for secure access to the governance platform (CUST_DF, CUSTOMER).

## 6. Data Lineage and Workflows

Performed By: Data Analysts, Data Stewards

Activity:
- Data analysts visualize data flow across systems and trace issues back to their source (all tables).
- Data stewards streamline data governance processes with built-in workflows for reviews, approvals, and change management (all tables).

## 7. Sensitive Data Discovery

Performed By: Data Stewards, IT Security Team

Activity:
- Data stewards automatically detect and classify sensitive or regulated data to support compliance and reduce risk (all tables).
- IT security team monitors and responds to potential data breaches or unauthorized access attempts.
