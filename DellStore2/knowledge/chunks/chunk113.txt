 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints by accessing the customer's contact information and interaction history.
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their personal details, contact information, and account credentials.
- HR managers manage employee data for each customer representative, including their roles, salaries, and performance metrics.

## 2. Customer Interaction Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives document interactions with customers, such as phone calls, emails, or chat sessions, in the customer interaction history.
- Sales representatives record details of customer meetings, negotiations, and agreements in the customer interaction history.

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by confirming product availability, calculating prices, and scheduling delivery times.
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered products to customers based on the order details.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels by recording product receipts and shipments.
- Purchasing teams place orders for new products based on inventory levels and demand forecasts.

## 5. Financial Management

Performed By: Finance Teams, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Finance teams record financial transactions related to sales, expenses, and profits in the appropriate columns.
- Accounting staff generate invoices for customers based on their orders and payment terms.

## 6. Data Governance

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- The data governance team ensures data quality by validating and correcting errors in customer data.
- They maintain a business glossary, cataloging all relevant data elements and their meanings.

## 7. Compliance and Reporting

Performed By: Legal Teams, Data Governance Team

Activity:
- Legal teams ensure compliance with data privacy regulations by reviewing customer data access and handling procedures.
- The data governance team generates reports on data quality, lineage, and usage for internal analysis and external audits.
