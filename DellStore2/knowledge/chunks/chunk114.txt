 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details and contact information (cust_df).
- HR managers update customer account details when a change of employment status occurs (CUSTOMER).

## 2. Customer History Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives log customer interactions, including purchases and complaints, in the customer history database (cust_hist, custhist_df).
- Customer service representatives access and review a customer's history to address inquiries or resolve issues.

## 3. Account Management

Performed By: Finance Department, IT Department

Activity:
- The finance department updates the account information of customers, including billing details and payment status (cust_df).
- The IT department manages customer accounts, ensuring secure access and data protection (CUSTOMER).

## 4. Data Quality Assurance

Performed By: Data Analysts, BI Teams

Activity:
- Data analysts and BI teams run automated quality checks on datasets to uncover errors, anomalies, and inconsistencies (Dataedo platform).
- They monitor data quality scores over time, assign fixes, and crowdsource feedback to ensure the data remains trustworthy.

## 5. Data Profiling

Performed By: Data Analysts, BI Teams

Activity:
- Data analysts and BI teams explore column statistics, value distributions, unique counts, and null percentages to gain insights into datasets (Dataedo platform).
- They identify potential issues that may signal data quality problems.

## 6. Data Governance

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- The data governance team establishes and enforces data quality policies, ensuring consistent and reliable data across the organization (Dataedo platform).
- They provide context that automated rules might miss, leveraging community feedback to refine validation rules.

## 7. Data Lineage

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- The data governance team tracks the lineage of datasets, understanding their origins and transformations throughout their lifecycle (Dataedo platform).
- They ensure regulatory compliance and protect the reputation of the organization by maintaining accurate and transparent data lineage.
