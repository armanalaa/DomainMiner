 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and creating a customer account (cust_df).
- HR managers assign roles and permissions to the newly registered users (cust_df).
- IT administrators set up user accounts with appropriate access levels (cust_df).

## 2. Customer Data Maintenance

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers update their personal and business information in their account (cust_df).
- Sales representatives modify customer details when necessary (cust_df).

## 3. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Support Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives log interactions with customers, including sales and support activities (custhist_df).
- Customer support agents record customer inquiries and responses (custhist_df).

## 4. Data Quality Assurance

Performed By: Data Quality Analysts

Activity:
- Data quality analysts monitor the accuracy, completeness, and consistency of customer data (data profiling).
- They investigate and resolve any issues found in the customer data (data lineage).

## 5. Compliance and Security Management

Performed By: IT Administrators, Legal Team

Activity:
- IT administrators implement security measures to protect customer data (role-based access control, user authentication).
- The legal team ensures compliance with data privacy regulations (consent details about data privacy by design).
