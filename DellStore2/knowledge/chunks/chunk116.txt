 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their personal details, contact information, and account credentials. (CUSTOMER table)
- Customer service agents update customer profiles with changes to contact information or account status. (CUSTOMER table)
- Sales representatives assign unique customer IDs to each registered user. (cust_df table)

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives record details of customer interactions and transactions in the customer history database. (CUST_HIST table)
- Customer service agents access and review a customer's history to address queries or issues. (CUST_HIST table)
- Sales representatives use historical data for sales forecasting and trend analysis. (CUST_HIST table)

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product code, name, category, and list price. (cust_df table)
- Warehouse staff update product information when inventory levels change or products are discontinued. (cust_df table)
- Warehouse staff track product locations within the warehouse for efficient picking and shipping. (Not specified in provided schema)

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by selecting products, calculating totals, and generating order numbers. (cust_df table, CUST_HIST table)
- Warehouse staff pick and pack ordered items for shipping. (Not specified in provided schema)
- Customer service agents confirm order status and provide tracking information to customers. (CUST_HIST table)

## 5. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Department, Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Finance department generates invoices based on customer orders and sends them to the relevant parties. (Not specified in provided schema)
- Sales representatives review and approve invoices before they are sent to customers. (Not specified in provided schema)
- Customer service agents assist customers with payment queries or issues. (Not specified in provided schema)
