 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information (cust_df).
- IT support assigns unique usernames and passwords to each customer for secure access (cust_df).

## 2. Customer Profile Management

Performed By: Customers, IT Support

Activity:
- Customers update their personal and contact details in their profiles (CUSTOMER).
- IT support verifies and approves changes to customer profiles (CUSTOMER).

## 3. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products, specifying quantities, and providing shipping information (cust_df).

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, IT Support

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when new items are received or shipped out (cust_df).
- IT support ensures accurate and up-to-date inventory data (cust_df).

## 5. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices based on customer orders and pricing information (cust_df).
- Finance records the invoice details and sends it to the customer (not specified in provided schema).

## 6. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance

Activity:
- Customers make payments by choosing a payment method and providing necessary payment information (not specified in provided schema).
- Finance records the payment details and updates the customer's account status (CUSTOMER).

## 7. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer queries and issues related to orders, products, or accounts (not specified in provided schema).
- IT support troubleshoots technical issues reported by customers (not specified in provided schema).
