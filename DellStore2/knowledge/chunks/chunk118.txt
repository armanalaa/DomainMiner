 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details and contact information (cust_df).
- HR managers update customer profiles with employment history and demographic data (CUSTOMER).

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives log interactions with customers, including transaction details and issue resolution notes (cust_hist, custhist_df).
- Data analysts analyze customer history data to identify trends and patterns (cust_hist, custhist_df).

## 3. Account Management

Performed By: Finance Department, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Finance department updates customer account information, such as billing address and payment details (CUSTOMER).
- Customer service representatives assist customers with account inquiries and resolve billing issues (CUSTOMER).

## 4. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product code, name, category, and list price (cust_df).
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels for each product (not shown in provided schema).

## 5. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by selecting products and confirming shipping details (not shown in provided schema).
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to customers (not shown in provided schema).

## 6. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Department, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Finance department generates invoices based on customer orders and account information (CUSTOMER)
- Customer service representatives review and send invoices to customers (not shown in provided schema).

## 7. Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Department, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Finance department processes customer payments and updates account balances (CUSTOMER).
- Customer service representatives handle payment inquiries and resolve disputes (not shown in provided schema).
