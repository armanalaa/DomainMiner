 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and preferences (cust_df).
- Customer service agents update customer information as needed (cust_df).
- Sales representatives assign customer accounts to specific account managers (CUSTOMER).

## 2. Account Management

Performed By: Account Managers, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Account managers maintain relationships with assigned customers, addressing their needs and concerns (CUSTOMER).
- Account managers track customer purchase history (custhist_df).
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries related to account information (cust_df, CUSTOMER).

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders from customers, including item selection and quantity (custhist_df).
- Warehouse staff pick and pack ordered items for shipment (not specified).
- Shipping clerks prepare and dispatch orders to the customer (not specified).

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Inventory Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Inventory managers monitor stock levels and reorder items as needed (not specified).
- Warehouse staff receive and store incoming shipments (not specified).
- Warehouse staff update inventory records for new arrivals (custhist_df).

## 5. Billing and Payment

Performed By: Finance Department, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Finance department generates invoices based on customer orders (not specified).
- Customer service agents send invoices to customers via email (CUSTOMER).
- Customers make payments towards their outstanding balances (not specified).

## 6. Partner Management

Performed By: Channel Partners, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives collaborate with channel partners to expand the customer base (090_resellers.pdf).
- Channel partners promote DellStore2 products and services to their networks (090_resellers.pdf).
- Sales representatives manage relationships with partner accounts (CUSTOMER).

## 7. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: Data Trust Associates, IT Department

Activity:
- Data Trust Associates ensure data governance and compliance within DellStore2 (Data Trust Associates).
- IT department implements and maintains security measures to protect customer data (not specified).
- IT department ensures adherence to data privacy regulations (not specified).
