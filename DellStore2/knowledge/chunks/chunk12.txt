 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, address, email, phone number, country, date of registration) from the provided form.
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to newly registered customers for account access.

## 2. Customer Account Maintenance

Performed By: Customers, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customers update their personal details (address, email, phone number) through the self-service portal or by contacting customer service representatives.
- Customer service representatives verify and update customer account information in the database as needed.

## 3. Credit Card Transactions

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customers

Activity:
- Sales representatives process credit card transactions using Stripe's services for secure online payments.
- Customers enter their credit card details (card number, expiration date, CVV) during checkout to complete a purchase.

## 4. Security and Consent Management

Performed By: Website Operators, Visitors

Activity:
- Website operators implement security measures such as cookies (cfuvid, _ab, _mc_anon_id, ak_bmsc, bcookie, connect.sid) to protect the website from spam and abuse.
- Visitors consent to the use of cookies by accepting the cookie policy on the website.

## 5. Calendar Integration

Performed By: Calendly

Activity:
- Calendly integrates with the website to allow users to schedule events directly from their calendars if they provide consent (_calendly_session).

## 6. Bot Detection and Management

Performed By: BotManager, Website Operators

Activity:
- BotManager detects, categorizes, and compiles reports on potential bots trying to access the website (bm_sz).
- Website operators review and address any detected bot activity to maintain website security.

## 7. Device Identification

Performed By: Stripe

Activity:
- Stripe determines the device used to access the website (m) for formatting purposes to ensure optimal user experience.

## 8. Inventory Management and Replenishment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and notify purchasing teams when stock is low.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to replenish depleted items in the warehouse.

## 9. Invoice Processing and Payment

Performed By: Finance, Customers

Activity:
- Finance records invoices (invoice number, customer ID, amount due, payment terms) for products or services provided to customers.
- Customers make payments (amount paid, payment method, date of payment) towards their outstanding invoices.

## 10. Analytics and Reporting

Performed By: Data Analysts

Activity:
- Data analysts collect and analyze data on user behavior, website traffic, sales, and inventory to generate reports for business decision making.
