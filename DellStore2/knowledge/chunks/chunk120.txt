 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Support Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, contact information, address, etc.) in the CUSTOMER table.
- Customer support agents update customer details as needed in the CUSTOMER table.
- Customer service representatives assign unique usernames and passwords to new customers.

## 2. Order Placement and Management

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products from the catalog and providing necessary details (order quantity, shipping address, etc.) in the CUST_HIST table.
- Sales representatives confirm order details with customers and update the CUST_HIST table accordingly.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and mark items as shipped once they are ready for dispatch.

## 3. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update the inventory levels in the CUSTOMER table when products are received or dispatched.
- Purchasing teams order new stock based on current inventory levels and sales forecasts.

## 4. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Finance Department

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices for customers based on their orders in the CUST_HIST table.
- The finance department records payments received from customers and updates the CUSTOMER table accordingly.

## 5. Customer Support and Complaints Handling

Performed By: Customer Support Agents, Quality Assurance Team

Activity:
- Customer support agents handle customer inquiries and complaints related to orders, products, or services.
- The quality assurance team reviews customer feedback and suggests improvements to the product catalog or service offerings.

## 6. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: IT Administrators, Data Governance Team

Activity:
- IT administrators ensure that data is securely stored and backed up in the DellStore2 database.
- The data governance team enforces data quality standards and ensures compliance with relevant regulations (e.g., GDPR).
