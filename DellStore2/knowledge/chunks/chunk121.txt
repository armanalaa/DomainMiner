 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their personal details, contact information, and account credentials. (cust_df)
- Customer service agents update customer profiles with changes to contact information or account settings. (CUSTOMER)
- Sales representatives assign unique customer IDs to each new account. (cust_df)

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service agents log interactions with customers, including support requests and sales inquiries. (CUST_HIST)
- Sales representatives document customer purchases and update their purchase history accordingly. (custhist_df)
- Customer service agents mark resolved cases in the customer history database. (CUST_HIST)

## 3. User Authentication and Access Control

Performed By: IT Administrators, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- IT administrators manage user accounts, setting passwords and access levels for each account.
- Customer service agents assist customers with password resets or account recovery.

## 4. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: IT Administrators, Data Governance Team

Activity:
- IT administrators implement data encryption practices to secure all user and system data.
- Data governance team ensures compliance with GDPR regulations by managing data privacy policies.

## 5. Metadata Collection and Management

Performed By: Data Governance Team, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Data governance team collects metadata structure for database management.
- IT administrators configure read-only connections to support encrypted connections.

## 6. Security Testing and Vulnerability Response

Performed By: IT Security Team

Activity:
- Independent third-party penetration tests are conducted to identify potential vulnerabilities.
- Rapid vulnerability remediation and security patch deployment are performed as needed.

## 7. Incident Response and Reporting

Performed By: IT Security Team, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- IT security team investigates and responds to security incidents, such as data breaches or unauthorized access attempts.
- Customer service agents notify affected customers of any security incidents and provide updates on the incident response.
