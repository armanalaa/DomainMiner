 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details, contact information, and account credentials. (cust_df)
- IT administrators assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer upon registration. (cust_df)
- Customer service representatives update customer details as needed, such as address or email changes. (cust_df)
- IT administrators manage customer accounts, including password resets and account deactivation when necessary. (cust_df)

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives log each interaction with a customer, including sales calls, emails, and meetings. (custhist_df)
- Customer service representatives document customer inquiries, complaints, and resolutions. (custhist_df)
- Both roles update the customer history records as needed to reflect ongoing interactions. (custhist_df)

## 3. License Management

Performed By: IT Administrators, Legal Department

Activity:
- IT administrators manage software licenses for DellStore2, including installation and updates.
- The legal department reviews and approves license agreements with vendors.

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by confirming availability, calculating prices, and providing order details to customers.
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to the customer's specified address.

## 5. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Accounting Department, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- The accounting department generates invoices based on order details and sends them to customers.
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries related to invoices, such as disputes or payment issues.
- The accounting department processes payments received from customers and updates the corresponding records.

## 6. Data Protection and Compliance

Performed By: IT Administrators, Legal Department

Activity:
- IT administrators implement data protection measures to ensure customer data privacy and security.
- The legal department ensures compliance with relevant data protection regulations and handles any related inquiries or issues.
