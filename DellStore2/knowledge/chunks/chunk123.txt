 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and assigning them a unique identifier (cust_df).
- IT support sets up customer accounts with appropriate access levels based on the assigned role (CUSTOMER).

## 2. Customer Profile Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives update customer profiles with changes in contact information or account details (CUST_DF).
- HR managers maintain employee records associated with each customer account (CUSTOMER).

## 3. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products and providing shipping information (not specified in the provided schema).
- Sales representatives confirm order details and process them for fulfillment (not specified in the provided schema).

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and update them when products are added or removed (not specified in the provided schema).
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders for replenishing low stock items (not specified in the provided schema).

## 5. Invoice Processing

Performed By: Finance, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices based on completed orders and send them to customers (not specified in the provided schema).
- Finance records incoming payments and updates customer accounts accordingly (CUST_DF).

## 6. Support Ticketing System Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Customer service representatives create support tickets for customer issues and assign them to the appropriate team (not specified in the provided schema).
- IT support resolves technical issues and updates ticket status accordingly (not specified in the provided schema).

## 7. Dataedo Product Usage

Performed By: Customer Users, Dataedo Support

Activity:
- Customer users access and utilize the Dataedo products according to their assigned roles and permissions (CUSTOMER).
- Dataedo support provides updates, enhancements, and technical assistance as needed (092_license.pdf p.4, 092_license.pdf p.6).
