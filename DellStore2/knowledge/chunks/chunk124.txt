 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and user credentials.
- IT support assigns unique customer IDs and sets up email accounts for each new customer.

## 2. Account Management

Performed By: Customer Service, IT Support

Activity:
- Customer service agents update customer information as needed, such as address changes or contact preferences.
- IT support manages password resets and account access issues.

## 3. License Agreement Management

Performed By: Legal Team, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- The legal team reviews and updates the license agreement based on changes in plans or policies.
- Sales representatives ensure that customers agree to the latest terms before granting access to the Products.

## 4. Offline License Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives determine eligibility for offline licenses and coordinate with IT support for installation and activation.
- IT support manages offline license keys and ensures compliance with the Supplement.

## 5. Legacy Plan Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Finance Team

Activity:
- Sales representatives identify customers on legacy plans and work with the finance team to renew subscriptions.
- The finance team processes payments for renewals and updates customer records accordingly.

## 6. Product License Distribution

Performed By: IT Support, Shipping Department

Activity:
- IT support generates and distributes product licenses to customers as needed.
- The shipping department ensures that physical copies of the Products are delivered to customers.

## 7. Compliance Monitoring

Performed By: Compliance Team, Legal Team

Activity:
- The compliance team monitors customer usage of the Products for proper distribution and adherence to the agreement.
- The legal team takes action in cases of non-compliance, including measures to address improper distribution or sharing.

## 8. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service, Technical Support

Activity:
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries and provide assistance with account management and product usage.
- Technical support troubleshoots issues related to the Products and assists customers with resolving technical problems.
