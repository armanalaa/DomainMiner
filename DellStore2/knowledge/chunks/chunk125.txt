 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information. (CUSTOMER table)
- IT support assigns each customer a unique identifier (cust_df table)

## 2. License Activation

Performed By: Customer Representatives, Dataedo Support

Activity:
- Customer representatives designate the license owner within their organization.
- Upon activation request, Dataedo support activates the license and grants access to the Products on the specified or defaulted activation date (Order Form, CUST_HIST table)

## 3. Subscription Management

Performed By: Customer Representatives, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customer representatives update the license owner as needed and manage subscription details such as plan upgrades and user count changes. (Order Form, CUST_HIST table)
- The finance department processes payments for increased user counts or upgraded plans during the Subscription Period.

## 4. User Access Management

Performed By: Customer Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Customer representatives assign and manage access permissions for named users within their organization. (CUST_HIST table)
- IT support ensures that only authorized users have access to the Products.

## 5. Report Generation

Performed By: Users

Activity:
- Users generate reports, exports, or other materials from the Products during the Subscription Period. (Products)

## 6. Material Sharing

Performed By: Users

Activity:
- Users share read-only materials generated by certain Product features with individuals outside of named users at no additional charge. (Products)

## 7. Termination and Cessation

Performed By: Dataedo Support, Customer Representatives

Activity:
- Dataedo support terminates the Agreement for cause upon breach by the customer and fails to cure the breach within the notice period. (Agreement)
- The customer may cease using the Products at any time but is not entitled to a refund or reimbursement of fees already paid. (Agreement)
