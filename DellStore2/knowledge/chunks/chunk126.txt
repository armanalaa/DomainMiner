 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by entering their personal and contact details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, DXPGGAE, Unnamed: 6, 00000, UK, 2, SLZCISTEWL@dell.com, 1467048332, 5)
- IT support assigns unique usernames and passwords to each customer (user10001, password)

## 2. Proof of Concept Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Support, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives offer customers free trials or paid proofs of concept for product evaluation (Trial, POC)
- Customer support assists customers in setting up and managing their trials or proofs of concept
- IT support ensures trialware is provided "as is" with no warranties (Trialware)

## 3. License Purchase and Renewal

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Finance Department, Customer Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives help customers purchase licenses after the evaluation period ends
- Finance department processes payments for license purchases
- Customer support assists existing customers in renewing their licenses or bridging intervals between subscription periods

## 4. Customer Support and Troubleshooting

Performed By: Customer Support, IT Support

Activity:
- Customer support addresses customer queries and issues related to products and services
- IT support troubleshoots technical problems reported by customers

## 5. Data Management and Backup

Performed By: IT Administrators

Activity:
- IT administrators manage and backup data stored in the database (cust_df, cust_hist, custhist_df)

## 6. Analytics and Reporting

Performed By: Data Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Data analysts collect and analyze data for business intelligence purposes
- Management uses reports to make informed decisions about the company's operations
