 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact information and user credentials. (CUSTOMER)
- IT support assigns unique customer IDs to each registered account. (CUST_DF)

## 2. Customer Account Management

Performed By: Customer Service, IT Support

Activity:
- Customer service team updates customer details as requested by the customers. (CUSTOMER)
- IT support modifies user permissions and access levels based on role changes or account status updates. (CUST_DF)

## 3. License Agreement Management

Performed By: Legal Team, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Legal team reviews and manages the license agreements with customers. (092_license.pdf)
- Sales representatives provide customers with access to supported versions of the Products. (092_license.pdf)

## 4. Product Purchase and Invoice Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Finance Team

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders and generate invoices for products or services. (Not specified in source materials)
- Finance team records the transactions, updates the customer's account balance, and sends invoices to customers. (Not specified in source materials)

## 5. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Team

Activity:
- Customers make payments towards their outstanding invoices using accepted payment methods. (092_license.pdf)
- Finance team updates the customer's account balance and marks the invoice as paid. (Not specified in source materials)

## 6. Technical Support Requests

Performed By: Customers, IT Support

Activity:
- Customers submit technical support requests for assistance with the Products. (Not specified in source materials)
- IT support addresses customer issues and provides solutions or workarounds as needed. (Not specified in source materials)

## 7. Usage Data Collection and Analysis

Performed By: Dataedo Team, Analytics Team

Activity:
- Dataedo team collects limited technical and usage information from customers interacting with the Products. (092_license.pdf)
- Analytics team analyzes the collected data to improve the Products and provide insights for decision making. (092_license.pdf)
