 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information (cust_df).
- IT support assigns unique user IDs and passwords to each customer for secure access (cust_df).

## 2. Customer Profile Management

Performed By: Customers, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customers update their personal and contact information in their profiles (CUSTOMER).
- Customer service representatives review and verify changes made to customer profiles (CUSTOMER).

## 3. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives log interactions with customers in their history (CUST_HIST).
- Data analysts analyze customer histories to identify trends and patterns (custhist_df).

## 4. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, IT Support

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the product catalog by recording their details (cust_df).
- IT support ensures that all added products are properly indexed and searchable in the catalog.

## 5. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders for products by selecting items from the catalog (CUST_HIST).
- Sales representatives review and confirm customer orders before they are processed.

## 6. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when new products arrive or existing ones are sold (cust_df).
- Purchasing teams order replenishments for items with low stock levels based on sales data.

## 7. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Finance generates invoices for customers based on their orders (cust_df).
- Customer service representatives send invoices to customers and follow up on any payment issues.

## 8. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance

Activity:
- Customers make payments towards their invoices using various methods (not specified in the provided data).
- Finance records payments received from customers and updates customer accounts accordingly (CUSTOMER).
