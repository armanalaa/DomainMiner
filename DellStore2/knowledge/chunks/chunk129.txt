 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representative, IT Administrator

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and user credentials. (cust_df)
- IT administrators assign unique customer IDs and set up email accounts for the newly registered customers. (cust_df, user10001, password)

## 2. Customer Information Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representative, IT Administrator

Activity:
- Customer service representatives update customer information as needed, such as address changes or contact preferences. (CUSTOMER)
- IT administrators maintain the integrity and security of customer data in the database. (CUSTOMER)

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Manager, IT Administrator

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the product catalog with their respective codes, names, categories, and list prices. (cust_df)
- IT administrators ensure that the updated product information is accurately reflected in the database. (cust_df)

## 4. Warranty Claim Processing

Performed By: Customer Service Representative, Quality Assurance Engineer

Activity:
- Customer service representatives receive and log warranty claims from customers. (CUSTOMER)
- Quality assurance engineers investigate the nonconforming products and determine whether to repair, replace, or terminate the affected license. (092_license.pdf p.22)

## 5. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Team, Customer Service Representative

Activity:
- Finance team generates invoices based on customer orders and sends them to customers. (not specified in the provided materials)
- Customer service representatives handle any payment-related issues or queries from customers. (not specified in the provided materials)

## 6. Order Fulfillment and Delivery Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Team, Customer Service Representative

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and prepare orders for shipment. (not specified in the provided materials)
- Shipping team arranges for the delivery of orders to customers. (not specified in the provided materials)
- Customer service representatives track order status and address any delivery-related issues or queries from customers. (not specified in the provided materials)

## 7. Customer Support and Complaint Resolution

Performed By: Customer Service Representative, Technical Support Specialist

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints, escalating them to technical support specialists when necessary. (CUSTOMER)
- Technical support specialists troubleshoot and resolve any technical issues reported by customers. (not specified in the provided materials)
