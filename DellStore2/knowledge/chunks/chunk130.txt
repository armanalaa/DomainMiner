 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and creating a user account (cust_df).
- IT administrators assign unique customer IDs and set up passwords for each new account.

## 2. Customer Profile Management

Performed By: Customers, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Customers update their personal information and contact details within their user accounts (CUSTOMER).
- IT administrators verify and approve changes to customer profiles as needed.

## 3. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products, specifying quantities, and providing shipping information (cust_df).
- Sales representatives review and confirm orders before they are processed further.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update the inventory levels when products are received or shipped (not specified in provided schema).
- IT administrators maintain an accurate record of available stock based on warehouse updates (not specified in provided schema).

## 5. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack orders according to their status and priority (not specified in provided schema).
- Shipping teams prepare and dispatch shipments to customers based on order details (not specified in provided schema).

## 6. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Teams, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Finance teams generate invoices for each completed order, including itemized product costs and shipping charges (not specified in provided schema).
- IT administrators store the generated invoices in the system for future reference (not specified in provided schema).

## 7. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Teams

Activity:
- Customers make payments through various methods, such as credit card or bank transfer (not specified in provided schema).
- Finance teams process and confirm payments, updating the customer's account status accordingly (not specified in provided schema).

## 8. Dispute Resolution

Performed By: Customer Support, Legal Teams

Activity:
- Customer support agents handle customer complaints and disputes related to orders or billing (not specified in provided schema).
- Legal teams provide guidance and assistance during the resolution process as needed.
