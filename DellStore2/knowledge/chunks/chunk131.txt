 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new users by entering their personal details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, address, email, phone number) and creating a unique username and password.
- IT administrators manage user accounts by updating account information, changing passwords, and deactivating or deleting accounts as needed.

## 2. Customer Order Placement

Performed By: Customers

Activity:
- Customers browse the catalog (SLZCISTEWL) and select products they wish to purchase, adding them to their shopping cart.
- Customers proceed to checkout, where they enter shipping information (address, phone number) and make a payment using the provided options.

## 3. Order Processing and Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Department

Activity:
- Upon receiving an order, warehouse staff locate the selected products in inventory and prepare them for shipping by packing them into appropriate boxes.
- The shipping department then labels the packages with the correct address and arrange for delivery through a courier service.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update the inventory levels (10001) when products are received or shipped out, ensuring accurate tracking of available stock.
- Purchasing teams order new products from suppliers based on current inventory levels and forecasted demand.

## 5. Financial Transactions and Reporting

Performed By: Finance Department, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Upon receiving payment for an order, finance department records the transaction (80000) and updates the customer's account balance accordingly.
- Accounting staff generate financial reports (UK, 2) detailing sales revenue, expenses, and profit margins for analysis and decision making.

## 6. Customer Support and Complaints Handling

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints related to orders, products, or account issues, providing solutions or escalating as necessary.

## 7. Database Maintenance and Backup

Performed By: IT Administrators

Activity:
- IT administrators perform regular database maintenance tasks (SQL Server 2008) to ensure optimal performance and data integrity, including indexing, backups, and recovery operations.
