 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details, such as name, email address, and phone number.
- HR managers update customer information, including personal details and employment status.

## 2. Customer Order Placement

Performed By: Customers

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products, specifying quantities, and providing shipping details.

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Warehouse staff retrieve ordered items from inventory based on order details.
- Warehouse staff package and prepare orders for shipment.

## 4. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Team

Activity:
- Finance team generates invoices based on customer orders and shipping costs.
- Finance team records payment information, such as payment method and amount, when customers make payments.

## 5. Order Tracking and Delivery

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives track order status and provide updates to customers regarding delivery progress.

## 6. Customer Support and Complaints Handling

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer complaints, providing solutions or escalating issues as necessary.

## 7. Data Analysis and Reporting

Performed By: Data Analysts

Activity:
- Data analysts analyze sales data to identify trends and patterns, generating reports for management.
