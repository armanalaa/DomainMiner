 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details, contact information, and account credentials. (CUSTOMER table)
- IT administrators assign unique customer IDs and manage user accounts, including password changes and access levels. (CUSTOMER table)
- Customer service representatives update customer profiles with address changes or other relevant information. (CUSTOMER table)

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives log interactions with customers and record the outcomes of these interactions in the customer history database (CUST_HIST table)
- Data analysts analyze customer history data to identify trends, patterns, and areas for improvement.

## 3. Product Management

Performed By: Product Managers, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Product managers add new products to the catalog by providing product details such as name, category, list price, and product code. (CUSTOMER table)
- IT administrators maintain the product database, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information for all listed items. (PRODUCT table)

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and update the database when stock changes occur. (INVENTORY table)
- Purchasing teams order new products based on inventory levels and demand forecasts.
- IT administrators manage the inventory management system, ensuring smooth operation and accurate data.

## 5. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by confirming availability, calculating shipping costs, and providing estimated delivery times. (ORDERS table)
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to customers.
- Customer service representatives update order statuses and handle any issues that arise during the fulfillment process.

## 6. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Teams, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Finance teams generate invoices based on customer orders and send them to customers. (INVOICES table)
- Customer service representatives handle any payment-related issues or inquiries from customers.

## 7. Error Handling and System Maintenance

Performed By: IT Administrators, Developers

Activity:
- IT administrators implement error handling measures to ensure the web driver program can gracefully handle unexpected HTTP errors. (WEB DRIVER PROGRAM)
- Developers modify data generation C programs to generate CSV files for any database size and eliminate the need for conversion tools. (ds2_create_cust.c, ds2_create_inv.c, ds2_create_orders.c, ds2_create_prod.c)
