 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new users by entering their personal details, contact information, and account credentials. (CUSTOMER)
- IT administrators assign unique user IDs and passwords to each registered customer. (CUSTOMER)
- Customer service representatives update customer profiles with changes in personal or contact information. (CUSTOMER)
- IT administrators manage user accounts, including creating, modifying, and deleting accounts as needed.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by selecting items from the catalog, calculating totals, and confirming availability. (cust_df)
- Warehouse staff pick and pack ordered items for shipment, updating order status accordingly. (cust_df)

## 3. Inventory Management

Performed By: Inventory Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Inventory managers maintain product information in the catalog, including product codes, names, categories, and list prices. (cust_df)
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and update them when items are added or removed from stock. (cust_df)

## 4. Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- The finance department records customer payments, including transaction amounts and dates. (not specified in provided schema)

## 5. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints, providing assistance and resolving issues as needed. (not specified in provided schema)

## 6. Accounting and Reporting

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- The finance department generates financial reports, including sales revenue, expenses, and profit margins. (not specified in provided schema)
