 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customer accounts by entering their personal details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, DXPGGAE, Unnamed: 4, Dell Way, email, phone number, password).
- HR managers assign unique usernames and update customer account information.
- IT administrators manage customer account security and access permissions.

## 2. Customer Interaction Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives document interactions with customers (cust_df) to follow up on sales leads or provide support.
- Customer service agents record customer inquiries and responses (custhist_df, cust_HIST).

## 3. Cookies Management

Performed By: IT Administrators, Marketing Analysts

Activity:
- IT administrators implement cookies for load balancing, statistics collection, and user experience optimization (lidc, _clck, _clsk, _cltk, _ga, _ga_#).
- Marketing analysts track visitor behavior across devices and marketing channels (_ga, _ga_#, ajs_anonymous_id, ajs_anonymous_id Calendly, dd_cookie_test_#, dtCookie, dtPC, gsc Getsitecontrol).

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels (not specified in the provided schema) as items are received or shipped.
- Purchasing teams place orders for new stock based on inventory levels and demand forecasts.

## 5. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives confirm customer orders (not specified in the provided schema).
- Customer service agents process order changes or cancellations.
- Warehouse staff pick and pack items for shipment.

## 6. Shipping and Delivery

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Courier Services

Activity:
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipping (not specified in the provided schema).
- Courier services deliver packages to customers' addresses.

## 7. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Department, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Finance department generates invoices based on order details (not specified in the provided schema).
- Customer service agents address billing inquiries or handle payment issues.

## 8. Analytics and Reporting

Performed By: Marketing Analysts, Business Intelligence Analysts

Activity:
- Marketing analysts analyze visitor behavior data from cookies (lidc, _clck, _clsk, _cltk, _ga, _ga_#, ajs_anonymous_id, ajs_anonymous_id Calendly, dd_cookie_test_#, dtCookie, dtPC, gsc Getsitecontrol).
- Business Intelligence Analysts generate reports on sales, inventory, and customer behavior trends.
