 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, email, phone number, address).
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer.
- IT support maintains the security of customer data in the database.

## 2. Customer Interaction Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives track customer interactions related to product inquiries or orders (cust_df).
- Customer service agents record details of customer complaints or feedback (custhist_df).

## 3. Website Behavior Analysis

Performed By: IT Analysts, Marketing Managers

Activity:
- IT analysts monitor and analyze visitor behavior on the website using cookies such as gscs, orionV3#identity, rxvisitid, rxVisitor, userleap.ids, sentryReplaySession, vuid (SOURCE MATERIALS).
- Marketing managers use this data to optimize website content and display targeted ads.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update the inventory levels when items are received or shipped (cust_df).
- Purchasing teams order new stock based on current inventory levels and sales forecasts.

## 5. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives confirm customer orders and provide estimated delivery times (cust_df).
- Customer service agents handle any order-related inquiries or issues.
- Warehouse staff prepare the ordered items for shipment.

## 6. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Department, Accounts Receivable Team

Activity:
- Finance department generates invoices based on customer orders (cust_df).
- Accounts receivable team records payments received from customers.

## 7. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, IT Support

Activity:
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries or complaints regarding products or services.
- IT support assists customers with technical issues related to the website or software.
