 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and preferences. (cust_df)
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer account. (cust_df)
- IT support ensures secure storage of customer data and maintains system integrity.
- Customer service representatives assist customers with account inquiries or issues.

## 2. Customer Interaction Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives document interactions with customers, including sales calls and emails. (cust_df)
- Customer service representatives log customer support requests and responses. (custhist_df)
- Both roles update the customer's interaction history accordingly.

## 3. Marketing Campaign Management

Performed By: Marketing Team, IT Support

Activity:
- Marketing team designs and implements targeted marketing campaigns for specific customer segments.
- IT support ensures proper tracking of campaign performance through cookies and other digital tools. (033_cookie-declaration.pdf)

## 4. Customer Segmentation and Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Marketing Team

Activity:
- Data analysts collect and analyze customer data to identify patterns and trends. (033_cookie-declaration.pdf, custhist_df)
- Marketing team uses these insights to segment customers and tailor marketing strategies accordingly.

## 5. Customer Support and Troubleshooting

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Customer service representatives address customer concerns and provide solutions or escalate issues as needed.
- IT support troubleshoots technical problems related to the customer's account or website experience.

## 6. Account Maintenance and Security

Performed By: HR Managers, IT Support

Activity:
- HR managers manage user account changes, such as password resets or role modifications. (cust_df)
- IT support ensures the security of customer accounts by implementing security measures and monitoring for suspicious activity.
