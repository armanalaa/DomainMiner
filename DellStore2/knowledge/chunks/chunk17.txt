 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, email addresses, phone numbers, job titles, company names, URLs, locations, public social media profiles).
- HR managers update customer information when employees change roles or leave the company.
- IT support maintains customer data integrity and security.

## 2. Customer Support Ticketing

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives log support tickets from customers regarding product issues, billing inquiries, or account management requests.
- They assign tickets to relevant teams for resolution and update ticket statuses as needed.
- They communicate with customers about the progress of their tickets.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Marketing Team

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, and list prices.
- The marketing team updates product descriptions, images, and promotional offers.

## 4. Order Placement and Processing

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Customers place orders through the website or by contacting sales representatives.
- Sales representatives confirm order details and process them in the system.
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to customers.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and update them when items are received or shipped.
- Purchasing teams order new stock based on sales data and inventory levels.

## 6. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices for customers based on their orders.
- Finance records invoice details (invoices numbers, dates, amounts, payment terms) and tracks payments.

## 7. Financial Reporting and Analysis

Performed By: Finance, Management

Activity:
- Finance generates financial reports (revenue, expenses, profit margins, etc.) for management review.
- Management analyzes these reports to make informed decisions about business strategy and operations.
