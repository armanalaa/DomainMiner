 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, email, address, phone number, country) and setting up user accounts with passwords.
- IT support assigns unique customer IDs (10001) and sets up access permissions for each account.

## 2. Product Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, and list prices.
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels (1467048332 Dell Way) when new products are added or existing ones are sold.

## 3. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and provide support by accessing customer details (email, phone number) and product information.

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Finance Department

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by confirming availability, calculating totals, and scheduling delivery times.
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipping by packing products and updating inventory levels.
- Finance department records sales (80000) and generates invoices.

## 5. Invoice Processing

Performed By: Finance Department, Accounts Receivable

Activity:
- Finance department sends invoices to customers (email) and updates the customer history table (CUST_HIST).
- Accounts receivable tracks payments and updates account balances accordingly.

## 6. Privacy Compliance

Performed By: IT Support, Data Management Team

Activity:
- IT support collects data on product usage, user activity, and system events (date, time, IP address) for privacy compliance purposes.
- Data management team ensures data is anonymized, encrypted, and securely stored in the metadata repository.

## 7. Product Updates and Maintenance

Performed By: IT Support, Development Team

Activity:
- IT support installs product updates on customer systems (product, edition, version) to ensure they have the latest features and security patches.
- Development team addresses any issues or bugs reported by customers and releases updates as needed.

## 8. Analytics and Reporting

Performed By: Data Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Data analysts generate reports on sales performance, customer behavior, and system usage to help management make informed decisions.
- Management reviews reports and adjusts strategies based on insights gained from the data.
