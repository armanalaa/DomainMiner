 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, email address, phone number, country, etc.) and creating a unique customer ID (10001).
- HR managers assign roles and permissions to each registered user based on the provided role (DXPGGAE) and password.

## 2. Subscription Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives manage customer subscriptions by updating subscription details (IP address, time of subscription).
- They also handle unsubscribing requests by removing customers from the newsletter list (custhist_df).

## 3. Data Collection and Profiling

Performed By: Automated Systems, Manual Profilers

Activity:
- Automated systems collect data on customer behavior (webinars attended, blog posts read) for profiling purposes.
- Manual profilers analyze the collected data to create customer profiles based on their interests and preferences.

## 4. Privacy Policy Compliance

Performed By: Data Protection Officers

Activity:
- Data protection officers ensure compliance with privacy policies by restricting access to personal data (email address, IP address) to authorized personnel only.
- They also anonymize personal data when necessary and securely store it in databases.

## 5. Data Security Measures

Performed By: IT Administrators

Activity:
- IT administrators implement data security measures such as encryption (SSL certificate) and password protection to safeguard customer data.

## 6. Consent Management

Performed By: Customers, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customers provide their consent for data processing by subscribing to the newsletter.
- Customer service representatives record the consent details (IP address, time of subscription) and retain this information as long as the customer remains a subscriber.

## 7. Data Access and Retrieval

Performed By: Customers, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customers can access their personal data by logging into their accounts (using the unique customer ID).
- Customer service representatives retrieve customer data for various purposes (e.g., account management, support requests) from the customer database (CUSTOMER).
