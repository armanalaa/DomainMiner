 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and preferences (cust_df).
- HR managers create employee accounts for new hires with login credentials (cust_df).
- IT support troubleshoots any issues related to customer account access or functionality (cust_df).

## 2. Customer Data Maintenance

Performed By: Customers, Customer Support

Activity:
- Customers update their personal and contact information as needed (cust_df).
- Customer support assists customers with any changes to their account details (cust_df).

## 3. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives log customer interactions and purchase history (CUST_HIST).
- Customer support reviews customer histories to address inquiries or resolve issues (CUST_HIST).

## 4. Privacy Policy Compliance

Performed By: Data Protection Officers, IT Support

Activity:
- Data Protection Officers ensure compliance with privacy policies and data protection regulations (034_privacy.pdf).
- IT support implements measures to protect customer data according to established policies (034_privacy.pdf).

## 5. Marketing and Advertising

Performed By: Marketing Team, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Marketing team develops targeted marketing campaigns based on customer behavior and preferences (not directly related to database content).
- Sales representatives utilize customer data for personalized outreach and follow-ups (not directly related to database content).

## 6. Data Sharing with Resellers

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Reseller Partners

Activity:
- Sales representatives share customer data with reseller partners as needed for streamlined customer interactions (034_privacy.pdf).
- Reseller partners use shared data to tailor their offerings to the local language, culture, and laws (034_privacy.pdf).

## 7. California Consumer Privacy Act Compliance

Performed By: Data Protection Officers, IT Support

Activity:
- Data Protection Officers ensure compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act for customers residing in California (CCPA).
- IT support implements measures to protect customer data according to CCPA regulations.
