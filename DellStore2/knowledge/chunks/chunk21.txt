 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal information (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, email, address, phone number, country, password).
- IT administrators assign unique customer IDs (10001) and create user accounts.
- Customer service representatives update customer details as needed.

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives log interactions with customers (cust_df columns).
- Data analysts analyze customer history data to identify trends and patterns.

## 3. Privacy Request Handling

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts, Legal Team

Activity:
- Customers can request their personal information, categories of sources, purposes for use, disclosure categories, and sales or sharing details (cust_df and custhist_df columns).
- Customer service representatives verify the requests and provide the required information.
- Legal team ensures compliance with data privacy regulations during the process.

## 4. Consent Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Administrators, Legal Team

Activity:
- Customer service representatives obtain consent for data collection, use, and sharing (cust_df columns).
- IT administrators implement user-enabled global privacy controls to opt-out of sale or sharing.
- Legal team ensures compliance with data privacy regulations during the process.

## 5. Data Correction

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Customers can request corrections for inaccurate personal information (cust_df columns).
- Customer service representatives verify and update the information.
- IT administrators implement the changes in the database.

## 6. Sensitive Data Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts, Legal Team, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Customer service representatives limit use and disclosure of sensitive personal information (cust_df columns).
- Data analysts analyze the data while respecting the limitations.
- Legal team ensures compliance with data privacy regulations during the process.

## 7. Data Portability Request Handling

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Customers can request their personal data in a usable format (cust_df columns).
- Data analysts prepare the data in the requested format.
- IT administrators provide the data to the customer.
