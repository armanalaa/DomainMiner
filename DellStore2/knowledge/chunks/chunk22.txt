 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle inquiries related to the privacy policy by responding to emails or phone calls.
- Sales representatives process customer requests for product demonstrations and book appointments.

## 2. New Customer Registration

Performed By: Customers, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customers register new accounts by providing personal details such as name, email address, and password.
- Customer service representatives verify the provided information and activate the account.

## 3. Customer Data Maintenance

Performed By: Customers, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customers update their personal data through the account settings.
- Customer service representatives make changes to customer data when necessary.

## 4. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives assist customers with technical issues or product-related questions.

## 5. Customer Inquiry Response

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives respond to customer inquiries and provide solutions or relevant information.

## 6. Data Governance

Performed By: Dataedo Team

Activity:
- The Dataedo team manages the data governance project, including data cataloging, data quality checks, and continuous monitoring.

## 7. Ticket Submission

Performed By: Customers, Support Team

Activity:
- Customers submit support tickets to report issues or request assistance.
- The support team processes the tickets and assigns them to the appropriate team member for resolution.
