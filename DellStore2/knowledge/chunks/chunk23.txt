 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and preferences.
- Customer service agents update customer information when contacted with changes.
- Sales representatives assign customer accounts to specific sales teams for targeted marketing efforts.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Finance Department

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by entering product details and shipping information.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and prepare items for shipment based on order details.
- Finance department records the financial aspects of each order, including payment and delivery terms.

## 3. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when items are received or shipped out.
- Purchasing teams place orders for new stock based on current inventory levels and sales forecasts.

## 4. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- Finance department generates invoices for customers based on their orders and payment terms.

## 5. Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Department, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Finance department records customer payments and updates account balances accordingly.
- Customer service agents address any payment-related issues or disputes that arise.

## 6. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Customer service agents handle inquiries, complaints, and technical support requests from customers.

## 7. Data Governance

Performed By: IT Department, Data Governance Team

Activity:
- IT department maintains the DellStore2 database and ensures its security and integrity.
- Data governance team manages data lineage, quality, and cataloging for better data governance across the organization.
