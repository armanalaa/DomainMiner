 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their contact information and creating a customer profile.
- HR managers update customer profiles with employment details when a customer is hired as an employee.

## 2. Account Maintenance

Performed By: Customers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers manage their account details, such as password changes and address updates.
- The finance department reviews and approves any requested changes to ensure accuracy.

## 3. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives respond to customer inquiries and provide assistance with product information or technical issues.

## 4. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when products are received or sold.

## 5. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by selecting items from the catalog and confirming availability.
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipment once they are ready to be dispatched.

## 6. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Department, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- The finance department generates invoices based on customer orders and sends them to the customers.
- Sales representatives review and confirm the accuracy of the invoice before it is sent.

## 7. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers make payments towards their outstanding invoices through various payment methods.
- The finance department records the payments and updates customer accounts accordingly.

## 8. Data Governance

Performed By: Data Management Team

Activity:
- The data management team ensures data quality, consistency, and accuracy by monitoring data lineage, detecting anomalies, and maintaining compliance with regulations.
