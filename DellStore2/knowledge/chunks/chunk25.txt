 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customer details such as name, contact information, and address.
- Customer service agents update customer records with changes in contact information or account status.
- Sales representatives assign customers to specific sales territories based on geographical location.
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries and complaints, updating the interaction history accordingly.

## 2. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the product catalog with details like product code, name, category, and list price.
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when products are received or shipped out.
- Product managers monitor stock levels and reorder products when necessary.

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by selecting appropriate items from the catalog and providing order details.
- Warehouse staff pick and pack ordered items for shipment.
- Customer service agents confirm order status and provide tracking information to customers.

## 4. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Department, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices based on customer orders and send them to the relevant customers.
- The finance department records invoice details in the system, including invoice number, date, total amount, and customer information.

## 5. Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Department, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Customers make payments through various methods (e.g., credit card, bank transfer).
- Customer service agents update the payment status in the system for each transaction.
- The finance department reconciles payments with invoices and marks them as paid.

## 6. Accounting

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- The finance department records financial transactions, including income from sales and expenses.
- They generate financial reports such as balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow statements.

## 7. Data Governance

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- The data governance team ensures that data is accurate, consistent, and secure across the organization.
- They implement data quality checks, data lineage tracking, and data classification rules.
- They manage roles and workflows related to data access and modification.
