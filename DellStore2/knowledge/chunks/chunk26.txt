 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact information and creating a user account.
- HR managers assign roles and permissions to each customer account.
- IT administrators manage the security of customer accounts, including password resets and access controls.

## 2. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the product catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.
- Data analysts maintain the accuracy and completeness of the product data in the catalog.

## 3. Customer Support Ticketing

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives create support tickets for customers to track issues and requests.

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by confirming availability and shipping details.
- Warehouse staff prepare and ship the ordered products.

## 5. Invoice Generation and Payment

Performed By: Finance Team, Accounts Receivable Team

Activity:
- Finance team generates invoices based on customer orders and sends them to customers.
- Accounts receivable team records payments received from customers.

## 6. Data Catalog Management

Performed By: Data Stewards, Business Users

Activity:
- Data stewards maintain the accuracy and completeness of metadata in the data catalog.
- Business users contribute knowledge about their data assets to improve discoverability and understanding.

## 7. Data Lineage Visualization

Performed By: Data Stewards, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Data stewards visualize data flow through systems to improve transparency and troubleshoot issues faster.
- IT administrators ensure trusted data by equipping data stewards with powerful documentation tools.

## 8. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: Data Stewards, Business Users, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Data stewards discover and classify sensitive data, assign ownership, and manage roles for audit-readiness and regulatory compliance.
- Business users and IT administrators collaborate to strengthen data governance and reduce organizational risk.
