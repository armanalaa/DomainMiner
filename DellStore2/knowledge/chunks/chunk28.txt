 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information. (CUSTOMER table)
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer for secure access to their accounts. (CUSTOMER table)
- Sales representatives update customer information as needed, such as address changes or contact preferences. (CUSTOMER table)
- HR managers manage employee data related to customers, including personnel records and performance evaluations.

## 2. Customer Interaction History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives document interactions with customers, such as phone calls, emails, or meetings, for future reference. (CUST_HIST table)
- Customer service agents record details of customer inquiries and support requests, including resolution steps taken. (CUST_HIST table)
- Both sales representatives and customer service agents update the interaction history to reflect ongoing communication with customers. (CUST_HIST table)

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Governance Team

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product code, name, category, and list price. (not specified table)
- The data governance team ensures that the product catalog is accurate, complete, and up-to-date by performing regular quality checks and addressing any errors or anomalies.

## 4. Data Lineage Documentation

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- The data governance team tracks how data flows across systems to understand its transformations, dependencies, and impacts. (not specified table)
- They document this information to ensure transparency, support compliance, and reduce errors in the data ecosystem.

## 5. Data Access Control

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- The data governance team controls who can view or edit different parts of the catalog to maintain security and accountability across the organization. (not specified table)
- They assign clear ownership, manage approvals, and ensure that everyone follows established data governance policies.
