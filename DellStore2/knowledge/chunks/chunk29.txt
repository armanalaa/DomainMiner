 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by entering their details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, contact information, address) into the customer database.
- IT support assigns a unique customer ID (10001) and sets up user accounts with login credentials (user10001, password).

## 2. Customer Information Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives update customer information as needed (address changes, contact details).
- HR managers maintain employee records related to each customer account (name, email, phone number).

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, IT Support

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the product catalog by recording product code, name, category, and list price.
- IT support ensures that the updated product information is reflected in the customer database.

## 4. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders for products by selecting items from the catalog and specifying quantities.
- Sales representatives confirm orders and process them in the system.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, IT Support

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and update them when new products are received or sold.
- IT support ensures that the updated inventory information is reflected in the customer database.

## 6. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Department

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack orders for shipment.
- Shipping department labels packages and arranges for delivery.

## 7. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Finance Department

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices based on order details and send them to customers.
- Finance department records invoice information in the customer database.

## 8. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers make payments towards their invoices using various methods (credit card, bank transfer).
- Finance department updates payment status and amount in the customer database.

## 9. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer queries and complaints related to orders, products, or account information.
- IT support troubleshoots technical issues reported by customers.
