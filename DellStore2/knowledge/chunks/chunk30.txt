 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and update customer details (cust_df).
- Sales managers assign sales leads to appropriate representatives based on geographical territory or product interest (cust_df).
- Sales representatives follow up with potential customers, providing product information and negotiating deals (cust_df).

## 2. Customer Registration

Performed By: New Accounts Team

Activity:
- The new accounts team creates customer profiles for new clients during onboarding, capturing personal details and contact information (CUSTOMER).
- They assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer for secure access to the DellStore2 platform.

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives confirm orders with customers, recording order details (cust_df) and generating order numbers.
- Warehouse staff retrieve ordered items from inventory and prepare them for shipping (not specified in the provided schema).

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels as they process orders, marking items as shipped or restocking shelves (not specified in the provided schema).
- Purchasing teams order new stock when inventory levels fall below a predetermined threshold (not specified in the provided schema).

## 5. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- The finance department generates invoices for customers based on their orders, including item details, quantities, and prices (cust_df).
- They send invoices to customers via email or mail, along with payment instructions (not specified in the provided schema).

## 6. Invoice Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- The finance department records customer payments against outstanding invoices (cust_df).
- They update customer accounts to reflect paid invoices and any remaining balances.

## 7. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Technical Support Team

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer complaints or issues, providing support and troubleshooting assistance (not specified in the provided schema).
- The technical support team investigates and resolves complex technical problems reported by customers.
