 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details and contact information (cust_df).
- HR managers update customer records with employment history and relevant documents (CUSTOMER).

## 2. Customer History Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives log customer interactions, including sales transactions and feedback (custhist_df).
- Customer service representatives update the customer history with support tickets and resolution details (CUST_HIST).

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product code, name, category, and list price (cust_df).
- Warehouse staff update product availability and location details based on inventory levels (not specified in provided schema).

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by selecting products, calculating totals, and confirming shipping details (cust_df, custhist_df).
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to the customer's specified address.

## 5. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Team

Activity:
- Finance team generates invoices based on order details and shipping costs (custhist_df).
- Invoices are sent to customers via email for payment processing (cust_df).

## 6. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Team

Activity:
- Customers make payments through various methods such as credit card or bank transfer.
- Finance team records and verifies customer payments (not specified in provided schema).

## 7. Support Ticketing

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives create support tickets for customers experiencing issues with products or services (CUST_HIST).
- They track ticket status, assign to relevant teams, and communicate updates to the customer.
