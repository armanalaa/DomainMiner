 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal and contact details.
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer for account access.
- Customer service representatives update customer information as needed.

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives log interactions with customers and their respective outcomes.
- Data analysts review customer histories to identify trends and patterns for business insights.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product code, name, category, and list price.
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels as products are sold or received.

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by selecting items from the catalog and confirming availability.
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipping upon confirmation.

## 5. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- The finance department generates invoices based on processed orders, including item details, quantities, prices, and totals.

## 6. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customer service representatives assist customers with payment inquiries and process payments received.
- The finance department records payments and updates customer accounts accordingly.

## 7. Shipping Coordination

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Department

Activity:
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipping by packaging items and affixing shipping labels.
- The shipping department arranges delivery of orders to customers' specified addresses.
