 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details and contact information (cust_df).
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer for account access (cust_df).

## 2. Customer History Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives log customer interactions, including sales transactions and product inquiries (custhist_df).
- Customer service representatives update customer histories with contact details changes or account status updates (custhist_df).

## 3. Product Data Governance

Performed By: Data Stewards, Business Analysts

Activity:
- Data stewards maintain a business glossary for product-related terms and KPIs (040_product__data-governance.pdf p.9).
- Business analysts document relationships between products and their associated data assets (040_product__data-governance.pdf p.10).

## 4. Data Catalog Management

Performed By: Data Stewards, Business Analysts

Activity:
- Data stewards create a searchable catalog of product data assets for easy access across the organization (040_product__data-governance.pdf p.8).
- Business analysts organize and document metadata related to products in the data catalog (040_product__data-governance.pdf p.8).

## 5. Workflow Management

Performed By: Data Stewards, Approval Managers

Activity:
- Data stewards create workflows for product data reviews and approvals to keep documentation up to date (040_product__data-governance.pdf p.10).
- Approval managers review and approve changes to product data within the established workflows.

## 6. Data Lineage Tracking

Performed By: Data Analysts, Data Stewards

Activity:
- Data analysts visualize how product data flows across systems to understand its origin and downstream impacts (040_product__data-governance.pdf p.11).
- Data stewards trace issues back to their source when analyzing product data lineage.

## 7. Sensitive Data Discovery

Performed By: Data Stewards, Compliance Officers

Activity:
- Data stewards automatically detect and classify sensitive or regulated product data (like PII or financial fields) to support compliance and reduce risk (040_product__data-governance.pdf p.11).
- Compliance officers monitor and ensure adherence to data privacy regulations related to product data.
