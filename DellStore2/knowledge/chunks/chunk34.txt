 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customer accounts by recording contact details and preferences (cust_df).
- Customer service agents update customer information as needed (cust_df).
- Sales representatives assign customers to appropriate sales territories based on location (cust_df).

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service agents record interactions with customers and their outcomes (custhist_df).
- Sales representatives access customer history to inform sales strategies (custhist_df).

## 3. Data Governance Administration

Performed By: Data Governance Administrators

Activity:
- Data governance administrators document data assets, including their definitions and lineage (040_product__data-governance.pdf).
- Data governance administrators ensure data quality by implementing and monitoring validation rules (041_product__data-quality.pdf).

## 4. Data Catalog Management

Performed By: Data Governance Administrators

Activity:
- Data governance administrators maintain a centralized, easy-to-use data catalog that includes all data assets and their quality scores (040_product__data-governance.pdf).

## 5. Data Quality Assessment

Performed By: Data Governance Administrators

Activity:
- Data governance administrators perform automated data quality checks to ensure reliable and trustworthy data (041_product__data-quality.pdf).

## 6. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Customer service agents respond to customer inquiries and provide assistance as needed (customer support tickets or emails).

## 7. Dataedo Platform Usage

Performed By: Data Governance Administrators, Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Users access the Dataedo platform to manage data assets, implement data quality rules, and view data catalogs (Dataedo platform login).
