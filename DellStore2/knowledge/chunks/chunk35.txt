 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact information and creating a customer account (cust_df).
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer for account access.
- IT support ensures secure storage of customer data in the DellStore2 database.
- Customer service representatives update customer details as needed (CUSTOMER).

## 2. Customer Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products and providing shipping information (cust_df).
- Sales representatives confirm order details with customers and process the orders in the system.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, IT Support

Activity:
- Product managers update product information such as name, category, list price, and images (cust_df).
- IT support ensures that the updated product data is accurately reflected in the DellStore2 database.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and update them as products are sold or received (cust_df).
- Purchasing teams order new stock when inventory levels fall below a certain threshold.

## 5. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Finance Department

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices based on customer orders (cust_df).
- The finance department reviews the invoices for accuracy before sending them to customers.
- Customers make payments through various methods, and the finance department records these transactions.

## 6. Data Quality Assurance and Improvement

Performed By: Data Analysts, IT Support

Activity:
- Data analysts run automated quality checks on datasets to uncover errors, anomalies, and inconsistencies (cust_df, custhist_df).
- IT support implements fixes for identified issues and monitors data quality over time.
- The community provides feedback on potential data issues, which are addressed by the data team.

## 7. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: Data Governance Team, Legal Department

Activity:
- The data governance team ensures that all data is stored securely and in compliance with relevant regulations (CUSTOMER).
- The legal department reviews and updates the privacy policy as needed to protect customer information.
