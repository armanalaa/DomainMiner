 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details and contact information (cust_df).
- HR managers update customer account details when necessary (CUSTOMER).

## 2. Customer History Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives log customer interactions and transactions (custhist_df).
- Customer service representatives access and review a customer's history during support calls (CUST_HIST).

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product code, name, category, and list price (cust_df).
- Warehouse staff update product availability based on inventory levels (not specified in provided schema).

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and manage stock movements (not specified in provided schema).
- Purchasing teams order new products based on inventory levels and demand forecasts (not specified in provided schema).

## 5. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by confirming availability and shipping details (not specified in provided schema).
- Customer service representatives update order status and notify customers about shipment (custhist_df).
- Warehouse staff prepare and ship ordered products (not specified in provided schema).

## 6. Financial Management

Performed By: Finance Teams, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Finance teams record financial transactions related to sales, expenses, and assets (not specified in provided schema).
- Accounting staff generate invoices based on order details and customer account information (custhist_df).

## 7. Data Quality Assurance

Performed By: Data Quality Analysts

Activity:
- Data quality analysts monitor data quality across all databases to ensure accuracy, completeness, consistency, and reliability (not specified in provided schema).
