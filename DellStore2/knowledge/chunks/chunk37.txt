 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints by referring to the customer's contact information and account history.
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their personal details, contact information, and account creation date.
- Sales representatives update customer accounts with purchase history and payment details.

## 2. Product Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the product catalog by recording their product code, name, category, and list price.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by updating the quantity of each item in stock.

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by selecting items from the catalog, specifying quantities, and calculating total costs.
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipment by picking items from inventory and packaging them for delivery.

## 4. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- The finance department generates invoices based on customer orders and shipping information.
- The finance department records the invoice number, date, total cost, and payment terms in the invoice database.

## 5. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers make payments by providing their payment information and selecting a payment method.
- The finance department records customer payments and updates the corresponding customer accounts.

## 6. Shipping and Delivery

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Carriers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipment by packaging them and affixing shipping labels.
- Carriers transport orders to their destinations according to the specified delivery address and method.

## 7. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives respond to customer inquiries and complaints related to orders, shipments, and products.
- Customer service representatives escalate issues to relevant departments as needed.

## 8. Data Governance

Performed By: Data Management Team

Activity:
- The data management team ensures the accuracy and consistency of data across all databases by maintaining business glossaries and data dictionaries.
- The data management team links definitions to their actual technical data and meaning, bridging the gap between business and technical users.
