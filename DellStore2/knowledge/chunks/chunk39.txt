 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customer accounts by recording contact details and preferences.
- Sales managers update customer account information when changes are reported.
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints, documenting interactions and solutions.

## 2. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the catalog by recording product details such as name, category, and list price.
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when stock changes occur.

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by confirming availability and shipping details.
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to customers.

## 4. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- The finance department generates invoices based on order information and sends them to customers.

## 5. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer payments and update account balances accordingly.
- The finance department records payment transactions and reconciles accounts.

## 6. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives address customer support requests, providing assistance with product usage or troubleshooting issues.

## 7. Data Governance

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- The data governance team maintains a business glossary to ensure consistent definitions and policies across the organization.
