 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, Customers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details, usernames, and passwords. (CUSTOMER)
- HR managers create employee accounts for new hires with personal information, roles, and access levels. (CUSTOMER)
- Customers update their profile information as needed, including addresses, phone numbers, and email addresses. (CUSTOMER)

## 2. Customer Support Interaction

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives respond to customer inquiries regarding products, orders, or account issues. (Not directly related to DellStore2 database)

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, and list prices. (Not directly related to DellStore2 database)

## 4. Order Placement and Processing

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders for products by selecting items, quantities, and shipping details. (Not directly related to DellStore2 database)
- Sales representatives review and confirm customer orders before they are processed. (Not directly related to DellStore2 database)

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update the inventory levels when products are received or shipped, recording quantities for each item. (Not directly related to DellStore2 database)

## 6. Order Fulfillment and Shipping

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack orders based on shipping details and inventory levels. (Not directly related to DellStore2 database)
- Shipping teams prepare and send out orders to customers. (Not directly related to DellStore2 database)

## 7. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Teams, Customers

Activity:
- Finance teams generate invoices for completed orders based on order details and pricing information. (Not directly related to DellStore2 database)
- Customers pay their invoices through various payment methods, which are recorded in the system. (Not directly related to DellStore2 database)

## 8. Customer Complaints and Returns Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer complaints and initiate return requests if necessary. (Not directly related to DellStore2 database)
- Warehouse staff process returns by inspecting items, updating inventory levels, and shipping the returned products back to the warehouse. (Not directly related to DellStore2 database)
