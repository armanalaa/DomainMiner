 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details, such as name, email, phone number, and address.
- HR managers update customer information when necessary, including changes to personal data or account status.

## 2. Customer Interaction Logging

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives log interactions with customers, such as calls, emails, and meetings, for future reference.
- Customer service agents document customer inquiries and responses to ensure effective resolution of issues.

## 3. Order Placement and Processing

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products, specifying quantities, and providing shipping details.
- Sales representatives confirm order details and process them for fulfillment.
- Warehouse staff prepare the ordered items for shipment.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when products are received or shipped out.
- Purchasing teams order new stock based on current inventory levels and sales forecasts.

## 5. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers make payments for their orders using various payment methods.
- The finance department records the transactions and updates customer accounts accordingly.

## 6. Invoice Generation and Sending

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- The finance department generates invoices based on order details and sends them to customers.

## 7. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: Data Governance Team, IT Department

Activity:
- The data governance team ensures that sensitive customer information is protected and properly classified.
- The IT department implements measures to maintain data security and comply with regulations such as PCI.
