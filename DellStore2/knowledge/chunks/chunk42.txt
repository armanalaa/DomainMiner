 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by entering their details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, contact information, etc.) into the customer table.
- HR managers update customer records with employment-related data (UK, 2, DXPGGAE, Unnamed: 6, etc.).

## 2. Customer Support and Inquiry Handling

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives respond to inquiries from customers by accessing their records in the customer table.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the product catalog by recording their details (product code, name, category, list price).
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels for specific products in the warehouse stock table.

## 4. Order Processing and Fulfillment

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by selecting items from the product catalog and updating the order status in the customer_hist table.
- Warehouse staff pick and pack ordered items for shipment.

## 5. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Accounting Team

Activity:
- Accounting team generates invoices based on order details from the customer_hist table.
- Accounting team records payments received from customers in the customer table.

## 6. Data Governance and Quality Assurance

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- Data governance team reviews data quality and ensures compliance with company policies by analyzing data from various tables (customer, cust_df, custhist_df).

## 7. Community Management and Collaboration

Performed By: Community Members

Activity:
- Community members share observations, ask questions, and provide answers about data assets in the community platform.
- Community members rate data assets on a 5-point scale to improve their quality.
