 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their personal and contact details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, 1467048332 Dell Way, Unnamed: 4, DXPGGAE, Unnamed: 6, 00000, UK, 2, SLZCISTEWL@dell.com, 1467048332, 5).
- IT support assigns a unique customer ID (10001) and sets up user accounts with passwords.
- IT support creates and manages customer profiles in the database.

## 2. Customer Profile Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives update customer profiles with changes to personal or contact information (SLZCISTEWL@dell.com, 1467048332).
- Customer service representatives view and access customer profiles for assistance or inquiries.

## 3. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products, quantities, and shipping details (ProductID, list price).
- Sales representatives confirm order details and process the transactions in the system.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels for each product (ProductID).
- Purchasing teams order new stock when inventory reaches a minimum threshold.

## 5. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship orders to customers based on their shipping details (SLZCISTEWL@dell.com).
- Warehouse staff update the order status in the system when an order is shipped.

## 6. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- Finance department generates invoices for each order based on the list price and quantity (list price, ProductID).
- Finance department sends invoices to customers via email (SLZCISTEWL@dell.com).

## 7. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers pay their invoices through various payment methods (80000, 44).
- Finance department records payments and updates customer accounts accordingly.

## 8. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints related to orders, products, or services (ProductID, list price).
- Customer service representatives escalate issues to relevant teams for resolution.

## 9. Data Maintenance and Analysis

Performed By: IT Support, Data Analysts

Activity:
- IT support maintains the database structure and ensures data integrity (cust_df, custhist_df).
- Data analysts analyze sales trends, customer behavior, and inventory performance using the stored data (ProductID, list price, order history).
