 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details and contact information (cust_df).
- HR managers update customer profiles with employment history and qualifications (CUSTOMER).

## 2. Customer History Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives log customer interactions and sales transactions (custhist_df).
- Customer service representatives record customer complaints or issues (CUST_HIST).

## 3. Account Management

Performed By: Finance Team, IT Department

Activity:
- Finance team updates customer account information such as billing address and payment details (CUSTOMER).
- IT department manages customer accounts' security and access permissions (cust_df).

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by entering product details and shipping information (custhist_df).
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to customers (not specified in provided data).

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams order new inventory based on sales data and stock levels (not specified in provided data).
- Warehouse staff receive, store, and track inventory levels (not specified in provided data).

## 6. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Team

Activity:
- Finance team generates invoices for customers based on their order history and account information (custhist_df, CUSTOMER).

## 7. Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Team, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Finance team processes customer payments and updates account balances (CUSTOMER).
- Customer service representatives handle payment disputes or issues (not specified in provided data).
