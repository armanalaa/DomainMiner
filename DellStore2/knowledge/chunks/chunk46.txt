 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their personal details, contact information, and account credentials. (CUSTOMER table)
- Customer service agents update customer records with changes to contact information or account status. (CUSTOMER table)
- Sales representatives assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer during registration. (CUSTOMER table)

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service agents log interactions with customers, including calls, emails, and support tickets. (CUST_HIST table)
- Data analysts access customer history data to analyze trends, identify issues, and improve customer service. (CUST_HIST table)

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the product catalog by recording their product code, name, category, and list price. (Not specified in provided schema)
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels for each item in the catalog as products are received or sold. (Not specified in provided schema)

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by confirming availability, calculating shipping costs, and providing order details. (Not specified in provided schema)
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to customers. (Not specified in provided schema)
- Customer service agents handle any issues that arise during the ordering or shipping process. (Not specified in provided schema)

## 5. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Team, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Finance team generates invoices for customers based on their order history. (Not specified in provided schema)
- Customer service agents send invoices to customers via email or mail. (Not specified in provided schema)
- Customers pay their invoices through various payment methods, which are recorded by the finance team. (Not specified in provided schema)
