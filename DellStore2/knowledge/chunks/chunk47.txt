 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information (cust_df).
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer for secure access (cust_df).

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives update the customer history (CUST_HIST) with details of purchases made by customers.
- Customer service agents access the customer history to resolve queries and provide assistance (CUST_HIST).

## 3. Customer Profile Management

Performed By: Customers, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customers update their personal and contact information in their profiles (CUSTOMER).
- HR managers verify and approve changes to customer profiles as needed (CUSTOMER).

## 4. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product details such as code, name, category, and list price (cust_df).
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels for each item in the catalog (not specified table).

## 5. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by selecting items from the catalog and specifying quantities (cust_df).
- Warehouse staff prepare and ship ordered items to customers (not specified table).

## 6. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Finance generates invoices based on order details and sends them to customers (not specified table).
- Customer service agents assist customers with payment inquiries and process payments (not specified table).

## 7. Data Documentation and Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, DBAs

Activity:
- Data analysts document the data model, explain tables and columns, and visualize relationships between them (Dataedo).
- DBAs analyze data quality in the Snowflake data warehouse to identify areas for improvement (Dataedo).
