- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to replenish depleted items.

## 7. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers make payments through various methods (e.g., credit card, bank transfer) and provide payment details to the finance department.

## 8. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries, complaints, and returns.
- They update the customer's profile and order history as needed.
