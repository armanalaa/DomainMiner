 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customer details such as name, contact information, and address.
- Customer service agents update customer records with changes in contact information or account status.
- Sales representatives assign unique customer IDs to each new customer.
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries and complaints, recording resolution details.

## 2. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the product catalog with details like product code, name, category, and list price.
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when products are received or shipped out.
- Product managers maintain accurate product descriptions and specifications.
- Warehouse staff track product locations within the warehouse.

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives or customer service agents process orders by selecting items from the catalog and specifying quantities.
- Customer service agents confirm order details with customers before processing.
- Warehouse staff pick and pack ordered items for shipment.
- Customer service agents notify customers when their orders are ready to be shipped.

## 4. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Team, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Finance team calculates the total cost of each order based on item prices and quantities.
- Customer service agents generate invoices for customers with order details and payment instructions.
- Customers pay their invoices through various methods, such as credit card or bank transfer.
- Finance team records payments and updates customer accounts accordingly.

## 5. Shipping and Delivery

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Agents

Activity:
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipment by packaging items securely and attaching shipping labels.
- Shipping agents transport orders to their destinations using courier services or other delivery methods.
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when shipped items are deducted from stock.
- Customer service agents notify customers when their orders have been shipped and provide tracking information.

## 6. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, Technical Support Staff

Activity:
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries related to products, orders, or account issues.
- Technical support staff troubleshoot technical problems customers may encounter with their purchases.
- Customer service agents escalate complex issues to technical support staff when necessary.
- Technical support staff provide solutions and guidance to help customers resolve their issues.

## 7. Data Management

Performed By: IT Administrators, Data Analysts

Activity:
- IT administrators ensure the database is secure, backed up regularly, and accessible only to authorized personnel.
- Data analysts extract data from the database for reporting, analysis, or decision-making purposes.
- IT administrators monitor database performance and address any issues that may arise.
- Data analysts maintain accurate and up-to-date documentation of the database structure and data lineage.
