 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and creating a customer account (cust_df).
- IT support assigns unique usernames and passwords to each new customer.

## 2. Customer Profile Management

Performed By: Customers, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customers update their personal information and preferences through the self-service portal (cust_df).
- Customer service representatives modify customer details as needed based on customer requests or changes in account status.

## 3. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products and providing shipping information (cust_df, CUSTOMER).
- Sales representatives review and confirm orders before they are processed for fulfillment.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and update them when products are received or shipped (CUSTOMER).
- Purchasing teams order new stock based on sales data and inventory levels.

## 5. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices for completed orders (cust_df, CUSTOMER).
- Finance records the invoice details and sends it to the customer.

## 6. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance

Activity:
- Customers make payments through various methods, which are recorded by finance (CUSTOMER).
- Finance updates the payment status for each order in the system.

## 7. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and resolve issues related to orders, products, or account information (cust_df, CUSTOMER).

## 8. Data Management

Performed By: IT Support, Data Analysts

Activity:
- IT support maintains the database structure and ensures data integrity (cust_df, CUSTOMER).
- Data analysts extract and analyze data for reporting and business intelligence purposes (cust_df, CUSTOMER, custhist_df, CUST_HIST).
