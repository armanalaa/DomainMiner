 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by entering their details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, contact information) and creating a user account.
- IT support assigns the customer a unique ID (10001) and sets up password protection for the account.

## 2. Customer Profile Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives update customer profiles with changes in contact information or preferences.
- HR managers maintain employee records related to each customer (name, address, etc.) within the customer profile.

## 3. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products and providing shipping details from their registered accounts.
- Sales representatives confirm order details and process them for further processing.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track product availability and update inventory levels accordingly.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to replenish depleted stock.

## 5. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Department

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack ordered items for shipment.
- Shipping department labels packages with appropriate shipping information and sends them to the customer's address.

## 6. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Finance Department

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices based on order details and send them to customers.
- Finance department records invoice details (invoices numbers, amounts, due dates) in the system.

## 7. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers make payments towards their outstanding invoices using various payment methods.
- Finance department updates customer accounts with payment status and records transaction details.

## 8. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries, complaints, and returns.
- They update customer profiles and order histories as necessary based on the interaction.
