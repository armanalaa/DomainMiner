 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, email addresses, phone numbers).
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each registered customer.
- Customer service representatives update customer information as needed.

## 2. Account Maintenance

Performed By: Customers, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customers manage their accounts by changing their personal details (addresses, emails, phone numbers).
- Customer service representatives verify and process changes to customer accounts.

## 3. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products, quantities, and shipping addresses.
- Sales representatives confirm order details and process them for fulfillment.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels of available products.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to replenish depleted stock.

## 5. Order Fulfillment and Shipping

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Delivery Services

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered products to customers.
- Delivery services transport packages to the specified shipping addresses.

## 6. Invoice Processing

Performed By: Finance Teams, Customers

Activity:
- Finance teams generate invoices based on order details and send them to customers.
- Customers review invoices and make payments as needed.

## 7. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints, providing assistance and resolving issues.
