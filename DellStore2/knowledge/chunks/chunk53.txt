 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and creating a customer account (cust_df).
- IT support assigns unique usernames and passwords to the newly registered customers.

## 2. Customer Profile Management

Performed By: Customers, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customers update their personal information and preferences within their account (CUSTOMER).
- Customer service representatives modify customer details as requested by the customer or when necessary (CUSTOMER).

## 3. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products and providing shipping information (cust_df).
- Sales representatives process orders and confirm order details with customers.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update the inventory levels when new products are received or existing ones are shipped out (cust_df).
- Purchasing teams order additional stock based on current inventory levels and sales forecasts.

## 5. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices for completed orders and send them to customers (cust_df).
- Finance records the invoice details and sends payment reminders to overdue customers.

## 6. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance

Activity:
- Customers make payments through various methods and update their account status accordingly (CUSTOMER).
- Finance processes incoming payments and updates the customer's account balance.

## 7. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries, complaints, and returns (cust_df).
- They escalate issues to relevant teams when necessary.

## 8. Reporting and Analytics

Performed By: Data Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Data analysts generate reports based on sales data, inventory levels, and customer behavior (cust_df).
- Management uses these reports for decision making and strategic planning.
