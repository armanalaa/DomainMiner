 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customer accounts by recording contact details and preferences (cust_df).
- Customer service agents update customer information as needed (cust_df).
- Sales representatives assign customers to appropriate sales territories based on location (cust_df).

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service agents log interactions with customers and record issue resolutions (CUST_HIST).
- Sales representatives document sales transactions for each customer (custhist_df).

## 3. Customer Profile Maintenance

Performed By: HR Managers, IT Administrators

Activity:
- HR managers update employee information related to customers (cust_df).
- IT administrators manage user accounts and permissions for customer-related applications (cust_df).

## 4. Data Governance and Lineage Documentation

Performed By: Data Analysts, Data Engineers

Activity:
- Data analysts document data lineage and quality for customer-related data sources (050_docs__databricks.pdf, 051_docs__microsoft-fabric.pdf, 052_sources__microsoft-azure.pdf).
- Data engineers ensure that customer data is properly cataloged and documented in Azure (052_sources__microsoft-azure.pdf).

## 5. Customer Support Ticketing System Management

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, IT Support Technicians

Activity:
- Customer service agents create support tickets for customer issues (not directly related to DellStore2).
- IT support technicians resolve support tickets and update their status in the system.

## 6. Data Integration and Transformation

Performed By: Data Engineers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Data engineers integrate customer data from various sources into the DellStore2 database (051_docs__microsoft-fabric.pdf, 052_sources__microsoft-azure.pdf).
- Data analysts transform and clean customer data for analysis and reporting purposes.

## 7. Data Analysis and Reporting

Performed By: Business Analysts, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Business analysts generate reports on customer behavior and preferences (Power BI, Azure Synapse Analytics).
- Sales managers use these reports to identify sales opportunities and optimize strategies.
