 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representative, IT Administrator

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and creating a user account (cust_df).
- IT administrators assign unique identifiers (SRIMVH) to each customer for internal tracking.

## 2. Customer Profile Management

Performed By: Customer, IT Administrator

Activity:
- Customers update their personal information (SLZCISTEWL@dell.com, password) through the user portal.
- IT administrators verify and maintain accurate customer data in the system (cust_df).

## 3. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representative, Customer Service Agent

Activity:
- Sales representatives log interactions with customers (CUST_HIST) to provide context for future sales opportunities.
- Customer service agents access customer history (CUST_HIST) to resolve issues and improve customer satisfaction.

## 4. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Manager, IT Administrator

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the catalog by recording product details (product code, name, category, list price).
- IT administrators ensure the accuracy and completeness of product data in the system (cust_df).

## 5. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representative, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by confirming availability and scheduling delivery (custhist_df).
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to customers.

## 6. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- Finance department generates invoices based on order details (custhist_df) and sends them to customers.

## 7. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customer, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers make payments through various methods (80000, UK, 2, SLZCISTEWL@dell.com).
- Finance department records and verifies customer payments in the system (cust_df).

## 8. Support Ticketing System Management

Performed By: Customer Service Agent, IT Administrator

Activity:
- Customer service agents create support tickets for customers with issues or questions (custhist_df).
- IT administrators resolve technical issues and update the status of support tickets in the system (custhist_df).
