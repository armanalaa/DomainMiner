 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Sales Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, 1467048332 Dell Way, Unnamed: 4, DXPGGAE, Unnamed: 6, 00000, UK, 2, SLZCISTEWL@dell.com, 1467048332, 5, 1281093623023480, 2012/09, user10001, password).
- IT support assigns a unique customer ID (10001) and sets up the customer's account in the system.
- IT support creates an initial profile for the customer, including their preferences and access permissions.

## 2. Customer Account Maintenance

Performed By: Customers, IT Support

Activity:
- Customers update their personal information (name, email, address) as needed through the self-service portal.
- IT support handles more complex changes to customer accounts, such as password resets or access level adjustments.

## 3. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products (ProductID), specifying quantities, and providing shipping details.
- Sales representatives review the order for accuracy before confirming it in the system.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels (SLZCISTEWL) and manage stock movements (1287944454 Dell Way).
- Purchasing teams order new products when inventory reaches a predefined threshold.

## 5. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack orders for shipment (XCXNWIS).
- Shipping teams prepare shipping labels and arrange for delivery of the order.

## 6. Invoice Processing

Performed By: Finance, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices based on confirmed orders (SLZCISTEWL@dell.com).
- Finance records the invoice details (80000, F) and sends it to the customer.

## 7. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance

Activity:
- Customers make payments through various methods (44, 80000).
- Finance records the payment details (UK, 2) and updates the customer's account accordingly.

## 8. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints, providing assistance with product issues or account management.

## 9. Data Analysis

Performed By: Business Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Business analysts extract data from the system (CUST_HIST, custhist_df) for analysis and reporting purposes.
- Management reviews the reports to identify trends, make decisions, and set strategic goals.
