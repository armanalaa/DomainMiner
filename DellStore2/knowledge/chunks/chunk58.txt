 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customer accounts by recording their details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, contact information, address, etc.).
- Customer service agents update existing customer records with any changes in personal or contact information.
- Sales representatives assign unique usernames and passwords to customers for account access.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by entering product details (product code, name, category, list price) and customer information.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and prepare items for shipment based on order details.
- Warehouse staff update order status to "Shipped" once the order is ready for delivery.

## 3. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Team

Activity:
- Finance team generates invoices for each completed order, including total amount due and payment terms.
- Finance team sends invoices to customers via email or mail.

## 4. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Team

Activity:
- Customers make payments towards their outstanding invoices using various methods (credit card, bank transfer, etc.).
- Finance team records payment details and updates the customer's account accordingly.

## 5. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries related to orders, products, or billing issues.
- Customer service agents escalate complex issues to relevant departments for resolution.

## 6. Data Management

Performed By: IT Administrators

Activity:
- IT administrators ensure the security and integrity of customer data by implementing access controls and backup procedures.
- IT administrators monitor system performance and resolve any technical issues that may arise.

## 7. Compliance and Reporting

Performed By: Legal Team, Finance Team

Activity:
- Legal team ensures compliance with data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA) by reviewing and updating data handling practices.
- Finance team generates reports on sales, inventory, and financial performance for internal analysis and external reporting.
