 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details and contact information (cust_df).
- HR managers update customer account details when employees leave or change roles (cust_df).

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives log customer interactions, including sales and support calls (CUST_HIST).
- Customer service representatives update the customer history record with each new interaction (custhist_df).

## 3. Account Maintenance

Performed By: Customers, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customers modify their account information through the self-service portal (cust_df).
- Customer service representatives verify and confirm changes made by customers (cust_df).

## 4. Password Management

Performed By: Customers, IT Support

Activity:
- Customers reset their passwords using the self-service portal (customer).
- IT support team updates the password record when necessary (customer).

## 5. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- The data governance team ensures that all customer data is stored securely and complies with relevant regulations (all tables).

## 6. Data Documentation

Performed By: Business Analysts, Data Governance Team

Activity:
- Business analysts document the structure and purpose of each customer-related table (all tables).
- The data governance team reviews and approves the documentation (Data Dictionary & Data Catalog).
