 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, Customers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and preferences.
- HR managers update customer information when changes occur, such as address or employment status.
- Customers manage their own accounts by modifying personal data through the self-service portal.

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives log customer interactions and purchase history in the system.
- Customer service agents access and review a customer's history to address queries or issues.

## 3. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: IT Administrators, Data Owners, Auditors

Activity:
- IT administrators ensure data is stored securely and complies with relevant regulations.
- Data owners assign clear ownership to datasets and track issues directly through built-in workflows.
- Auditors review the catalog to identify where personal or regulated data is stored and how it moves across systems.

## 4. Data Discovery and Understanding

Performed By: Business Analysts, Data Scientists

Activity:
- Business analysts find trusted customer data quickly using a shared business glossary and catalog.
- Data scientists understand how to use the data correctly for analysis and modeling purposes.

## 5. Data Product Definition

Performed By: Data Product Managers, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Data product managers define data products and document contracts and schemas.
- IT administrators expose these curated datasets in the portal for safe reuse by teams.

## 6. Self-Service Analytics

Performed By: Business Users

Activity:
- Business users discover and understand data on their own, while keeping sensitive data secure.

## 7. Impact Analysis

Performed By: Data Engineers, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Before making changes to the database schema, data engineers assess the impact on reports, dashboards, and ETL jobs using full impact analysis.
