 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, Customers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and user credentials. (CUSTOMER)
- HR managers assign unique identifiers to each customer upon successful registration. (CUST_DF)
- Customers update their personal information as needed through the customer portal. (CUSTOMER)

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Support Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives log interactions with customers, including sales transactions and follow-ups. (CUST_HIST)
- Customer support agents document customer inquiries and resolutions for future reference. (CUST_HIST)

## 3. Account Management

Performed By: Finance Team

Activity:
- The finance team manages customer accounts, including billing and payment details. (CUSTOMER)

## 4. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- The data governance team ensures regulatory compliance by documenting data sources, lineage, and quality. (056_solutions.pdf p.6, 057_solutions__ai-governance.pdf p.1)

## 5. AI Model Governance

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- The data governance team tracks the training and scoring data of AI models for compliance purposes. (057_solutions__ai-governance.pdf p.1)

## 6. Data Catalog Management

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- The data governance team maintains a centralized catalog of data assets for easier discovery and knowledge sharing. (056_solutions.pdf p.8)

## 7. Support and Maintenance

Performed By: IT Support Team

Activity:
- The IT support team addresses technical issues related to the DellStore2 database and associated systems. (Not specified in source materials)
