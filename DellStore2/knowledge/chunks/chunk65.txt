 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, IT Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and creating a user account (cust_df).
- HR managers assign roles and permissions to the newly registered users (cust_df).
- IT support sets up email accounts for new customers (cust_df).

## 2. Customer Data Maintenance

Performed By: Customers, IT Support

Activity:
- Customers update their personal information through their account dashboard (cust_df).
- IT support modifies customer data as needed based on requests or system updates (cust_df).

## 3. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Support

Activity:
- Sales representatives log interactions with customers and their purchase history (custhist_df).
- Customer support agents update the customer history when resolving issues or complaints (custhist_df).

## 4. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: IT Compliance Team, Data Analysts

Activity:
- IT compliance team ensures data is collected, stored, and processed in accordance with regulations (cust_df, custhist_df).
- Data analysts monitor and document data lineage for AI models that use customer data (cust_df, custhist_df).

## 5. Sensitive Data Classification

Performed By: Data Analysts, IT Compliance Team

Activity:
- Data analysts tag personal or sensitive information in the customer data (cust_df, custhist_df).
- IT compliance team enforces masking rules and retention windows for sensitive customer data (cust_df, custhist_df).

## 6. AI Model Development and Training

Performed By: Data Scientists, Machine Learning Engineers

Activity:
- Data scientists collect and preprocess customer data for use in machine learning models.
- Machine learning engineers train AI models using the prepared customer data to predict customer behavior or preferences.

## 7. Model Evaluation and Deployment

Performed By: Data Scientists, IT Operations

Activity:
- Data scientists evaluate the performance of AI models and iterate as needed.
- IT operations deploys the approved AI models into production for use in customer-facing applications or internal decision making.
