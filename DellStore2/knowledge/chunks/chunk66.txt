 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details and contact information (cust_df).
- HR managers update customer records with employment details when a customer becomes an employee (CUSTOMER).

## 2. Customer History Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives log customer interactions and transactions (custhist_df).
- Customer service representatives update the customer history with new records based on customer inquiries or complaints (CUST_HIST).

## 3. Account Management

Performed By: Finance Team, IT Department

Activity:
- The finance team manages customer accounts by recording financial transactions and account details (cust_df, CUSTOMER).
- The IT department ensures secure access to customer data through password management (cust_df, CUSTOMER).

## 4. AI Governance

Performed By: Data Governance Team, Compliance Officers
