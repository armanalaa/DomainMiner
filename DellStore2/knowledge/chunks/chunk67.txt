 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, contact information, address) and setting up accounts.
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer for account access.
- IT administrators maintain the customer database (cust_df) and ensure data integrity.

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives update the customer history (CUST_HIST) with each transaction, including product details, quantities, and timestamps.
- Customer service agents access the customer history to resolve queries and provide support.

## 3. Customer Profile Management

Performed By: HR Managers, IT Administrators

Activity:
- HR managers update customer profiles (CUSTOMER) with changes in personal information or account settings.
- IT administrators manage and secure customer data, ensuring compliance with privacy regulations.

## 4. Order Placement and Processing

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Customers place orders through the online store, specifying products, quantities, and shipping details.
- Sales representatives review and confirm orders before they are processed.
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items, updating order statuses accordingly.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels (not specified in the provided schema) as they receive and dispatch products.
- Purchasing teams monitor inventory levels and place orders for restocking when necessary.

## 6. Financial Transactions

Performed By: Finance Department, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives record sales transactions (not specified in the provided schema) in the system.
- The finance department calculates and records revenues, expenses, and profits based on these transactions.

## 7. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: IT Administrators, Legal Department

Activity:
- IT administrators ensure data quality, security, and compliance with regulations (e.g., PII protection).
- The legal department reviews and updates policies related to data governance and privacy.
