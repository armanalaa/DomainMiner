 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their contact information and account details. (CUSTOMER table)
- Sales managers update customer records with purchase history and preferences for personalized sales strategies. (CUST_HIST, CUSTOMER tables)

## 2. Account Maintenance

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Customer service representatives reset customer passwords upon request. (CUSTOMER table)
- IT support staff handle account security issues and ensure data privacy compliance.

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives confirm orders with customers and update order status in the system. (CUST_HIST table)
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to customers.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Managers, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse managers track inventory levels and notify purchasing teams when restocking is needed.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders for necessary items to suppliers.

## 5. Financial Transactions

Performed By: Finance Department, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Finance department records sales transactions and calculates revenue. (CUST_HIST table)
- Accounting staff prepares financial reports and ensures accurate bookkeeping.

## 6. Data Governance

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- Data governance team maintains data quality, ensuring consistency and accuracy of customer data in the system. (CUSTOMER, CUST_HIST tables)

## 7. Compliance Monitoring

Performed By: Legal Department, IT Security Team

Activity:
- Legal department reviews and updates privacy policies to ensure compliance with regulations.
- IT security team implements measures to protect customer data from unauthorized access or breaches.
