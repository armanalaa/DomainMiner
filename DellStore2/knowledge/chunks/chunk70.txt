 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details and contact information (cust_df).
- HR managers onboard new employees by entering their employment details into the system (cust_df).

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives update customer history records with sales transactions (custhist_df).
- Customer service representatives access and review customer history records to address inquiries or resolve issues (custhist_df).

## 3. Account Management

Performed By: Finance Department, IT Department

Activity:
- The finance department manages user accounts by assigning roles and permissions based on the user's position within the company (customer table).
- The IT department maintains system security by updating passwords for user accounts (customer table).

## 4. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: Data Governance Team, Audit Department

Activity:
- The data governance team classifies sensitive data according to its regulatory requirements (not specific to DellStore2 database).
- The audit department reviews the classified data to ensure compliance with relevant regulations (not specific to DellStore2 database).

## 5. Data Lineage and Audit Trails

Performed By: Data Governance Team, IT Department

Activity:
- The data governance team tracks the origin and movement of data within the system (not specific to DellStore2 database).
- The IT department maintains audit trails for metadata changes in the system (not specific to DellStore2 database).

## 6. Data Reporting and Analysis

Performed By: Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Analysts generate reports based on data from various tables within the DellStore2 database.
- Management reviews these reports for insights into sales, customer behavior, and other key performance indicators.
