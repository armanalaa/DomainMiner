 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, Customers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and preferences.
- HR managers update customer information when changes occur, such as address or employment status.
- Customers manage their accounts by modifying personal data, passwords, and billing information.

## 2. Customer Support Ticketing

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives log customer support tickets related to product issues or inquiries.
- They assign tickets to appropriate teams for resolution and track their progress until completion.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the catalog by recording product code, name, category, and list price.
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when stock changes occur.

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by selecting items from the catalog and specifying quantities.
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipping by packing products and updating inventory levels accordingly.

## 5. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Team, Customers

Activity:
- Finance team generates invoices based on order details and sends them to customers.
- Customers pay their invoices through various payment methods, which are recorded in the system.

## 6. Data Governance and Quality Assurance

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- Data governance team assigns owners to every table and column for accountability purposes.
- They track data quality issues, discuss solutions within the community, and ensure timely resolution.

## 7. Reporting and Analytics

Performed By: Business Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Business analysts create reports based on data from various tables to analyze sales trends, customer behavior, and inventory levels.
- Management uses these reports for decision-making purposes, such as setting pricing strategies or adjusting marketing efforts.
