 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and preferences.
- Customer service agents update customer information as needed, such as address or account status.
- Sales representatives assign customer accounts to specific sales teams for targeted marketing efforts.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Finance Teams

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by entering product details and shipping information.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and prepare orders for shipment.
- Finance teams record order-related transactions, such as payments and refunds.

## 3. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when products are received or shipped.
- Purchasing teams place orders for new stock based on current inventory levels and sales forecasts.

## 4. Financial Management

Performed By: Finance Teams, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Finance teams record financial transactions related to orders, payments, and refunds.
- Accounting staff prepare financial reports and statements for internal use and external stakeholders.

## 5. Data Governance

Performed By: IT Administrators, Data Governance Team

Activity:
- IT administrators manage the database schema, including creating, modifying, or deleting tables and columns.
- The data governance team ensures data quality, consistency, and security across all databases.

## 6. Impact Analysis

Performed By: Data Governance Team, Change Management Teams

Activity:
- The data governance team uses impact analysis tools to identify downstream dependencies of any table or column changes.
- Change management teams review the impact assessment and plan updates accordingly.

## 7. Compliance and Reporting

Performed By: Legal Team, Compliance Officers, Regulatory Bodies

Activity:
- The legal team ensures that all data collection, storage, and usage comply with relevant regulations and laws.
- Compliance officers monitor and report on the company's adherence to these regulations.
- Regulatory bodies may request access to specific data for audits or investigations.
