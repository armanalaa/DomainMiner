 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information.
- Customer service agents update customer profiles with changes in contact information or account status.
- Sales representatives assign customer accounts to specific sales teams for follow-up and support.

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Sales representatives log interactions with customers, including calls, emails, and meetings.
- Customer service agents document customer complaints, resolutions, and follow-ups.
- Data analysts analyze customer history data to identify trends, patterns, and opportunities for upselling or cross-selling.

## 3. Account Management

Performed By: Account Managers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Account managers monitor customer accounts for payment status, credit limits, and account health.
- Finance department updates account information with new payments, adjustments, or changes in account status.

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Shipping Department

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by confirming availability and pricing.
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and prepare orders for shipping.
- Shipping department ships orders to customers and updates order status in the system.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Inventory Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Inventory managers monitor stock levels and reorder inventory when necessary.
- Warehouse staff receive and store incoming shipments, update inventory levels accordingly.

## 6. Billing and Invoicing

Performed By: Finance Department, Account Managers

Activity:
- Finance department generates invoices based on customer orders and account information.
- Account managers review and send invoices to customers, follow up on any outstanding payments.

## 7. Technical Support

Performed By: Technical Support Agents

Activity:
- Technical support agents assist customers with product-related issues or questions.
- They document customer inquiries, troubleshoot problems, and provide solutions or escalate issues as needed.
