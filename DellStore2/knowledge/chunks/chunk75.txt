 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details and contact information (cust_df).
- HR managers update customer employment statuses in the employee database (cust_df) when a customer becomes an employee.

## 2. Customer Data Maintenance

Performed By: Data Analysts, IT Support

Activity:
- Data analysts maintain and update customer data in the centralized customer database (CUSTOMER).
- IT support staff resolve any technical issues related to the customer database (CUSTOMER).

## 3. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives record customer purchase history in the customer history table (cust_hist).
- Customer service representatives access and review a customer's purchase history (cust_hist) to address queries or issues.

## 4. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Support

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints, providing solutions or escalating as necessary.
- IT support staff assist with technical issues related to the customer's account (CUSTOMER).

## 5. Account Management

Performed By: Account Managers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Account managers manage customer accounts, including billing and payment arrangements.
- The finance department records and tracks payments from customers (CUSTOMER).

## 6. Customer Segmentation and Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Marketing Team

Activity:
- Data analysts segment customers based on various factors like purchasing history, demographics, or behavior.
- The marketing team uses customer segments for targeted advertising and promotions.

## 7. Customer Compliance Management

Performed By: Legal Department, IT Support

Activity:
- The legal department ensures that customer data is handled in compliance with regulations like GDPR and CCPA.
- IT support staff implement necessary measures to maintain compliance with data protection regulations (CUSTOMER).
