 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives respond to customer inquiries and update customer details (cust_df).
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their personal information (cust_df).

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives log interactions with customers and update customer history records (CUST_HIST).
- Data analysts analyze customer history data to identify trends and patterns (CUST_HIST).

## 3. Customer Data Maintenance

Performed By: IT Administrators, Data Analysts

Activity:
- IT administrators manage the DellStore2 database schema and ensure data integrity (cust_df, CUST_HIST).
- Data analysts maintain and clean customer data to improve data quality (cust_df, CUST_HIST).

## 4. Product Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Product managers define product specifications and update product information (product table not specified in the provided schema).
- Inventory managers track inventory levels and update product availability (not specified in the provided schema).

## 5. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by recording order details (not specified in the provided schema).
- Warehouse staff pick and pack ordered items for shipment.

## 6. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Accounting Team

Activity:
- Accounting team generates invoices based on customer orders and updates invoice records (not specified in the provided schema).

## 7. Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Team, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Finance team processes customer payments and updates payment records (not specified in the provided schema).
- Customer service representatives handle payment-related inquiries from customers.
