 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, contact information, address, etc.).
- Customer service agents update customer records with any changes in personal or contact details.
- Customer service agents handle customer queries and complaints, updating the interaction history accordingly.

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service agents log interactions with customers, including date, issue, resolution, and agent details (CUST_HIST).
- Data analysts analyze customer history data to identify trends, patterns, and areas for improvement.

## 3. Data Product Creation

Performed By: Data Analysts, Data Stewards

Activity:
- Data analysts create new data products by curating and organizing datasets (cust_df).
- Data stewards define clear ownership, documentation, and quality standards for each data product.

## 4. Data Lineage Documentation

Performed By: Data Analysts, Data Stewards

Activity:
- Data analysts document the lineage of data products, showing how they are derived from raw operational tables (custhist_df).
- Data stewards ensure that all data dependencies and downstream impact are transparent.

## 5. Data Product Tagging

Performed By: Data Stewards

Activity:
- Data stewards tag data products with badges to distinguish trusted assets (e.g., "Certified", "Production Ready").
- Data stewards document deprecation plans for outdated or irrelevant data products.

## 6. Data Discovery and Usage

Performed By: Data Consumers, Analytics Teams

Activity:
- Data consumers can easily find and understand which datasets are production-ready and which are experimental or incomplete.
- Analytics teams use certified datasets for reporting and analysis, reducing duplicate work and improving speed.
