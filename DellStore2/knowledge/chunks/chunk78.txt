 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and preferences.
- HR managers update customer information when changes occur, such as address or employment status.

## 2. Order Placement and Processing

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products and providing shipping details.
- Sales representatives confirm order details and process them for fulfillment.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and prepare orders for shipment.

## 3. Invoice Generation and Payment

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Finance Department

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices based on completed orders.
- The finance department records payments received from customers.

## 4. Customer Support and Inquiries

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries related to orders, products, or services.

## 5. Data Maintenance and Integrity

Performed By: IT Department

Activity:
- The IT department ensures data accuracy and consistency across all tables in the DellStore2 database.

## 6. Analytics and Reporting

Performed By: Business Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Business analysts generate reports based on data from the DellStore2 database to inform decision-making.
- Management reviews these reports to monitor business performance and identify areas for improvement.
