 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, Customers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and preferences.
- HR managers update customer information when changes occur, such as address or employment status.
- Customers manage their own accounts by modifying personal data and passwords through the portal.

## 2. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives respond to inquiries and resolve issues related to customer accounts, products, or services.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the catalog by recording product details like code, name, category, and list price.

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by confirming availability, pricing, and shipping details with customers.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels to ensure order fulfillment.

## 5. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Teams

Activity:
- Finance teams generate invoices based on orders and send them to customers.
- Finance teams record payments received from customers against corresponding invoices.

## 6. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: IT Teams, Dataedo Engineers

Activity:
- IT teams ensure data security by implementing access controls and monitoring for potential breaches.
- Dataedo engineers maintain the business glossary and documentation to improve data understanding across the organization.

## 7. Analytics and Reporting

Performed By: Business Users, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Business users explore and analyze data using tools like Power BI or Tableau, guided by the data catalog and business glossary.
- Data analysts create reports based on user requirements to support decision-making processes.
