 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information.
- Sales managers update customer accounts with any changes in personal or billing information.
- Customer service representatives track customer interactions and issues, logging them in the customer history table.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by entering product details, quantities, and shipping addresses.
- Warehouse staff confirm order availability and prepare items for shipment.
- Sales representatives update order statuses as they move through the fulfillment process.

## 3. Inventory Management

Performed By: Inventory Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Inventory managers monitor stock levels and reorder products when quantities fall below a certain threshold.
- Warehouse staff receive and store incoming shipments, updating inventory counts accordingly.
- Inventory managers track product movements and adjust inventory levels for returns or damaged goods.

## 4. Financial Management

Performed By: Accounting Staff, Finance Managers

Activity:
- Accounting staff record transactions such as sales, purchases, and payments in the appropriate tables.
- Finance managers generate financial reports based on the recorded data, analyzing trends and making recommendations for improvement.
- Accounting staff update customer account balances and payment histories.

## 5. Data Governance

Performed By: Data Governance Team

Activity:
- The data governance team ensures that all data is accurate, consistent, and secure by implementing policies and procedures for data quality and security.
- They monitor the database for any issues or anomalies, investigating and resolving them as necessary.
- The data governance team collaborates with other departments to understand their data needs and provide appropriate access and support.
