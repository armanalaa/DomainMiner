 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal details and contact information (cust_df).
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to newly registered customers (cust_df).

## 2. Account Maintenance

Performed By: Customers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers update their account information as needed (cust_df).
- The finance department reviews and approves changes to customer accounts (cust_df).

## 3. Sales Process

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders from customers by recording product details, quantities, and prices (custhist_df).
- Customer service representatives confirm order details with customers and update the order status in the system (custhist_df).

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by updating product quantities (custhist_df).
- Purchasing teams order new products when inventory levels fall below a certain threshold (custhist_df).

## 5. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Department, Data Governance and Management Team

Activity:
- The finance department generates financial reports based on sales data (custhist_df).
- The data governance and management team ensures the accuracy and completeness of the financial reports (CUST_HIST).
