 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, contact information, address) and setting up user accounts with passwords.
- HR managers update customer account information as needed, including changes to personal details or roles within the company.

## 2. Customer Support Ticketing

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives create support tickets for customers reporting issues or requesting assistance, recording relevant details (ticket ID, customer information, issue description).

## 3. Order Processing and Fulfillment

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by confirming availability, calculating prices, and updating order statuses in the system.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and pack orders for shipment based on confirmed orders.

## 4. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Accounting Team

Activity:
- Accounting team generates invoices for customers based on order details, calculating total amounts due and applying any discounts or promotions.
- Accounting team records payments received from customers, updating the payment status in the system.

## 5. Financial Reporting and Analysis

Performed By: Finance Team

Activity:
- Finance team generates financial reports for management review, summarizing sales, expenses, and profitability by product, customer, or time period.
- Finance team analyzes trends and performance metrics to identify opportunities for improvement or cost savings.
