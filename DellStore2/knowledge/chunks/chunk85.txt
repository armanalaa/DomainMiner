 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, Customers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL, contact information, address, country).
- HR managers update customer records with personal details (name, gender, date of birth) and employment information (user ID, password, role, email).
- Customers manage their accounts by changing their passwords.

## 2. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries related to products, services, or account issues.
- They update the customer's support history (1467048332 Dell Way, SLZCISTEWL@dell.com) with details of each interaction.

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by entering product details (product code, name, category, list price) and shipping information.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels to ensure items are available for shipment.

## 4. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Accounting Team

Activity:
- The accounting team generates invoices based on order details (product, quantity, price) and customer information.
- They record payments received from customers (80000, UK, 1.1, FQIVBKWIZC@dell.com).

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when items are received or shipped (RNPOJG, FQIVBKWIZC, 1287944454 Dell Way).

## 6. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: IT Administrators, Compliance Officers

Activity:
- IT administrators maintain the data catalog and ensure end-to-end data lineage across clinical and administrative systems.
- Compliance officers identify protected health information (PHI) and tag critical data assets for consistent reporting and HIPAA compliance.
