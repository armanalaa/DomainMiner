 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their personal and contact details (cust_df).
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer for account access (cust_df).

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives update the customer history table (CUST_HIST) with details of interactions and transactions.
- Data analysts analyze customer history data to identify patterns and trends (custhist_df).

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product code, name, category, and list price (cust_df).
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels based on sales and restocking (not shown in provided schema).

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by confirming availability, calculating shipping costs, and scheduling delivery (not shown in provided schema).
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to customers.

## 5. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Team

Activity:
- Finance team generates invoices based on customer orders and shipping costs (not shown in provided schema).

## 6. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Finance Team

Activity:
- Customer service representatives assist customers with payment processing by answering questions and resolving issues.
- Finance team records payments and updates customer accounts accordingly (cust_df).

## 7. Account Maintenance

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle account maintenance tasks such as password resets and account deactivation (cust_df).
- HR managers update customer records with changes in personal or contact information (cust_df).
