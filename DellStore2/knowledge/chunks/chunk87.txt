 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their personal details, contact information, and account credentials. (CUSTOMER table)
- Customer service agents update customer records with changes to contact information or account status. (CUSTOMER table)
- Sales representatives assign unique identifiers (SRIMVH) to each customer for internal tracking. (CUSTOMER table)

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service agents log interactions with customers and record the outcomes of these interactions. (CUST_HIST table)
- Data analysts access customer history data to analyze trends and patterns in customer behavior. (CUST_HIST table)

## 3. Account Maintenance

Performed By: Customers, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Customers update their account information, such as passwords or billing addresses. (CUSTOMER table)
- Customer service agents verify and process customer-initiated changes to account information. (CUSTOMER table)

## 4. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Department, Customers

Activity:
- The finance department generates invoices for customers based on their purchase history. (Not directly related to the provided database schema)
- Customers pay their invoices through various payment methods, which are then recorded in the system. (Not directly related to the provided database schema)

## 5. Data Governance and Compliance

Performed By: IT Department, Data Analysts, Compliance Officers

Activity:
- The IT department ensures that sensitive data is properly stored, protected, and managed according to company policies and regulations. (Not directly related to the provided database schema)
- Data analysts use standardized definitions for key terms and metrics across departments to improve collaboration and reduce confusion. (Not directly related to the provided database schema)
- Compliance officers review data documentation and audit trails to ensure adherence to regulatory requirements. (Not directly related to the provided database schema)
