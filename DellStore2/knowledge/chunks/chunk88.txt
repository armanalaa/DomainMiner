 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information. (CUSTOMER)
- HR managers assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer for account access. (CUSTOMER)
- HR managers update customer records with personal details and employment status changes. (CUSTOMER)

## 2. Customer Support Ticketing System

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives create support tickets for customers to track their issues or inquiries. (cust_df)
- Customer service representatives update ticket statuses as they resolve customer issues. (cust_df)
- Customer service representatives escalate unresolved tickets to technical support teams when necessary. (cust_df)

## 3. Order Processing and Fulfillment

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by confirming availability, pricing, and shipping details. (cust_df)
- Warehouse staff pick and pack ordered items for shipment to the customer's specified address. (unspecified table)
- Warehouse staff update order statuses as items are picked, packed, and shipped. (unspecified table)

## 4. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Finance Department

Activity:
- Sales representatives generate invoices for customers based on their orders. (unspecified table)
- Finance department records customer payments against outstanding invoices. (unspecified table)
- Finance department updates customer accounts with payment history and balances. (unspecified table)

## 5. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Sales representatives record customer interactions, purchases, and support tickets in the customer history database. (CUST_HIST)
- Data analysts analyze customer histories to identify trends, preferences, and opportunities for upselling or cross-selling. (CUST_HIST)
- Data analysts generate reports on customer behavior and performance for sales and marketing purposes. (unspecified table)
