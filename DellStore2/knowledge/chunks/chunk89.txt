 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information.
- Customer service agents update customer records with any changes to personal or billing information.
- Sales representatives assign customer accounts to specific sales teams for targeted marketing efforts.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Finance Team

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by entering product details and shipping instructions.
- Warehouse staff retrieve ordered items from inventory and prepare them for shipment.
- Finance team records the financial transaction associated with each order.

## 3. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when items are received or shipped.
- Purchasing teams place orders for new stock based on current inventory levels and sales forecasts.

## 4. Financial Management

Performed By: Finance Team, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Finance team records financial transactions related to orders, payments, and returns.
- Accounting staff generate monthly reports detailing revenue, expenses, and profitability.

## 5. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, Technical Support Specialists

Activity:
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries and complaints regarding products or services.
- Technical support specialists troubleshoot issues with products and provide solutions to customers.

## 6. Compliance and Data Governance

Performed By: Compliance Officers, IT Staff

Activity:
- Compliance officers ensure that all data is collected, stored, and processed in accordance with relevant regulations.
- IT staff implement security measures to protect sensitive customer and financial data.

## 7. Data Analysis and Reporting

Performed By: Business Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Business analysts analyze sales trends, customer behavior, and inventory performance.
- Management uses these insights to make informed decisions about marketing strategies, product offerings, and operational efficiency.
