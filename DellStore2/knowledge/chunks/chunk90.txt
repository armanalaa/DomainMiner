 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact information and account details.
- Customer service agents update customer records with changes to personal or billing data.
- Sales representatives assign customer accounts to specific sales teams based on geographical location or industry.

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service agents log interactions with customers and record the outcomes of those interactions.
- Data analysts analyze customer history data to identify trends and patterns in customer behavior.
- Customer service agents update the customer history table with new records for each interaction.

## 3. Pricing Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Pricing Analysts

Activity:
- Sales representatives set prices for products based on market conditions and customer needs.
- Pricing analysts review and adjust product prices to ensure competitiveness and profitability.
- Sales representatives update the pricing information in the customer records when changes occur.

## 4. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers add new products to the catalog by entering product details such as name, category, and list price.
- Data entry clerks update existing product records with changes to product information or specifications.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by updating the quantity of each product in stock.
- Purchasing teams order new products from suppliers when inventory levels fall below a certain threshold.
- Warehouse staff update the inventory table with the receipt or shipment of products.

## 6. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by confirming availability and calculating shipping costs.
- Customer service agents update order statuses and notify customers when their orders are ready to ship.
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship products to customers according to the order details.

## 7. Billing and Invoicing

Performed By: Finance Teams, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Finance teams generate invoices based on customer orders and update the billing information in customer records.
- Customer service agents send invoices to customers and follow up on any outstanding payments.
- Finance teams record payments received from customers and update the account balances accordingly.
