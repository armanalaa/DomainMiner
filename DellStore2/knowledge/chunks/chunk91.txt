 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details (SRIMVH, SLZCISTEWL@dell.com, DXPGGAE, etc.) and address (1467048332 Dell Way).
- HR managers onboard new employees by entering their personal information (user10001, password, 44, M) into the customer database.
- Sales representatives update customer details as needed.

## 2. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives respond to customer inquiries and address issues related to products or services.
- They track customer interactions (11001, 7259) and update the customer database accordingly.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the product catalog by recording their product code, name, category, and list price.
- They update product information as needed.

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by confirming availability and pricing.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels (1287944454 Dell Way) and prepare orders for shipping.

## 5. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Team

Activity:
- The finance team generates invoices based on customer orders and sends them to customers (SLZCISTEWL@dell.com).
- They record invoice details (80000, UK) in the customer database.

## 6. Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Team

Activity:
- The finance team processes customer payments and updates the customer database accordingly (49, 1.1).

## 7. Demo Booking and Sales Follow-up

Performed By: Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives book demos for potential customers and follow up on leads.
- They track demo requests and update the customer database accordingly (RNPOJG, FQIVBKWIZC@dell.com).
