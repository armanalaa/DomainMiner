 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Representatives, HR Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints by accessing the customer's contact information and interaction history.
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their personal details, contact information, and account creation date.
- HR managers process employee data updates, such as changes to addresses or job titles, within the customer database.

## 2. Account Maintenance

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customer service representatives update customer account details, including billing and shipping addresses, contact information, and passwords upon request.
- The finance department records payment transactions and updates the corresponding customer accounts accordingly.

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Shipping Department

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by confirming product availability, calculating prices, and generating invoices.
- Warehouse staff retrieve ordered items from inventory and prepare them for shipping.
- The shipping department packages the order and arranges for its delivery to the customer's specified address.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams place orders for new inventory based on sales data and stock levels.
- Warehouse staff receive and store incoming shipments, updating the inventory database accordingly.

## 5. Invoice Processing

Performed By: Finance Department, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- The finance department generates invoices for customers based on their orders and updates the customer accounts with payment due dates.
- Customer service representatives follow up on overdue payments and assist customers with any billing inquiries or disputes.

## 6. Support Services

Performed By: Technical Support Team, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- The technical support team troubleshoots customer issues related to Dell products and provides solutions or escalates them as necessary.
- Customer service representatives assist customers with non-technical inquiries, such as returns, warranties, and general product information.

## 7. Data Governance

Performed By: Data Stewards, Approvers, Editors

Activity:
- Data stewards create, curate, and manage high-quality data documentation within the DellStore2 database.
- Approvers review and approve changes to data documentation to ensure accuracy and accountability in the data governance process.
- Editors manage and enrich metadata repositories for consistent and up-to-date documentation across the data ecosystem.
