 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Portal Administrators, Subject Matter Experts, Subscription Owners and Admins

Activity:
- Portal Administrators configure and manage the Dataedo Portal to ensure smooth access for users.
- Subject Matter Experts share their knowledge and enrich the organization's data documentation.
- Subscription Owners and Admins manage the subscription, users, and licenses through the Account Portal.

## 2. Customer Registration

Performed By: Customers

Activity:
- Customers create new accounts by providing personal details such as name, email, password, and address.

## 3. Customer Authentication

Performed By: Customers

Activity:
- Customers log in to their accounts using their email and password.

## 4. Customer Profile Management

Performed By: Customers

Activity:
- Customers update their personal details, such as address or contact information, within their profiles.

## 5. Customer Support

Performed By: Integration Engineers

Activity:
- Integration Engineers provide technical support to help customers set up and maintain Dataedo in their environment.

## 6. Customer Data Tracking

Performed By: Cust_df, custhist_df, CUSTOMER tables

Activity:
- The system records customer data, including user ID, username, email, password, address, and other relevant details.

## 7. Customer History Management

Performed By: Cust_HIST table

Activity:
- The system tracks customers' historical interactions, such as purchases or account changes, for future reference.
