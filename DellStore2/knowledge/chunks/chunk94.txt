 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, contact information, and addresses.
- Data entry clerks update customer details as provided by the customers or received from other departments.
- Customer service representatives assign unique usernames and passwords to each customer for account access.

## 2. Account Maintenance

Performed By: Customers, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customers change their personal information, such as addresses or contact details, through the customer portal.
- Customer service representatives verify and update customer information when necessary.

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by recording product codes, quantities, and shipping addresses.
- Warehouse staff retrieve the ordered items from inventory and prepare them for shipment.

## 4. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Accounting Team

Activity:
- The accounting team generates invoices based on order details and customer account information.

## 5. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers make payments through various methods (credit card, bank transfer, etc.).
- The finance department records the payments and updates the corresponding customer accounts.

## 6. Shipping and Delivery

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Courier Services

Activity:
- Warehouse staff package and label orders for shipment.
- Courier services deliver the packages to the specified addresses.

## 7. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints, providing assistance and solutions as needed.
