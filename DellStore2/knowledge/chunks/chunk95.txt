 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and update customer details (cust_df).
- Sales managers review customer profiles to identify potential leads for upselling or cross-selling opportunities (CUSTOMER).
- Customer service representatives process returns and refunds based on the return reason provided by customers (cust_df).

## 2. New Customer Registration

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, IT Administrators

Activity:
- Customer service representatives assist new customers in registering accounts by providing necessary information (cust_df).
- IT administrators create user accounts and assign passwords for newly registered customers (cust_df).

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives confirm orders with customers and update order statuses in the system (custhist_df).
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and ship ordered items to customers based on their shipping addresses (custhist_df).

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Inventory Managers, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Inventory managers monitor stock levels and reorder items when necessary (custhist_df).
- Purchasing teams negotiate with suppliers to secure the best deals for product purchases (custhist_df).

## 5. Financial Management

Performed By: Finance Managers, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Finance managers record and analyze financial transactions related to sales, expenses, and inventory costs (custhist_df).
- Accounting staff prepare invoices for customers based on their order history (custhist_df).

## 6. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Technical Support Specialists

Activity:
- Customer service representatives assist customers with product-related inquiries and troubleshooting issues (custhist_df).
- Technical support specialists provide technical assistance to customers experiencing hardware or software problems (custhist_df).

## 7. Data Documentation

Performed By: Data Governance Team, IT Administrators

Activity:
- The data governance team documents the database schema and data lineage for compliance purposes (all tables).
- IT administrators ensure that all changes to the database are properly documented and tracked (all tables).
