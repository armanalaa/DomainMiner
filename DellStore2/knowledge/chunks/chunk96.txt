 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details, location, and account type. (CUSTOMER)
- Customer service agents update customer information when contacted, such as address changes or password resets. (CUSTOMER)
- Customer service agents create support tickets for any reported issues or inquiries related to the customer's account. (Not specified table)

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives log interactions with customers, including sales calls and meetings, in the customer history. (CUST_HIST)
- Customer service agents document customer complaints, resolutions, and follow-ups in the customer history. (CUST_HIST)
- Both roles update the customer history when a customer's account status changes, such as upgrades or cancellations. (CUST_HIST)

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price. (Not specified table)
- Warehouse staff update stock levels when products are received or shipped out. (Not specified table)
- Warehouse staff track inventory movements and report any discrepancies to product managers. (Not specified table)

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives or customer service agents process orders by confirming product availability, calculating shipping costs, and providing order confirmation numbers. (Not specified table)
- Warehouse staff pick and pack ordered items for shipment. (Not specified table)
- Warehouse staff update the order status when an order is shipped out. (Not specified table)

## 5. Invoice Generation and Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Department, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Finance department generates invoices based on orders and sends them to customers. (Not specified table)
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries regarding invoices, such as disputes or payment issues. (Not specified table)
- Customer service agents update the invoice status when payments are received or overdue notices are sent. (Not specified table)

## 6. Shipping and Delivery Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Providers, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Warehouse staff prepare shipments by packing ordered items and affixing shipping labels. (Not specified table)
- Shipping providers deliver orders to customers according to the provided address and shipping method. (Not specified table)
- Customer service agents update the order status when a delivery is confirmed or if there are any delivery issues. (Not specified table)

## 7. Returns and Refunds Processing

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Customer service agents process returns by creating return requests and providing return shipping labels. (Not specified table)
- Warehouse staff receive returned items, inspect them for damage or defects, and update the inventory accordingly. (Not specified table)
- Customer service agents issue refunds or exchange products based on the return request and product condition. (Not specified table)
