 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customer details such as name, contact information, and location. (cust_df)
- Customer service agents update customer records with any changes in personal or account data. (cust_df)
- Sales representatives assign unique usernames and passwords to customers for account access. (cust_df)

## 2. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers add new items to the product catalog with details like product code, name, category, and list price.
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when products are received or shipped out. (cust_df)

## 3. Download Management

Performed By: Customers, IT Support

Activity:
- Customers download software packages from the DellStore2 website based on their needs and product versions.
- IT support team members address any issues related to software downloads or installations.

## 4. Consent Management

Performed By: Dataedo Team, Customers

Activity:
- The Dataedo team provides customers with information about data usage, cookies, and privacy policies.
- Customers accept the terms of use and consent to data collection for personalization, social media features, and traffic analysis.

## 5. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Finance Team

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by selecting appropriate products and quantities. (cust_df)
- Warehouse staff prepare the ordered items for shipment and update inventory levels accordingly. (cust_df)
- The finance team records transaction details such as order ID, total cost, and payment information.

## 6. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Team

Activity:
- The finance team generates invoices for customers based on their orders and sends them via email. (cust_df)

## 7. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Team

Activity:
- Customers make payments for their orders using various methods like credit cards or bank transfers.
- The finance team verifies payment details and updates the customer's account status accordingly. (cust_df)
