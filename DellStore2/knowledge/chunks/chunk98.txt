 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information. (CUSTOMER)
- Customer service agents update customer records with changes to personal or billing information. (CUSTOMER)
- Sales representatives assign customer accounts to specific sales teams for targeted marketing efforts. (CUSTOMER)

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Shipping Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by entering product details and shipping information. (cust_df)
- Warehouse staff retrieve ordered items from inventory and prepare them for shipment.
- Shipping agents package the order and arrange for delivery to the customer's specified address.

## 3. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update stock levels when items are received or shipped out. (cust_df)
- Purchasing teams place orders for new inventory based on sales data and forecasts.
- Warehouse staff receive and store incoming shipments of new inventory.

## 4. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Department, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Finance department generates invoices based on completed orders and sends them to customers. (cust_df)
- Customer service agents address any billing inquiries or disputes from customers.

## 5. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Customers make payments towards their invoices using various payment methods.
- Finance department records and confirms receipt of customer payments. (cust_df)

## 6. Customer Support

Performed By: Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Customer service agents handle inquiries, complaints, and issues raised by customers.
- Customer service agents escalate complex issues to relevant departments for resolution.

## 7. Data Maintenance

Performed By: IT Department, Data Management Team

Activity:
- IT department maintains the integrity and security of the DellStore2 database.
- Data management team ensures accurate and up-to-date data across all tables in the DellStore2 database. (cust_df, custhist_df)
