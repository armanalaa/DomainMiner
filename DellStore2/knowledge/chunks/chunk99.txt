 # DellStore2 — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration and Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, HR Managers, Customers

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their contact details and account information. (CUSTOMER table)
- HR managers onboard new employees by entering their personal and employment data. (CUSTOMER table)
- Customers update their profile information as needed. (CUSTOMER table)

## 2. Customer History Tracking

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Support Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives log customer interactions and sales history. (CUST_HIST table)
- Customer support agents record customer inquiries and resolution details. (CUST_HIST table)

## 3. Account Management

Performed By: Finance Team, IT Department

Activity:
- Finance team manages user accounts and their associated permissions. (CUSTOMER table)
- IT department handles password resets and account recovery. (CUSTOMER table)

## 4. Data Documentation and Maintenance

Performed By: Database Administrators, Technical Writers

Activity:
- Database administrators maintain the integrity and security of the data in DellStore2.
- Technical writers document the database schema and workflows for easier understanding and management. (unspecified documents)

## 5. Data Analysis and Reporting

Performed By: Business Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Business analysts generate reports based on customer data to inform decision making. (unspecified reports)
- Management reviews the reports to assess performance and identify opportunities for improvement. (unspecified reports)
