 # Sakila — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Registration

Performed By: Staff, New Customers

Activity:
- Staff members create new customer accounts by entering the customer's first name, last name, email, and address details.
- New customers provide their active status, store ID, and create date during account creation.
- Staff update customer records when necessary to reflect changes in contact information or account status.

## 2. Film Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers

Activity:
- Product managers register new films by recording the film code, title, category, list price, rental duration, rental rate, length, replacement cost, rating, and special features.
- They update film details as needed to reflect changes in product information or availability.

## 3. Category Assignment

Performed By: Product Managers

Activity:
- Product managers assign films to appropriate categories for easy browsing and organization within the catalog.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by updating film availability in each store.
- Sales representatives update inventory when films are rented or returned.

## 5. Rental Processing

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place rental requests for desired films at the store.
- Sales representatives confirm film availability and process the rental by recording the rental date, inventory ID, customer ID, staff ID, and other relevant details.

## 6. Payment Processing

Performed By: Customers, Staff

Activity:
- Customers make payments for their rentals at the store or online.
- Staff record payment details, including payment ID, customer ID, staff ID, rental ID, amount, and payment date.

## 7. Return Processing

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers return rented films to the store or by mail.
- Sales representatives update inventory and rental records with the return date and staff ID upon receipt of returned films.

## 8. City Management

Performed By: City Managers

Activity:
- City managers add new cities, including city name, country ID, and district, to the database for geographical reference.

## 9. Address Management

Performed By: Staff, Customers (for personal addresses)

Activity:
- Staff members enter address details for stores and city offices.
- Customers provide their home addresses during account creation or updates.
