 # WideWorldImporters — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by providing their details such as name, contact information, and delivery address.
- Customer service agents update customer records with changes in contact details or credit limit.
- Sales representatives assign customers to specific buying groups based on their purchasing habits.

## 2. Buying Group Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Management

Activity:
- Purchasing teams create and manage buying groups for efficient organization of customer purchases.
- Management reviews and edits buying group details, including validity period and name.

## 3. City Management

Performed By: HR Managers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- HR managers add new cities to the database with city names, locations, and population data.
- Data analysts update city records with latest recorded population figures.

## 4. Cold Room Temperature Tracking

Performed By: Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Warehouse staff record temperatures in cold rooms using sensors and log the readings for future reference.

## 5. Color Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Designers

Activity:
- Product managers and designers add new colors to the database with color names and relevant details.

## 6. Customer Category Assignment

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Management

Activity:
- Sales representatives categorize customers based on their purchasing behavior and assign them to appropriate customer categories.
- Management reviews and edits customer category assignments as needed.

## 7. Invoice Processing

Performed By: Accounting Staff, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Accounting staff generate invoices for customers based on their purchases.
- Customer service agents address any invoice-related inquiries from customers.
