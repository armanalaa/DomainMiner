 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales analysts update customer details as needed based on customer inquiries or changes.
- Customer service representatives process customer complaints and address any issues related to accounts.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by associating them with existing customers, setting order statuses, total prices, order dates, order priorities, clerks, ship priorities, and comments.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels to ensure sufficient stock for fulfilling orders.
- Shipping clerks prepare orders for shipping by recording ship dates, commit dates, receipt dates, ship instructions, ship modes, and comments.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Inventory Analysts

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, list prices, and comments.
- Inventory analysts update product details as needed based on inventory levels or price changes.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Procurement Analysts

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders by associating them with suppliers, recording available quantities, supply costs, and comments.
- Procurement analysts update supplier details as needed based on changes in supplier information or contracts.

## 5. Nation and Region Management

Performed By: Geographic Information Analysts

Activity:
- Geographic information analysts manage the nation and region data by recording names, regions, and comments.

## 6. Inventory Tracking

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Analysts

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels for each product by updating available quantities in the database.
- Inventory analysts analyze inventory data to identify trends and make recommendations for reordering or adjustments.

## 7. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Analysts, Accountants

Activity:
- Finance analysts generate financial reports by querying account balances, sales revenues, and other relevant financial data from the database.
- Accountants review and verify the accuracy of financial reports before distribution to stakeholders.
