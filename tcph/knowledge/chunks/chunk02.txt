 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales managers update customer information when changes occur.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Clerks update order details as necessary during the order lifecycle.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and retail price.
- Warehouse staff update product information when inventory levels change.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams record supplier details such as supply key, name, address, nation key, phone number, account balance, and comments.
- Accounting staff update supplier information when financial transactions occur.

## 5. Part Management

Performed By: Inventory Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Inventory managers register new parts by recording part key, name, manufacturer, brand, type, size, container, retail price, and comments.
- Warehouse staff update part information when inventory levels change or parts are discontinued.

## 6. Order Line Item Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Warehouse staff record order line items by recording order key, part key, supplier key, quantity, extended price, discount, tax, return flag, line status, ship date, commit date, receipt date, ship instructions, ship mode, and comments.
- Accounting staff update order line item details when invoices are issued or payments are processed.

## 7. Nation Management

Performed By: HR Managers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- HR managers record nation details such as nation key, name, region key, and comments.
- Data analysts update nation information when geographical changes occur.
