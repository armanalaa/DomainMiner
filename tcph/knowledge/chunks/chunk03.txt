 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales managers update customer information when changes occur.

## 2. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by specifying order details such as order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.

## 3. Order Fulfillment Preparation

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track available items in the warehouse by referring to the quantity column of the lineitem table.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders for items not available in the warehouse.

## 4. Order Fulfillment Execution

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Department

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack ordered items based on line number and ship mode.
- Shipping department prepares shipments for delivery by recording the ship date, ship instruct, and shipmode.

## 5. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Accounting Department

Activity:
- Accounting department generates invoices for orders by calculating extended price, discount, tax, return flag, line status, and total price based on the lineitem table.

## 6. Part Management

Performed By: Inventory Managers, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Inventory managers update part information such as name, manufacturer, brand, type, size, container, retail price, and comment.
- Purchasing teams track part availability by referring to the availqty column of the partsupp table.

## 7. Supplier Management

Performed By: Procurement Managers

Activity:
- Procurement managers register new suppliers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
