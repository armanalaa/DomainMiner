 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales managers update customer information as needed to reflect changes in customer preferences or contact details.
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries related to account balances, orders, or product information.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by associating them with a customer, setting the order status, total price, order date, priority, and comments.
- Clerks process incoming orders by assigning order keys, linenumbers, and ship priorities.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, list price, and comments.
- Warehouse staff update product information when inventory levels change or products are discontinued.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Finance Department

Activity:
- Purchasing teams establish relationships with suppliers by recording supplier details such as name, address, nationkey, phone, account balances, and comments.
- Finance department records supply costs for each product from each supplier.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by updating the available quantity for each product from each supplier.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to replenish low inventory levels.

## 6. Shipping and Delivery

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Department

Activity:
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipping by determining the ship date, commit date, receipt date, ship instructions, ship mode, and comments.
- Shipping department ships the orders according to the specified ship priority.

## 7. Regional Analysis

Performed By: Analytics Team

Activity:
- Analytics team analyzes regional data by examining nation names, regions, and associated customer and order information.
