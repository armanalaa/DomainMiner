 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationality, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales managers update customer accounts with new transactional data and balance adjustments.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by associating them with specific customers, setting order statuses, total prices, order dates, priority levels, and comments.
- Clerks process incoming orders by assigning order keys, line numbers, and ship priorities.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, list prices, and comments.
- Warehouse staff update product information with availability quantities and retail prices.

## 4. Supply Chain Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Supplier Representatives

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to suppliers by specifying part keys, supplier keys, available quantities, supply costs, and comments.
- Supplier representatives update partsupp information with new availability quantities and supply costs.

## 5. Shipping and Delivery

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shippers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track shipments by recording order keys, ship dates, commit dates, receipt dates, ship instructions, ship modes, and comments.
- Shippers update lineitem records with actual shipping details.

## 6. Regional Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Business Intelligence Managers

Activity:
- Data analysts extract regional data from the nation and region tables to perform market segmentation analysis.
- Business intelligence managers use this data for strategic decision making and business planning.

## 7. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Teams, Accountants

Activity:
- Finance teams record financial transactions in the database by associating them with specific customers, orders, products, suppliers, and regions.
- Accountants generate financial reports based on this data for internal analysis and external reporting purposes.
