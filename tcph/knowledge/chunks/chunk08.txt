 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Customer service agents update customer information as needed, including changes to addresses, phone numbers, and account balances.
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries and complaints, documenting interactions in the comment column.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording order keys, customer keys, order statuses, total prices, order dates, order priorities, clerks, ship priorities, and comments.
- Warehouse staff track order fulfillment by updating the order key, ship date, ship priority, and comment columns.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders based on customer orders, recording part keys, supplier keys, and comments.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, list prices, and comments.
- Product managers update product information as needed, including changes to product codes, names, categories, list prices, and comments.

## 4. Supply Chain Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams negotiate supplier agreements, recording part keys, supplier keys, available quantities, supply costs, and comments.
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels by adjusting the available quantity column for each part key.

## 5. Shipping Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipping by recording order keys, ship dates, ship modes, and comments.
- Shipping clerks assign carriers and shipping instructions, updating the ship mode and ship instruct columns for each order key.

## 6. Financial Management

Performed By: Finance Teams

Activity:
- Finance teams record financial transactions by updating account balances, total prices, and comments for orders, customers, and suppliers.

## 7. Market Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts

Activity:
- Data analysts perform market analysis by querying customer, orders, lineitem, part, nation, region, and supplier tables to identify trends, patterns, and insights.
