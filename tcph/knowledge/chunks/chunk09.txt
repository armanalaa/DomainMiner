 TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information when changes are reported.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by specifying the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and prepare items for shipping based on the order details.
- Customer service representatives handle any issues related to order processing and update the order status accordingly.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, brand, type, size, container, retail price, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update product information when changes are reported.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information when changes are reported.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff record the available quantity for each product based on inventory levels.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to replenish low stock items by specifying the part key, supplier key, and required quantity.

## 6. Shipment Processing

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Warehouse staff prepare shipments based on the order details and record the ship date, commit date, receipt date, ship instruct, ship mode, and comments.
- Shipping clerks track the status of each shipment and update it accordingly.

## 7. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Teams

Activity:
- Finance teams record extended prices, discounts, taxes, return flags, line statuses, and comments for each line item in the orders table.
- Finance teams generate financial reports based on the data stored in the database.
