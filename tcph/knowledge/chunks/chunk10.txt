 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and market segments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information when changes occur.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by assigning order keys, customer identifiers, order statuses, total prices, order dates, order priorities, clerks, ship priorities, and comments.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels to ensure sufficient stock for fulfilling orders.
- Shipping clerks record ship dates, commit dates, receipt dates, shipping instructions, ship modes, and comments for each order line item.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, brand names, types, sizes, container details, retail prices, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update product information when changes occur.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams establish relationships with suppliers by recording supplier names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information when changes occur.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff record the availability of each product by updating the quantity on hand for each part key.
- Inventory managers monitor inventory levels and place orders for replenishment when necessary.

## 6. Purchase Order Processing

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Finance Department

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders by recording part keys, supplier keys, available quantities, supply costs, and comments.
- Finance department records the financial details of each purchase order, including account debits and credits.

## 7. Nation Management

Performed By: Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Data entry clerks register new nations by recording nation keys, names, regions, and comments.
