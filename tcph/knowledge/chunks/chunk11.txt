 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer details when changes are reported.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels to ensure sufficient stock for each order.
- Clerks update order details when items are shipped or returned.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, list price, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update product details when changes are reported.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier details when changes are reported.

## 5. Part Supply Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders for specific parts by recording the part key, supplier key, available quantity, supply cost, and comments.
- Warehouse staff receive and store received parts accordingly.

## 6. Sales Analysis

Performed By: Financial Analysts, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Financial analysts calculate sales revenue by summing the extended prices of all line items for each order.
- Data analysts generate reports on sales performance based on region, product category, or time period.

## 7. Inventory Control

Performed By: Warehouse Managers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Warehouse managers monitor inventory levels to ensure sufficient stock for each part.
- Data analysts generate reports on inventory turnover and stockouts.
