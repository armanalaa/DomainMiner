 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives update customer details like name, address, and phone number when contacted by customers.
- Sales managers assign nation keys to new customers based on their geographical location.
- Customer service representatives record account balances for each customer during transactions.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by associating them with customer keys and setting order statuses.
- Clerks input order details such as order date, priority, and ship priority for each order.
- Clerks record total prices for each order based on the items included.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, and list prices.
- Warehouse staff update part keys when new products are added to inventory.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Finance Department

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders by associating them with supplier keys and setting supply costs.
- Finance department records account balances for each supplier during transactions.

## 5. Inventory Tracking

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update available quantities for each product in the inventory.
- Inventory managers track part keys associated with low stock levels to initiate replenishment orders.

## 6. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Department

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack items for each order based on the line item details.
- Shipping department records ship dates and ship modes for each order.

## 7. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Accounting Department

Activity:
- Accounting department generates invoices by calculating extended prices, taxes, and return flags for each order line item.

## 8. Performance Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts

Activity:
- Data analysts analyze sales performance using region keys to identify trends and opportunities.
