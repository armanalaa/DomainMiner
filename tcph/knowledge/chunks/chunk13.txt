 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales managers update customer accounts with new purchase orders, adjusting the account balance accordingly.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by specifying the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Clerks update order details with shipping information such as ship date, ship mode, and ship instructions.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, list price, and comments.
- Warehouse staff update product information with quantity on hand when products are received or shipped.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Finance Department

Activity:
- Purchasing teams establish relationships with suppliers by recording supplier key, name, address, nation key, phone number, account balance, and comments.
- Finance department records supplier supply costs for each product.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by updating the available quantity for each part.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to replenish low inventory levels.

## 6. Sales Analysis and Reporting

Performed By: Sales Managers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Sales managers generate sales reports based on order data, including total sales by customer, sales by product, and sales by region.
- Finance department uses the same data to calculate revenue and profit margins for each product and region.

## 7. Regional Analysis

Performed By: Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Analysts analyze regional performance based on customer, order, and nation data, identifying trends and opportunities.
- Management uses this analysis to make strategic decisions about resource allocation and market expansion.
