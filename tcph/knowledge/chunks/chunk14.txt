 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer details as necessary, ensuring accuracy and completeness of the information.
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints, updating customer records accordingly.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by recording order keys, customer keys, order statuses, total prices, order dates, order priorities, clerks, ship priorities, and comments.
- Clerks verify order details before confirming them with customers.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels to ensure ordered items are available for shipping.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, and list prices.
- Data entry clerks update product details as necessary, ensuring accuracy and completeness of the information.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording supplier keys, names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier details as necessary, ensuring accuracy and completeness of the information.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff record incoming shipments by recording order keys, part keys, available quantities, supply costs, and comments.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders based on inventory levels and supplier availability.

## 6. Financial Management

Performed By: Finance Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Finance teams record financial transactions by recording order keys, customer keys, total prices, and account balances.
- Data entry clerks update financial records as necessary, ensuring accuracy and completeness of the information.

## 7. Reporting and Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Data analysts generate reports based on sales, inventory, customer, and financial data to support decision making.
- Management reviews reports to monitor business performance and make strategic decisions.
