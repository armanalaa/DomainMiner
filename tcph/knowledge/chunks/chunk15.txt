 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information when changes occur.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Clerks update order details as necessary throughout the order lifecycle.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.
- Data entry clerks update product information when changes occur.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information when changes occur.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by recording the part key, quantity, extended price, discount, tax, return flag, line status, ship date, commit date, receipt date, ship instruct, ship mode, and comments for each item.
- Inventory managers update inventory details as necessary throughout the inventory lifecycle.

## 6. Part Supply Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams record the part key, supplier key, available quantity, supply cost, and comments for each part.
- Data entry clerks update part supply details when changes occur.

## 7. Nation and Region Management

Performed By: HR Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- HR managers register new nations by recording their names, regions, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update nation information when changes occur.
- HR managers also register new regions by recording their names and comments.

## 8. Order Analysis and Reporting

Performed By: Analysts, Financial Managers

Activity:
- Analysts generate reports on orders by querying the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, and other relevant details.
- Financial managers use these reports to analyze sales performance and make strategic decisions.
