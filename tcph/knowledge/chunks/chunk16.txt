 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information as necessary, including changes to account balance, address, or contact details.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Customer service representatives update order status when necessary.
- Warehouse staff track order progress, updating the ship date, receipt date, and ship mode as orders are processed.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, list price, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update product information as necessary, including changes to product details or pricing.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording the supplier key, name, address, nation key, phone number, account balance, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information as necessary, including changes to contact details or account balance.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff record the availability of products by updating the quantity on hand for each product.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to replenish inventory levels when necessary.

## 6. Financial Record Keeping

Performed By: Finance Staff, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Finance staff record financial transactions, including sales, purchases, and account balances.
- Data entry clerks update financial records as necessary, including changes to account balances or transaction details.

## 7. Report Generation

Performed By: Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Analysts generate reports based on specific queries, using the database to retrieve and analyze data.
- Management uses these reports for decision making and strategic planning.
