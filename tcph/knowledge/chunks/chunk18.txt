 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales managers update customer information as needed based on interactions with clients.

## 2. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by specifying the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comment.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.

## 4. Inventory Tracking

Performed By: Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by updating the available quantity for each part.

## 5. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Finance Department

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to suppliers by specifying the supplier key, order key, and supply cost.
- Finance department records payments to suppliers by recording their account balances.

## 6. Region Management

Performed By: HR Managers

Activity:
- HR managers assign regions to employees based on their work locations and update the region key accordingly.

## 7. Query Generation and Execution

Performed By: Data Analysts, IT Staff

Activity:
- Data analysts generate queries to extract specific data from the database using commercially available SQL implementations.
- IT staff execute these queries and retrieve the results for further analysis.
