 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information when changes are reported.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Order Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Order clerks update order details as items are shipped or returned.

## 3. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track available products by recording their part keys, quantities, and locations.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders for new items based on inventory levels and demand forecasts.

## 4. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, list price, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update product information when changes are reported.

## 5. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording their supply keys, names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information when changes are reported.

## 6. Part Supply Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track available parts by recording their part keys, suppliers' supply keys, quantities, and supply costs.
- Purchasing teams update part supply information when changes occur.

## 7. Nation and Region Management

Performed By: Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Data entry clerks register new nations and regions by recording their names and comments.
