 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Part Supply Sourcing

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Supplier Analysts

Activity:
- Purchasing teams identify required parts based on production needs. (part)
- Purchasing teams determine the desired part type and size for each order. (part)
- Purchasing teams search for suppliers who can provide the specified parts in the given region. (supplier, nation, region)
- Supplier analysts evaluate supplier account balances to prioritize potential suppliers. (supplier)
- Supplier analysts compare supply costs from multiple suppliers offering the same part. (partsupp)

## 2. Minimum Cost Supplier Selection

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Supplier Analysts

Activity:
- Purchasing teams select the supplier with the minimum cost for each part in a given region. (supplier, partsupp)
- Purchasing teams prioritize suppliers with higher account balances when multiple suppliers have the same minimum cost. (supplier)
- Supplier analysts record the selected supplier's details and the part's information. (supplier, part)

## 3. Order Placement

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams place orders for the required parts with the selected suppliers. (orders, lineitem)
- Warehouse staff receive and record incoming shipments of ordered parts. (lineitem)

## 4. Customer Order Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders and update order details accordingly. (orders, lineitem)
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries related to orders and provide updates as necessary. (orders, lineitem)

## 5. Inventory Tracking

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when new parts are received or existing parts are shipped out. (lineitem)
- Inventory managers monitor stock levels and ensure adequate supplies for production needs. (part)

## 6. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- Finance department records financial transactions, including supplier payments and customer invoices. (orders, lineitem)
- Finance department generates reports on revenue, expenses, and profitability for analysis and decision making. (finance)

## 7. Performance Analysis

Performed By: Management Team

Activity:
- Management team reviews performance reports to identify trends, opportunities, and areas for improvement. (finance)
- Management team makes decisions based on the insights gained from the performance analysis. (none)
