 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Revenue Forecasting

Performed By: Financial Analysts, Data Scientists

Activity:
- Financial analysts generate random years within the range of [1993-1997] for revenue forecasting. (DATE)
- Data scientists select a discount percentage between 0.02 and 0.09 for the analysis. (DISCOUNT)
- Financial analysts determine a quantity threshold between 24 and 25 for lineitems to be considered in the forecast. (QUANTITY)
- Data scientists calculate the potential revenue increase by summing extended prices multiplied by discounts for qualifying lineitems.

## 2. Lineitem Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Warehouse staff record details like product code, name, category, list price when new items are registered in the catalog. (product_code, product_name, category, list_price)
- Sales representatives enter order key, customer key, quantity, extended price, discount, tax, return flag, line status, ship date, commit date, receipt date, ship instructions, ship mode, and comment for each lineitem in an order. (l_orderkey, l_custkey, l_quantity, l_extendedprice, l_discount, l_tax, l_returnflag, l_linestatus, l_shipdate, l_commitdate, l_receiptdate, l_shipinstruct, l_shipmode, l_comment)

## 3. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording customer key, name, address, nation key, phone, account balance, market segment, comment for each customer. (c_custkey, c_name, c_address, c_nationkey, c_phone, c_acctbal, c_mktsegment, c_comment)

## 4. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by associating order key with customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comment. (o_orderkey, o_custkey, o_orderstatus, o_totalprice, o_orderdate, o_orderpriority, o_clerk, o_shippriority, o_comment)
- Warehouse staff update the status of each lineitem in an order based on its lifecycle stage. (l_linestatus)

## 5. Part Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new parts by recording part key, name, manufacturer, brand, type, size, container, retail price, and comment for each part. (p_partkey, p_name, p_mfgr, p_brand, p_type, p_size, p_container, p_retailprice, p_comment)
- Inventory managers track the availability of parts by updating the available quantity for each part in the inventory system. (ps_availqty)

## 6. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording supplier key, name, address, nation key, phone, account balance, and comment for each supplier. (s_suppkey, s_name, s_address, s_nationkey, s_phone, s_acctbal, s_comment)
- Accounting staff record supply costs for each part supplied by a specific supplier. (ps_supplycost)

## 7. Shipping Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Logistics Managers

Activity:
- Data analysts select two nations to analyze shipping volumes between them during 1995 and 1996. (NATION1, NATION2)
- Logistics managers calculate the gross discounted revenues derived from lineitems shipped between the selected nations during the specified period.
