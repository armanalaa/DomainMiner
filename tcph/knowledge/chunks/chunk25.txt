 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when receiving new shipments, referencing the quantity column in the lineitem table.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives create orders for customers by specifying the order details, including order key, customer key, and order date.
- Customer service agents process returns by updating the return flag in the lineitem table for affected orders.
- Warehouse staff prepare shipments based on the ship date and ship mode from the orders table.

## 3. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Finance Department

Activity:
- Purchasing teams negotiate supply agreements with suppliers by agreeing on the supplier key, supply cost, and available quantity in the partsupp table.
- Finance department records payments to suppliers, referencing the account balance column in the supplier table.

## 4. Market Share Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Marketing Managers

Activity:
- Data analysts generate market share reports by calculating the fraction of revenue from a specific region and nation for a given part type over two years.
- Marketing managers use these reports to identify trends and adjust marketing strategies accordingly.

## 5. Product Profit Measurement

Performed By: Finance Department, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Finance department calculates profit margins by subtracting supply cost from extended price (excluding discount) for a specified product line and supplier nation.
- Data analysts use these profit measures to inform purchasing decisions and pricing strategies.
