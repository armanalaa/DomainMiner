 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their name, address, nation, phone number, account balance, and market segment. (customer)
- Customer service representatives update customer information when changes are reported. (customer)
- Data analysts generate reports on customer behavior, including those who frequently return items. (customer, lineitem)

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comment. (orders)
- Warehouse staff track shipped items by updating the ship date, ship instruct, and ship mode for each line item. (lineitem)
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders based on customer demands and available stock. (partsupp)

## 3. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update the availability of parts in stock when items are shipped or received. (partsupp)
- Purchasing teams monitor the supply cost and quantity of each part. (partsupp)

## 4. Part Information Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Product managers register new parts by recording their part key, name, manufacturer, brand, type, size, container, retail price, and comment. (part)
- Data analysts generate reports on part performance, including the most important subset of suppliers' stock in a given nation. (partsupp, supplier, nation)

## 5. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording their supply key, name, address, nation key, phone number, account balance, and comment. (supplier)
- Data analysts generate reports on supplier performance, including the total value of all available parts they supply. (partsupp, supplier)

## 6. Nation Management

Performed By: Data Analysts

Activity:
- Data analysts register new nations by recording their nation key, name, and comment. (nation)

## 7. Report Generation

Performed By: Data Analysts

Activity:
- Data analysts generate reports on customer behavior, including those who have returned items and lost revenue for a given quarter. (customer, orders, lineitem, nation)
- Data analysts generate reports on the most important subset of suppliers' stock in a given nation. (partsupp, supplier, nation)
