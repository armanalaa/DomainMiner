 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Promotion Management

Performed By: Marketing Managers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Marketing managers devise promotional strategies for specific product types (PROMO%) based on market trends and customer preferences.
- Data analysts generate a random date within the specified year range to determine the promotion period for analysis.
- Data analysts execute the Promotion Effect Query to calculate the percentage of revenue generated by promotional parts during the selected month.

## 2. Top Supplier Identification

Performed By: Purchasing Managers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Purchasing managers identify potential suppliers for recognition or increased business opportunities.
- Data analysts generate a random date within the specified year and month range to determine the quarter for analysis.
- Data analysts execute the Top Supplier Query to find the supplier with the highest revenue contribution during the selected quarter.

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives receive orders from customers and record order details (orderkey, custkey, orderdate, orderpriority) in the Orders table.
- Warehouse staff retrieve items with matching partkeys from inventory to fulfill customer orders.
- Customer service agents update order statuses (l_linestatus) based on order processing progress.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Managers, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse managers track the availability of items in stock by monitoring availqty in the partsupp table.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to replenish low inventory levels when necessary.

## 5. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Managers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Finance managers record financial transactions (l_extendedprice, l_discount) in the lineitem table.
- Data analysts calculate total revenue for a given period by executing appropriate queries on the lineitem and part tables.

## 6. Customer Service

Performed By: Customer Service Agents, Account Managers

Activity:
- Customer service agents handle customer inquiries related to orders (c_custkey, c_phone, c_acctbal) and provide updates or resolutions.
- Account managers maintain customer relationships by addressing long-term account issues and providing personalized service.

## 7. Geographical Analysis

Performed By: Market Researchers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Market researchers gather data on customer nations (c_nationkey) to identify regional trends and opportunities for expansion.
- Data analysts group customers by nation and region (r_regionkey) to analyze market performance across different geographical areas.
