 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Part Selection for Customer Requirements

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Product Managers

Activity:
- Purchasing teams identify parts with specific attributes based on customer requirements. (part)
- Product managers verify that the selected parts do not belong to a supplier with registered complaints. (supplier)
- Product managers filter out parts of a given brand and type. (part)
- Product managers select eight different sizes for the identified parts. (part)

## 2. Supplier Identification for Customer Requirements

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams find suppliers who can provide the required parts with the selected attributes. (partsupp)
- Warehouse staff check the availability of the required parts at each supplier. (partsupp)

## 3. Order Placement for Customer Requirements

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customers

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders for the selected parts based on customer requirements. (orders)
- Customers specify the quantity of each part in their order. (lineitem)

## 4. Order Processing and Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Department

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick the ordered parts from inventory based on the specified quantities. (lineitem, part)
- Warehouse staff prepare the order for shipping by packaging the selected parts. (part)
- Shipping department assigns a shipment mode and tracks the ship date for each order. (orders, lineitem)

## 5. Small Quantity Order Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Data analysts calculate the average yearly revenue lost if orders for small quantities of certain parts are no longer filled. (lineitem, part)
- Purchasing teams evaluate whether concentrating sales on larger shipments would be beneficial based on the calculated loss. (lineitem, part)

## 6. Complaint Registration and Tracking

Performed By: Better Business Bureau, Supplier Management Team

Activity:
- The Better Business Bureau registers complaints against suppliers. (supplier)
- Supplier management team tracks suppliers with registered complaints to ensure they are not selected for customer orders. (supplier)
