 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Order Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Sales representatives process customer orders by entering order details such as product code, quantity, and shipping instructions. (orders, lineitem)
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and prepare items for shipment based on the received orders. (partsupp, lineitem)
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to suppliers for restocking low inventory items. (orders, partsupp, supplier)

## 2. Customer Registration

Performed By: HR Managers

Activity:
- HR managers register new customers in the system by recording customer details like name, address, and nationality. (customer)

## 3. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack ordered items according to the provided shipping instructions. (lineitem, partsupp)
- Shipping clerks label and dispatch the packed orders to the specified addresses. (orders, customer)

## 4. Inventory Control

Performed By: Warehouse Managers, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse managers monitor inventory levels and alert purchasing teams when stock is low for specific items. (partsupp)
- Purchasing teams analyze demand trends and forecast future inventory needs. (orders, lineitem)

## 5. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Finance Department

Activity:
- Purchasing teams negotiate prices and terms with suppliers for the purchase of goods. (partsupp, supplier)
- Finance department records payments made to suppliers for the purchased items. (supplier)

## 6. Sales Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Data analysts generate reports on sales performance based on historical order data. (orders, lineitem)
- Sales managers use these reports to identify trends, set targets, and make strategic decisions. (orders, customer)

## 7. Customer Segmentation

Performed By: Marketing Managers

Activity:
- Marketing managers categorize customers based on their purchasing behavior and preferences for targeted marketing campaigns. (customer, orders, lineitem)
