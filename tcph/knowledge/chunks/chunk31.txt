 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Potential Part Promotion Identification

Performed By: Data Analysts, Marketing Managers

Activity:
- Data analysts query the database to find parts with specific names (using wildcard search).
- Data analysts filter the results to include only parts that have more than 50% of their total quantity shipped in a given year.
- Marketing managers review the list of potential parts for promotional offers.

## 2. Supplier Selection for Potential Part Promotion

Performed By: Data Analysts, Marketing Managers

Activity:
- Data analysts identify suppliers who have the selected potential parts in excess.
- Marketing managers evaluate the suppliers based on their location and other factors to decide which ones are suitable for promotional offers.

## 3. Query Execution for Potential Part Promotion Identification

Performed By: Database Administrators

Activity:
- Database administrators execute the Potential Part Promotion Query against the TPC-H database.

## 4. Supplier Information Extraction

Performed By: Data Analysts

Activity:
- Data analysts extract supplier names and addresses from the query results.

## 5. Potential Part Promotion Report Generation

Performed By: Marketing Managers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Marketing managers and data analysts compile the list of selected suppliers into a report.

## 6. Supplier Notification for Potential Part Promotion

Performed By: Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives contact the identified suppliers to discuss potential promotional offers.

---

## 7. Order Management

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Shipping Managers

Activity:
- Customers place orders through various channels (online, phone, etc.).
- Sales representatives process the orders and confirm availability of items.
- Warehouse staff pick, pack, and prepare the ordered items for shipping.
- Shipping managers arrange delivery of the orders to customers.

## 8. Order Status Tracking

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Shipping Managers

Activity:
- Customers track their order status through various channels (online, phone, etc.).
- Sales representatives update order status in the system as necessary.
- Warehouse staff update order status when items are picked and ready for shipping.
- Shipping managers update order status when orders are shipped.

## 9. Order Delay Detection

Performed By: Data Analysts, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Data analysts query the database to identify orders that have been delayed beyond their committed delivery date.
- Customer service representatives contact affected customers to inform them about the delay and provide updates on expected delivery times.

## 10. Supplier Performance Evaluation

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Purchasing teams evaluate supplier performance based on factors such as order fulfillment rate, delivery time, and quality of goods.
- Data analysts query the database to gather data for the evaluation process.
