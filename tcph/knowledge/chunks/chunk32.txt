 TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Segmentation and Opportunity Identification

Performed By: Data Analysts, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Data analysts extract customer account balance data from the CUSTOMER table.
- Data analysts calculate the average account balance for active customers.
- Sales managers identify countries with a higher than average account balance and no orders in the last 7 years.
- Sales managers compile a list of potential sales opportunities based on the identified countries.

## 2. Customer Account Balance Update

Performed By: Finance Department, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Finance department reviews customer account balances and updates them in the CUSTOMER table as needed.
- Data entry clerks record new or updated account balance information provided by the finance department.

## 3. Order Generation for Sales Opportunities

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives contact potential sales opportunities to discuss product offerings.
- Customer service representatives create and submit new order requests on behalf of customers.

## 4. Order Processing and Invoice Generation

Performed By: Order Processing Team, Accounting Department

Activity:
- Order processing team reviews and approves incoming orders from sales representatives.
- Accounting department generates invoices based on approved orders and updates the ORDERS table accordingly.

## 5. Inventory Management and Product Availability Check

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff check product availability in the warehouse before fulfilling orders.
- Inventory managers update the PART and PARTSUPP tables with current stock levels.

## 6. Order Fulfillment and Shipping

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Department

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack products for shipping based on approved orders.
- Shipping department prepares and ships orders to the customer's specified address.

## 7. Sales Performance Analysis and Reporting

Performed By: Data Analysts, Management Team

Activity:
- Data analysts collect and analyze sales data from the ORDERS and LINEITEM tables.
- Management team reviews sales performance reports and makes decisions based on the analysis.
