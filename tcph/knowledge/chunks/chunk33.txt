 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationality, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information as necessary, such as changes in address or account balance.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Customer service representatives update order information as necessary, such as changes in order status or ship priority.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.
- Data entry clerks update product information as necessary, such as changes in product details or list price.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by recording the part key, quantity, and comments for each item.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders based on inventory levels and supplier availability.

## 5. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording the supplier key, name, address, nation key, phone number, account balance, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information as necessary, such as changes in address or account balance.

## 6. Sales Reporting

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- Finance department records sales data by extracting order key, customer key, order date, order status, total price, and comments from the ORDERS table.

## 7. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Accounts Receivable Clerks

Activity:
- Accounts receivable clerks generate invoices by extracting order key, customer key, order date, total price, and comments from the ORDERS table, and line item details from the LINEITEM table.

## 8. Old Sales Removal

Performed By: Database Administrators

Activity:
- Database administrators remove old sales information by deleting rows from the ORDERS and LINEITEM tables based on specific primary keys.
