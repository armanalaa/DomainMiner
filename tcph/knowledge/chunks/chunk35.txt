 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Order Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new orders by entering customer details, order priority, and order date. (orders)
- Sales representatives add items to the order by selecting products, quantities, and shipping instructions. (lineitem)
- Customer service agents update order status based on customer inquiries or issues. (orders)

## 2. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track available inventory by updating part availability for each supplier. (partsupp)
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to replenish low stock levels based on demand forecasts and supplier agreements. (not directly represented in the schema)

## 3. Financial Management

Performed By: Finance Department, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Finance records order totals for each transaction. (orders)
- Accounting staff calculates extended prices, taxes, and discounts for each line item. (lineitem)
- Accounting staff updates the account balance of customers and suppliers based on transactions. (not directly represented in the schema)

## 4. Data Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Business Intelligence Specialists

Activity:
- Data analysts generate reports on sales performance by querying order totals and line item details. (orders, lineitem)
- Business intelligence specialists analyze inventory levels and trends using part availability data. (partsupp)

## 5. Historical Tracking

Performed By: System Administrators

Activity:
- System administrators record transaction history for auditing purposes. (HISTORY)

## 6. Customer Relationship Management

Performed By: Marketing Teams, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Marketing teams segment customers based on their preferences and purchasing behavior. (customer)
- Customer service agents update customer comments and address changes as needed. (customer)

## 7. Geographical Analysis

Performed By: Market Researchers, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Market researchers analyze regional trends using nation and region data. (nation, region)
