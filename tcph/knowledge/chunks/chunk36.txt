 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales managers update customer information as needed.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Clerks update order details such as order status, total price, and comments throughout the order lifecycle.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the part key, name, manufacturer, brand, type, size, container, retail price, and comments.
- Warehouse staff update product information as needed.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Finance Department

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording the supplier key, name, address, nation key, phone number, account balance, and comments.
- Finance department updates supplier information as needed.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by recording the part key, available quantity, and comments.
- Purchasing teams update inventory levels when new shipments arrive or existing items are sold.

## 6. Order Line Item Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Warehouse staff record new order line items by recording the order key, part key, supplier key, line number, quantity, extended price, discount, tax, return flag, line status, ship date, commit date, receipt date, ship instructions, ship mode, and comments.
- Sales representatives update order line item details as needed.

## 7. Nation Management

Performed By: HR Managers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- HR managers register new nations by recording the nation key, name, region key, and comments.
- Data analysts update nation information as needed for reporting and analysis purposes.

## 8. Region Management

Performed By: HR Managers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- HR managers register new regions by recording the region key, name, and comments.
- Data analysts update region information as needed for reporting and analysis purposes.
