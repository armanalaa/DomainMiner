 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationality keys, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer details when changes are reported.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Clerks update order details as they progress through various stages.
- Warehouse staff track shipped items by updating the shipdate, receiptdate, and shipmode for each line item in an order.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, list price, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update product details when changes are reported.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording their names, addresses, nationality keys, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier details when changes are reported.

## 5. Inventory Control

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track available quantities for each part by updating the availqty for each record in partsupp.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders based on current inventory levels and supplier availability.

## 6. Financial Record Keeping

Performed By: Finance Staff, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Finance staff record financial transactions by updating the account balances for customers and suppliers.
- Data entry clerks update financial records when changes are reported.

## 7. Report Generation

Performed By: Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Analysts generate reports based on data from various tables to monitor sales, inventory levels, customer behavior, and supplier performance.
- Management uses these reports for strategic decision making.
