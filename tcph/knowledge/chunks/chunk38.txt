 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Order Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new orders by entering the customer key, order date, and order priority.
- Customer service agents update order status based on customer inquiries.
- Sales representatives modify order details such as ship priority or comments when necessary.

## 2. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by updating the available quantity for each part key.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to suppliers based on low inventory notifications.

## 3. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Finance Department

Activity:
- Purchasing teams establish relationships with new suppliers by recording supplier details like name, address, and account balance.
- Finance department processes payments to suppliers based on invoices received.

## 4. Customer Management

Performed By: Marketing Team, HR Managers

Activity:
- Marketing team segments customers based on their purchasing behavior and demographics.
- HR managers update customer information such as name, address, or phone number when changes occur.

## 5. Nation and Region Management

Performed By: Data Administrators

Activity:
- Data administrators maintain a list of nations with their respective regions.

## 6. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Marketing Team

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product code, name, category, and retail price.
- Marketing team updates product details or promotions as needed.

## 7. Order Line Item Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update order line items with the extended price after applying discounts and taxes.
- Sales representatives record shipment details like ship date, ship mode, or ship instructions for each order line item.

## 8. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- Finance department generates financial reports based on total prices from orders and account balances of customers and suppliers.
