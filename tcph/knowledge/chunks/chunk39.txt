 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales managers update customer accounts with new purchase orders, total prices, order dates, order priorities, clerks, ship priorities, and comments.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by selecting customer keys, registering order statuses, and specifying order priorities.
- Warehouse staff track the ship dates of orders based on their line numbers.

## 3. Product Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, and retail prices.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders for specific parts by specifying part keys, available quantities, and supply costs.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Procurement Managers, Finance Department

Activity:
- Procurement managers register new suppliers by recording supplier names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Finance department records payments to suppliers based on their supply keys.

## 5. Nation and Region Management

Performed By: Administrative Assistants

Activity:
- Administrative assistants update the nation and region databases with new names and comments as needed.

## 6. Inventory Tracking

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track available quantities of parts based on their part keys.
- Purchasing teams update the availability of parts by specifying new quantities for specific suppliers.

## 7. Transaction Processing

Performed By: Database Administrators

Activity:
- Database administrators manage transactions affecting different tables concurrently to ensure data integrity and consistency.

## 8. Data Analysis

Performed By: Business Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Business analysts execute queries against the database to analyze sales trends, inventory levels, and supplier performance.
- Management uses the results of these analyses to make informed decisions about business strategy and operations.
