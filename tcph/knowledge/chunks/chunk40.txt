 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales managers update customer accounts with transaction history, payment records, and communication logs.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by associating them with specific customers, setting order statuses, total prices, order dates, priority levels, and comments.
- Clerks process incoming orders by assigning order keys, linenumbers, and ship priorities.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, list prices, and comments.
- Warehouse staff update product information with availability statuses and quantities on hand.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Finance Departments

Activity:
- Purchasing teams establish relationships with suppliers by recording supplier names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Finance departments manage supplier accounts by tracking payments, invoices, and financial transactions.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by updating part quantities on hand and available quantities for each product.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to replenish low or depleted inventory items.

## 6. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Departments

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack order items based on order keys and linenumbers.
- Shipping departments prepare shipments by recording ship dates, commit dates, receipt dates, ship instructions, ship modes, and comments.

## 7. Region and Nation Management

Performed By: HR Managers, Geographic Analysts

Activity:
- HR managers assign employees to specific regions based on their locations and job roles.
- Geographic analysts update region and nation information with names, comments, and any relevant demographic data.
