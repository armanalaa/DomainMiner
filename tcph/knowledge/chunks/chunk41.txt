 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information when changes are reported.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Order Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording order keys, customer keys, order statuses, total prices, order dates, order priorities, clerks, ship priorities, and comments.
- Order clerks update order details as items are shipped or returned.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, and list prices.
- Data entry clerks update product information when changes are reported.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by recording part keys, quantities, and availability statuses for each item.
- Inventory managers adjust stock levels based on sales, returns, and deliveries.

## 5. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams record supplier details such as names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information when changes are reported.

## 6. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack items for orders based on order keys and line numbers.
- Shipping clerks record ship dates, ship priorities, ship modes, and shipping instructions for each order.

## 7. Financial Management

Performed By: Finance Staff, Accountants

Activity:
- Finance staff record extended prices, discounts, taxes, return flags, line statuses, and supply costs for each item sold.
- Accountants generate financial reports based on the data in the database.
