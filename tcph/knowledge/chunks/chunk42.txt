 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information when changes are reported.
- Customer service representatives look up customer details using the customer key for various inquiries.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Clerks update order details when necessary, such as changing order status or shipping priority.
- Clerks generate invoices based on order information and send them to customers.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, list price, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update product information when changes are reported.
- Product managers look up product details using the part key for various inquiries.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Procurement Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Procurement managers register new suppliers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information when necessary.
- Procurement managers look up supplier details using the supplier key for various inquiries.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by recording the part key, available quantity, and comments.
- Inventory managers update inventory information when stock changes occur.
- Inventory managers generate reports on inventory levels for analysis and decision making.

## 6. Purchase Order Processing

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders by recording the part key, supplier key, available quantity, supply cost, and comments.
- Clerks update purchase order details when necessary, such as changing the available quantity or supply cost.
- Clerks generate purchase invoices based on purchase order information and send them to suppliers.

## 7. Shipping and Receiving

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipping by recording the order key, ship date, commit date, receipt date, ship instructions, ship mode, and comments.
- Shipping clerks update order details when they are shipped or received.
- Shipping clerks generate shipping reports for analysis and decision making.
