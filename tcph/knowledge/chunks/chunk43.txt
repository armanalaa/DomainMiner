 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer details when changes occur.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Clerks update order details as they progress through various stages (e.g., pending, shipped, delivered).

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.
- Data entry clerks update product details when changes occur.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording their names, addresses, nation keys, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier details when changes occur.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by recording the part key, available quantity, supply cost, and comments for each item.
- Inventory managers adjust inventory quantities based on orders, shipments, and returns.

## 6. Order Line Items Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new order line items by recording the order key, part key, supplier key, quantity, extended price, discount, tax, return flag, line status, ship date, commit date, receipt date, ship instructions, ship mode, and comments.
- Clerks update order line item details as they progress through various stages (e.g., pending, shipped, delivered).

## 7. Region Management

Performed By: Geographical Analysts, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Geographical analysts register new regions by recording their region keys and comments.
- Data entry clerks update region details when changes occur.

## 8. Nation Management

Performed By: International Relations Officers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- International relations officers register new nations by recording their nation keys, names, region keys, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update nation details when changes occur.
