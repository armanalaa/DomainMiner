 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information when changes are reported.
- Customer service representatives assign customer accounts to appropriate sales teams based on market segment.

## 2. Order Placement

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customers

Activity:
- Sales representatives process orders from customers by recording order keys, customer keys, order statuses, total prices, order dates, order priorities, clerks, ship priorities, and comments.
- Customers place orders by specifying their chosen items, quantities, shipping instructions, and modes.

## 3. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Warehouse staff locate and prepare ordered items for shipment based on order keys and line numbers.
- Shipping clerks package the items according to their containers and ship dates.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to suppliers by recording part keys, supplier keys, available quantities, supply costs, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information when changes are reported.

## 5. Part Management

Performed By: Inventory Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Inventory managers track parts inventory by recording part keys, names, manufacturers, brands, types, sizes, containers, retail prices, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update part information when changes are reported.

## 6. Financial Record Keeping

Performed By: Accountants, Finance Officers

Activity:
- Accountants record financial transactions related to orders, customers, and suppliers by recording order keys, customer keys, supplier keys, extended prices, discounts, taxes, return flags, line statuses, ship dates, commit dates, receipt dates, ship instructs, ship modes, comments, and account balances.

## 7. Performance Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Business Intelligence Specialists

Activity:
- Data analysts and business intelligence specialists analyze sales, inventory, and financial data to identify trends, patterns, and areas for improvement.
