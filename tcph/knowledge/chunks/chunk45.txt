 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer details when changes are reported.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Order Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by specifying the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Order clerks update order details as they progress through various stages (e.g., pending, shipped, delivered).

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.
- Data entry clerks update product details when changes are reported.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier details when changes are reported.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by recording the part key, quantity, and availability for each product.
- Inventory managers adjust stock quantities based on orders, returns, and supplier deliveries.

## 6. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack items for shipment by referencing the order key, part key, quantity, and shipping instructions.
- Shipping clerks update the order status to "shipped" once the package is ready for delivery.

## 7. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Analysts, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Finance analysts generate financial reports by summarizing sales, expenses, and profits based on order data.
- Data entry clerks update the finance database with new transactions and adjustments.
