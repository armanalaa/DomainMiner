 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Product Management

Performed By: Product Managers

Activity:
- Register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.
- Assign a manufacturer and brand to each product.
- Set the retail price based on the product key.
- Define the product type and size.
- Specify the container for the product.
- Provide a comment describing the product's features or uses.

## 2. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Identify potential suppliers for each product based on their availability and cost.
- Negotiate supply costs with selected suppliers.
- Record the quantity of each product available from each supplier.
- Update the PartSupp table with the new supplier information.

## 3. Customer Acquisition

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Marketing Teams

Activity:
- Identify potential customers based on market segmentation.
- Contact prospective customers to introduce products and services.
- Register new customers in the customer database with their contact information.

## 4. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Browse the catalog to select desired items.
- Place an order by specifying the order key, customer details, and line items.
- Choose a shipping priority for each order.
- Record any comments related to the order.

## 5. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Teams

Activity:
- Retrieve ordered items from inventory based on the line item details.
- Package and label each order for shipping.
- Assign a ship mode to each order.
- Record the ship date, commit date, and receipt date for each order.

## 6. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Finance Teams

Activity:
- Calculate the total price of each order based on the line items and discounts.
- Generate an invoice for each order with the customer details, order key, and total price.
- Record any comments related to the invoice.

## 7. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Track the quantity of each product in inventory.
- Update the inventory when new products are received or existing products are sold.
- Replenish inventory by ordering more supplies from suppliers based on availability and demand.

## 8. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Teams, Management

Activity:
- Record account balances for each customer.
- Generate financial reports to analyze sales, expenses, and profitability.
- Use the reports to make informed decisions about pricing, inventory, and marketing strategies.
