 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales managers update customer accounts with transaction details such as purchases, returns, and payments.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by selecting products, setting quantities, and specifying order priorities.
- Clerks assign order keys, record total prices, and input order dates for each order.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, retail prices, and comments.
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when products are received or shipped.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams negotiate supply costs with suppliers and record them in the system.
- Accounting staff manage supplier accounts by recording account balances and payments.

## 5. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick items from inventory based on order details and prepare them for shipping.
- Shipping teams package orders, determine shipping modes, and schedule shipments.

## 6. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Accounting Staff, Finance Department

Activity:
- Accounting staff generate invoices based on order details and send them to customers.
- Finance department records payments received against outstanding invoices.

## 7. Performance Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Data analysts extract data from the system for analysis, such as sales trends, inventory levels, and customer behavior.
- Management uses the analysis to make informed decisions about pricing, marketing strategies, and operational efficiency.
