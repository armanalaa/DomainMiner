 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information when changes are reported.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives receive orders from customers, noting order details such as order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Clerks process orders by confirming availability of items, calculating extended prices, applying discounts, determining taxes, and recording return flags and line statuses.
- Warehouse staff track shipped orders by recording ship dates, commit dates, receipt dates, ship instructions, ship modes, and comments.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, list prices, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update product information when changes are reported.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to suppliers by recording supplier keys, names, addresses, nation keys, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information when changes are reported.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by recording available quantities for each part.
- Purchasing teams order supplies based on current inventory levels and projected demand.

## 6. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- Finance department records financial transactions, including sales, purchases, and account balances.

## 7. Performance Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts

Activity:
- Data analysts analyze sales, inventory, and financial data to identify trends, make predictions, and inform business decisions.
