 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information when changes are reported.
- Customer service representatives retrieve customer details for inquiries or complaints.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Order Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Order clerks update order details as items are shipped or returned.
- Sales representatives track order progress and provide updates to customers.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, list price, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update product information when changes are reported.
- Product managers retrieve product details for sales or inventory purposes.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Procurement Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Procurement managers register new suppliers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information when changes are reported.
- Procurement managers retrieve supplier details for purchasing negotiations.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by recording the part key, quantity, and comments.
- Inventory managers update inventory levels when items are received or shipped.
- Inventory managers generate reports on inventory status for decision making purposes.

## 6. Purchasing and Supply Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders by recording the part key, supplier key, available quantity, supply cost, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update purchase order details when changes are reported.
- Purchasing teams track purchase order progress and negotiate prices with suppliers.

## 7. Financial Management

Performed By: Finance Staff, Accountants

Activity:
- Finance staff record financial transactions by recording the order key, total price, and comments.
- Accountants generate reports on sales, expenses, and profits for analysis and decision making purposes.
