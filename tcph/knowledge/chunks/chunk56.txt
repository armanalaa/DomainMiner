 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Database Refresh Management

Performed By: Database Administrators, Test Sponsors

Activity:
- Database administrators generate, permute, and relocate DBGEN data for RF1 and RF2 refresh functions.
- Test sponsors schedule pairs of refresh functions (RF1 followed by RF2) according to the throughput test requirements.
- Test sponsors trickle the scheduling of refresh functions to maintain a more even refresh load throughout the throughput test.

## 2. Query Execution Management

Performed By: Database Administrators, Test Sponsors

Activity:
- Database administrators execute a single refresh stream that simulates a sequence of batched data modifications against the database.
- Test sponsors schedule individual refresh functions within an instance of RF1 or RF2 to meet the requirements of Clause 2.5.2 and Clause 2.5.3.
- Test sponsors ensure that a given pair completes before the next pair can be initiated, and that within a pair RF1 must complete before RF2 can be initiated.

## 3. Query Performance Metrics Calculation

Performed By: Data Analysts

Activity:
- Data analysts compute the TPC-H Composite Query-per-Hour Metric (QphH@Size) based on the performance metric defined in Clause 5.4.3.
- Data analysts calculate the TPC-H Price/Performance ($/kQphH/@Size) using the definition provided in Clause 5.4.4.

## 4. Power Consumption Measurement

Performed By: IT Infrastructure Team

Activity:
- IT infrastructure team measures the power consumption of the system to compute the TPC-H query processing power at the chosen database size, as defined in Clause 5.4.1.

## 5. Data Generation and Formatting

Performed By: Database Administrators

Activity:
- Database administrators generate, permute, and format DBGEN data for RF1 and RF2 refresh functions according to the TPC-H specifications.

## 6. Sales Data Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Sales representatives and purchasing teams execute RF1 (New Sales refresh function) and RF2 (Old Sales refresh function) as part of the refresh stream to bring the database up to date with its operational data source.

## 7. Customer Data Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Marketing Teams

Activity:
- Customer service representatives update customer information such as address, phone number, and account balance in the customer table.
- Marketing teams segment customers based on their purchasing behavior and demographics using the c_mktsegment column in the customer table.
