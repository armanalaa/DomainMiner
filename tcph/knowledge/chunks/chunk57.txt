 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data analysts update customer information as needed based on changes reported by customers or identified through data analysis.
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints, updating the comment column accordingly.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comment.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and fulfill orders by updating the quantity column in the lineitem table when items are shipped.

## 3. Product Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, list price, and comment.
- Data analysts analyze sales data to identify popular products and adjust pricing strategies accordingly.

## 4. Supply Chain Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Warehouse Staff, Supplier Representatives

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders by recording the part key, supplier key, available quantity, supply cost, and comment.
- Warehouse staff track incoming shipments by updating the receipt date column in the lineitem table when items are received.
- Supplier representatives update supplier information as needed based on changes reported by suppliers or identified through data analysis.

## 5. Financial Management

Performed By: Finance Staff, Accountants

Activity:
- Finance staff record financial transactions by updating the account balances of customers and suppliers.
- Accountants generate financial reports by analyzing data from various tables and summarizing it in a clear and concise format.

## 6. Data Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Business Intelligence Specialists

Activity:
- Data analysts analyze sales, inventory, and financial data to identify trends and opportunities for improvement.
- Business intelligence specialists create dashboards and reports based on the analyzed data to help decision makers make informed choices.

## 7. Quality Assurance

Performed By: Quality Assurance Staff

Activity:
- Quality assurance staff verify the accuracy of data in the database by comparing it with external sources or through manual checks.
- Quality assurance staff correct any errors found and ensure that the data conforms to the specified standards.
