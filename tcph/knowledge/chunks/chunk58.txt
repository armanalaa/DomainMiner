 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information when changes are reported.

## 2. Order Placement

Performed By: Customers, Sales Representatives

Activity:
- Customers place orders by selecting products, specifying quantities, and providing shipping instructions.
- Sales representatives confirm orders, record order keys, and update the order status.

## 3. Order Fulfillment Coordination

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Warehouse staff retrieve ordered items from inventory based on order keys.
- Shipping clerks pack orders, record ship dates, and assign shipping modes.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Inventory Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Inventory managers monitor product availability and adjust stock levels as needed.
- Warehouse staff update the available quantity for each partkey when items are shipped or received.

## 5. Supplier Relationship Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Procurement Managers

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to suppliers based on product requirements and supplier availability.
- Procurement managers record supplier keys, supply costs, and availqty for each partkey.

## 6. Financial Record Keeping

Performed By: Accountants, Finance Managers

Activity:
- Accountants record financial transactions related to orders, such as total prices and account balances.
- Finance managers generate reports on revenue, expenses, and profitability based on the recorded data.

## 7. Market Segment Analysis

Performed By: Marketing Analysts, Business Intelligence Specialists

Activity:
- Marketing analysts categorize customers into different market segments based on their purchasing behavior and demographics.
- Business intelligence specialists analyze market trends and customer preferences using the segment data.
