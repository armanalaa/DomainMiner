 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales managers update customer accounts with new purchase orders, total prices, order dates, order priorities, clerk details, ship priorities, and comments.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by associating them with customer accounts, setting order statuses, and specifying ship priorities.
- Warehouse staff track order details such as quantities, extended prices, discounts, taxes, return flags, line statuses, ship dates, commit dates, receipt dates, ship instructions, ship modes, and comments.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, list prices, and comments.
- Purchasing teams update product information with availability quantities and supply costs.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Procurement Managers, Accounting Teams

Activity:
- Procurement managers register new suppliers by recording supplier names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Accounting teams record supplier supply costs for each product.

## 5. Nation and Region Management

Performed By: Geographical Information Specialists

Activity:
- Geographical information specialists manage nation and region data by recording names and comments.

## 6. Partsupp and Lineitem Data Maintenance

Performed By: Inventory Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Inventory managers update partsupp data with available quantities for each product.
- Warehouse staff record lineitem details such as quantities, extended prices, discounts, taxes, return flags, line statuses, ship dates, commit dates, receipt dates, ship instructions, ship modes, and comments.

## 7. Query Execution and Performance Testing

Performed By: Database Administrators, IT Specialists

Activity:
- Database administrators execute queries on the TPC-H database to measure its performance.
- IT specialists ensure that the communication interface used is an industry standard (e.g., Ethernet, Token Ring, or RS232).
