 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, and account balances.
- Customer service agents update customer details when contacted for inquiries or complaints.
- Sales representatives categorize customers based on market segments to tailor sales strategies.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by specifying order keys, customer keys, order statuses, total prices, and order dates.
- Clerks issue shipping priorities for orders based on customer preferences and order importance.
- Clerks record order comments to document any special instructions or issues related to an order.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, and list prices.

## 4. Inventory Tracking

Performed By: Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track available quantities of products by updating the supply cost for each item.

## 5. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Purchasing teams maintain supplier information by recording supplier names, addresses, nationalities, account balances, and comments.

## 6. Part Supply Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders for parts based on availability and demand.
- Warehouse staff update the available quantity of a part when it is received.

## 7. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Departments

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack orders based on line numbers and ship dates.
- Shipping departments determine the shipping mode for each order based on shipping instructions and priorities.

## 8. Region Management

Performed By: HR Managers

Activity:
- HR managers assign regions to customers based on their geographical locations for strategic planning purposes.

## 9. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Teams

Activity:
- Finance teams record financial transactions related to orders, such as extended prices, discounts, taxes, and return flags.

## 10. Performance Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts

Activity:
- Data analysts analyze the performance of the system by executing queries on various tables to generate reports and insights.
