 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information when changes are reported.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Order Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Order clerks update order details as items are shipped or returned.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.
- Data entry clerks update product information when changes are reported.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording the supplier key, name, address, nation key, phone number, account balance, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information when changes are reported.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by recording the part key, available quantity, supply cost, and comments.
- Inventory managers update inventory details when items are received or sold.

## 6. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack orders based on the order key, part key, quantity, ship date, ship instruct, and ship mode.
- Shipping clerks prepare and dispatch orders for delivery.

## 7. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Teams

Activity:
- Finance teams record financial transactions by recording the order key, total price, and account balance changes.
- Finance teams generate reports on sales, expenses, and profits based on the recorded data.

## 8. Market Analysis

Performed By: Marketing Analysts

Activity:
- Marketing analysts analyze market trends by examining customer segments, order priorities, and sales volumes.
- Marketing analysts use this information to develop marketing strategies and forecast future sales.
