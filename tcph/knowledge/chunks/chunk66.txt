 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information when changes are reported.
- Customer service representatives handle customer inquiries and complaints, updating the comment field accordingly.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Order Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comment.
- Order clerks update order details as goods are shipped or if there are any changes in the order status.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.
- Data entry clerks update product information when changes are reported.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by recording the quantity of each item in stock.
- Inventory managers order new supplies based on low inventory alerts and current demand.

## 5. Supplier Management

Performed By: Procurement Officers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Procurement officers register new suppliers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information when changes are reported.

## 6. Purchasing and Receiving

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to suppliers for required items.
- Warehouse staff receive goods from suppliers, updating the receipt date and confirming the quantity received.

## 7. Sales and Revenue Tracking

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- Finance records sales revenue by updating the account balances of customers and suppliers based on orders and purchases.
