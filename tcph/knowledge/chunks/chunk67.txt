 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by entering their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments into the customer table.
- Data entry clerks update customer information as needed, such as changes in address or account balance.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Order Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording order keys, customer keys, order statuses, total prices, order dates, order priorities, clerks, ship priorities, and comments in the orders table.
- Order clerks process incoming orders by entering order details from the lineitem table into the orders table.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, and list prices in the part table.
- Data entry clerks update product information as needed, such as changes in brand, type, size, or retail price.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by entering supplier keys, names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments in the supplier table.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information as needed, such as changes in address or account balance.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track available quantities of products by updating the availqty column in the partsupp table based on incoming shipments and outgoing orders.
- Inventory managers monitor inventory levels and reorder supplies when necessary.

## 6. Shipping and Delivery

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Warehouse staff prepare orders for shipping by recording ship dates, commit dates, receipt dates, ship instructions, ship modes, and comments in the lineitem table.
- Shipping clerks package and dispatch orders to customers based on the information recorded in the lineitem table.

## 7. Nation and Region Management

Performed By: Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Data entry clerks register new nations and regions by entering nation keys, names, and comments in the nation and region tables respectively.
