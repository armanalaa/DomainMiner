 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer details when changes are reported.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by specifying the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Clerks record order details from sales representatives into the orders table.

## 3. Order Line Item Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives add line items to existing orders by specifying the order key, part key, quantity, extended price, discount, tax, return flag, line status, ship date, commit date, receipt date, ship instructions, ship mode, and comments.
- Warehouse staff update line item quantities when stock levels change.

## 4. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.
- Data entry clerks update product details when changes are reported.

## 5. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier details when changes are reported.

## 6. Part Supply Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams record the part key, supplier key, available quantity, supply cost, and comments for each part in the partsupp table.
- Warehouse staff update partsupp data when stock levels change or new suppliers are added.

## 7. Nation and Region Management

Performed By: Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Data entry clerks record nation names, regions, and comments in the nation and region tables.

## 8. Reporting and Analysis

Performed By: Analysts, Managers

Activity:
- Analysts generate reports based on data from the customer, orders, lineitem, part, partsupp, nation, region, and supplier tables to analyze sales trends, inventory levels, and supplier performance.
