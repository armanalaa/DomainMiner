 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and market segments.
- Customer service agents update customer details when contacted about changes or inquiries.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by specifying the customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comment.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and prepare items for shipping based on the order details.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to suppliers for necessary parts or products.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.
- Warehouse staff update product information when stock levels change or products are discontinued.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Finance Department

Activity:
- Purchasing teams establish relationships with suppliers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Finance department records supplier invoices and payment details.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels when items are received or shipped by recording the part key, available quantity, and supply cost.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to replenish low stock items based on current inventory levels.

## 6. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Department

Activity:
- Finance department records financial transactions related to customer accounts, orders, and suppliers, and generates reports for analysis and decision making.

## 7. Data Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Data analysts extract and analyze data from the database to identify trends, patterns, and insights that help inform business decisions.
- Management uses these insights to make strategic decisions about pricing, marketing, inventory management, and supplier relationships.
