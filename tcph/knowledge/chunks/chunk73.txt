 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data analysts update customer information as needed based on changes reported by customers or identified through data analysis.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Finance Department

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Warehouse staff track order fulfillment by updating the lineitem table with details such as quantity, extended price, discount, tax, return flag, line status, ship date, commit date, receipt date, ship instructions, ship mode, and comment for each item in an order.
- Finance department records payments and updates account balances accordingly.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.
- Data analysts update product information as needed based on changes reported by suppliers or identified through data analysis.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Finance Department

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to suppliers by recording the part key, supplier key, available quantity, supply cost, and comment.
- Finance department records payments and updates account balances accordingly.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Warehouse staff update inventory levels by recording the part key, available quantity, and comment.
- Data analysts analyze inventory data to identify trends and make recommendations for reordering or adjusting stock levels.

## 6. Regional Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts

Activity:
- Data analysts analyze regional data by recording the region key, name, and comment.

## 7. Performance Test Execution

Performed By: System Administrators, Database Administrators

Activity:
- System administrators execute performance tests by running queries on the database and recording the results.
