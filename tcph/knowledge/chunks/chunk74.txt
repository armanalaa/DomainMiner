 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information when changes occur, such as address or phone number updates.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Representatives, Order Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Customer service representatives process customer inquiries about existing orders, updating the order status as necessary.
- Order clerks prepare shipping instructions for orders, specifying ship mode and other details.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, list price, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update product information when changes occur, such as price adjustments or category reassignments.

## 4. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Inventory Managers

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by recording the quantity of each item available in stock.
- Inventory managers order new supplies based on predicted demand and current inventory levels.

## 5. Purchasing and Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Procurement Managers

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to suppliers for necessary items, recording the part key, supplier key, available quantity, supply cost, and comments.
- Procurement managers negotiate contracts with suppliers, updating supplier information as needed.

## 6. Financial Record Keeping

Performed By: Finance Staff, Accountants

Activity:
- Finance staff record financial transactions related to orders, such as payments received and expenses incurred.
- Accountants generate financial reports based on these records, analyzing trends and making recommendations for future decisions.

## 7. Geographical Analysis

Performed By: Market Research Analysts, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Market research analysts analyze customer data by nation key, name, region key, and comments to identify market segments and trends.
- Data analysts use this information to make recommendations for marketing strategies and product offerings in different regions.
