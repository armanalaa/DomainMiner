 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationality keys, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update customer information as needed, such as changes in address or account balance.
- Customer service representatives look up customer details using the customer key for various purposes, like resolving queries or updating accounts.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording order keys, customer keys, order statuses, total prices, order dates, order priorities, clerks, ship priorities, and comments.
- Clerks update order details as necessary, such as changing the order status or shipping priority.
- Clerks generate reports on order processing using various order details like order key, customer key, and order date.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording product codes, names, categories, list prices, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update product information as needed, such as changes in price or category.
- Product managers look up product details using the part key for various purposes, like resolving queries or updating products.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording supplier keys, names, addresses, nationality keys, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Data entry clerks update supplier information as needed, such as changes in address or account balance.
- Purchasing teams look up supplier details using the supplier key for various purposes, like negotiating contracts or placing orders.

## 5. Inventory Management

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Data Entry Clerks

Activity:
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels by recording part keys, available quantities, and supply costs.
- Data entry clerks update inventory details as necessary, such as changes in availability or cost.
- Warehouse staff generate reports on inventory levels using various inventory details like part key and available quantity.

## 6. Order Fulfillment

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Warehouse staff pick and pack items for orders by recording order keys, part keys, quantities, and ship dates.
- Shipping clerks prepare shipping instructions using the ship date, ship mode, and ship instruct details.
- Shipping clerks update order fulfillment statuses as necessary, such as changes in ship mode or ship date.

## 7. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Teams, Data Analysts

Activity:
- Finance teams generate financial reports by recording total prices, account balances, and comments.
- Data analysts analyze financial data using various financial details like total price and account balance.
- Finance teams update financial records as necessary, such as changes in account balance or total price.
