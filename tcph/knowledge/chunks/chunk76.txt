 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Database Generation and Verification

Performed By: DBGen Operators, Quality Assurance Analysts

Activity:
- DBGen operators generate the database according to specified scale factors using provided command lines (Appendix F).
- Quality assurance analysts verify that the generated data matches the reference data set provided in Appendix F.

## 2. Database Loading and Integrity Check

Performed By: Database Administrators, Quality Assurance Analysts

Activity:
- Database administrators load the generated database into the qualification and test databases.
- Quality assurance analysts verify referential integrity in the database after the initial load.

## 3. Performance Test Execution

Performed By: DBMS Administrators, TPC Benchmark Testers

Activity:
- DBMS administrators ensure that storage and processing elements not included in the priced configuration are removed or made inaccessible during the performance test.
- TPC Benchmark testers execute queries against the test database according to the specified execution rules for power and throughput tests.

## 4. Metrics Computation and Reporting

Performed By: DBMS Administrators, TPC Benchmark Testers, Reporting Analysts

Activity:
- DBMS administrators measure the timing intervals and compute the required metrics according to specified rules.
- TPC Benchmark testers verify that the reported metrics are repeatable.
- Reporting analysts compile major portions of the full disclosure report, ensuring accuracy and compliance with reporting requirements.

## 5. Configuration Compliance Verification

Performed By: System Architects, Compliance Analysts

Activity:
- System architects verify that the SUT composition is compliant and its components will be commercially available software or hardware products according to clause 7 of the Pricing Specification.
- Compliance analysts verify implementation compliance with Clause 6.2.4, if an implementation specific layer is used.

## 6. Driver Compliance Verification

Performed By: DBMS Vendors, Compliance Analysts

Activity:
- DBMS vendors verify that their driver meets the requirements of Clause 5.2 and Clause 6.3.
- Compliance analysts verify that any profile-directed optimization performed by the test sponsor conforms to the requirements of Clause 5.2.9.

## 7. Full Disclosure Report Accuracy Check

Performed By: Reporting Analysts, Quality Assurance Analysts

Activity:
- Reporting analysts verify that major portions of the full disclosure report are accurate and comply with the reporting requirements, including the executive summary, numerical quantity summary, diagrams, and block diagram illustrating the database load process.
