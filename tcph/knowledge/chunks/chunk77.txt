 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales Representatives enter customer details and preferences into the customer table.
- Customer Service Agents update customer contact information as needed.
- Sales Representatives record sales transactions in the orders table for each customer.
- Customer Service Agents respond to customer inquiries about orders, shipping status, or products.

## 2. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Inventory Specialists

Activity:
- Product Managers add new products and their specifications to the product table.
- Inventory Specialists update product availability and pricing in the inventory table.
- Product Managers categorize products according to department and category in the departments and categories tables.

## 3. Order Processing

Performed By: Warehouse Workers, Shipping Clerks

Activity:
- Warehouse Workers pick and pack items from inventory for each order.
- Shipping Clerks prepare shipping labels and package orders for delivery.
- Shipping Clerks update the orders table with tracking numbers and estimated delivery dates.

## 4. Invoice Generation

Performed By: Accounting Department

Activity:
- The accounting department generates invoices based on order details from the orders table.
- Invoices are sent to customers via email or mail.

## 5. Payment Processing

Performed By: Finance Department, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Customers make payments through various methods (credit card, bank transfer, etc.).
- The finance department records payments in the payments table.
- Customer Service Agents confirm receipt of payments and update customer accounts accordingly.

## 6. Inventory Management

Performed By: Inventory Specialists, Purchasing Managers

Activity:
- Inventory Specialists monitor stock levels and reorder items when necessary.
- Purchasing Managers negotiate prices with suppliers for new inventory.
- Inventory Specialists update the inventory table with new shipments and adjustments.

## 7. Performance Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Data Analysts extract data from various tables (orders, payments, inventory) to generate reports.
- Management reviews reports to identify trends, make decisions, and set goals for future growth.
