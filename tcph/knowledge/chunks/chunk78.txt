 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Analysts

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales analysts update customer accounts with transactional data from orders, such as order dates, order priorities, and total prices.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives place new orders by specifying the product codes, quantities, extended prices, discounts, shipment instructions, and shipping modes.
- Warehouse staff track order fulfillment by updating the receipt dates of line items.

## 3. Product Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording their product codes, names, categories, and retail prices.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders for specific products from suppliers.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Procurement Managers, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Procurement managers register new suppliers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Accounting staff record supply costs for each product from each supplier.

## 5. Nation and Region Management

Performed By: Geographic Information Specialists

Activity:
- Geographic information specialists manage the geographical data of nations and regions by recording their names and comments.

## 6. Data Analysis

Performed By: Business Analysts, Financial Managers

Activity:
- Business analysts analyze sales performance by market segment using aggregated order data.
- Financial managers calculate supplier revenue based on extended prices and discounts for specific time periods.
