 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales managers update customer information as needed, including changes to account balances and marketing segments.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by recording the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Clerks update order details such as order status, total price, and comments throughout the order lifecycle.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, list price, and comments.
- Warehouse staff update product information as needed, including changes to list prices and comments.

## 4. Supply Chain Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders by recording the part key, supplier key, available quantity, supply cost, and comments.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels and update partsupp table with the available quantity as items are received or shipped.

## 5. Nation and Region Management

Performed By: Geographical Information Specialists

Activity:
- Geographical information specialists record nation names, regions, and comments in the respective tables.

## 6. Financial Reporting

Performed By: Finance Teams

Activity:
- Finance teams record financial transactions and update account balances for customers and suppliers.

## 7. Performance Analysis and Optimization

Performed By: Data Analysts, Business Intelligence Specialists

Activity:
- Data analysts and business intelligence specialists run queries on the TPC-H database to analyze sales trends, inventory levels, customer behavior, and other key performance indicators.
- Based on their findings, they make recommendations for process improvements and cost reductions.
