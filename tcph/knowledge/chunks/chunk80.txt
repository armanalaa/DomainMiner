 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Customer Service Agents

Activity:
- Sales representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Customer service agents update customer information when contacted about changes or inquiries.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff, Finance Department

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by specifying the order status, total price, order date, priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Warehouse staff track inventory levels to ensure items are available for shipping.
- Finance department records the receipt of payments from customers for completed orders.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and list price.
- Purchasing teams issue purchase orders to suppliers for necessary parts based on inventory levels and demand forecasts.

## 4. Supplier Relationship Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Finance Department

Activity:
- Purchasing teams negotiate prices and terms with suppliers for the procurement of parts.
- Finance department processes payments to suppliers for delivered goods and services.

## 5. Inventory Control

Performed By: Warehouse Staff, Purchasing Teams

Activity:
- Warehouse staff receive and store incoming shipments from suppliers.
- Purchasing teams update inventory levels when items are received or shipped out.

## 6. Performance Analysis

Performed By: Data Analysts, Management

Activity:
- Data analysts collect and analyze data from various tables to generate reports on sales performance, inventory levels, customer behavior, and supplier relationships.
- Management uses these reports to make informed decisions about pricing strategies, marketing efforts, and operational improvements.
