 # TPC-H — Business Workflows

## 1. Customer Management

Performed By: Customer Service Representatives, Sales Managers

Activity:
- Customer service representatives register new customers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, market segments, and comments.
- Sales managers update customer information when changes occur.

## 2. Order Processing

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Order Clerks

Activity:
- Sales representatives create new orders by specifying the order key, customer key, order status, total price, order date, order priority, clerk, ship priority, and comments.
- Order clerks update order details as items are shipped or returned.

## 3. Product Catalog Management

Performed By: Product Managers, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Product managers register new items in the catalog by recording the product code, name, category, and retail price.
- Warehouse staff update product information when inventory levels change.

## 4. Supplier Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Accounting Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams register new suppliers by recording their names, addresses, nationalities, phone numbers, account balances, and comments.
- Accounting staff update supplier information when financial transactions occur.

## 5. Part Supply Management

Performed By: Purchasing Teams, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Purchasing teams record the availability quantity and supply cost for each part from suppliers.
- Warehouse staff update part supply details as inventory levels change or supplier information is updated.

## 6. Nation and Region Management

Performed By: HR Managers, Geographical Analysts

Activity:
- HR managers register new nations by recording their names, regions, and comments.
- Geographical analysts update nation and region information as necessary.

## 7. Order Line Item Management

Performed By: Sales Representatives, Warehouse Staff

Activity:
- Sales representatives create order line items by specifying the order key, part key, supplier key, quantity, extended price, discount, tax, return flag, line status, ship date, commit date, receipt date, ship instructions, ship mode, and comments.
- Warehouse staff update order line item details as inventory levels change or orders are shipped or returned.
